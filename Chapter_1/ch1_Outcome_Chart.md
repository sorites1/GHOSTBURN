| Outcome              | Description                                                             | Effect                                                                           | Example                                                                                                 |
| :------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Success**          | The result equals or exceeds the DL.                                    | You achieve your goal.                                                           | *You hit the target; roll damage.*                                                                      |
| **Critical Success** | You roll a natural 20, or the result is at least 10 higher than the DL. | You achieve your goal and gain an extra benefit.                                 | *You hit the target; roll double damage or pick a body part to apply a condition.*                      |
| **Failure**          | The result is lower than the DL.                                        | Your attempt fails. You do not achieve your goal.                                | *You miss the target and deal no damage.*                                                               |
| **Critical Failure** | You roll a natural 1, or the result at least 10 lower than the DL.      | Your attempt fails. You do not achieve your goal, and you suffer a complication. | *You miss the target and deal no damage. Also, your weapon runs out of ammo and needs a reload (1 AP).* |
