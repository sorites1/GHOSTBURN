## Outcomes

When you use a skill action, roll a d20 and add the appropriate [[ch1_Abilities|ability]] score to get the result. Compare the result to the [[ch1_Difficulty_Level|Difficulty Level]] (DL) to determine the outcome: [[ch1_Success|success]], [[ch1_Critical_Success|critical success]], [[ch1_Failure|failure]], or [[ch1_Critical_Failure|critical failure]].
