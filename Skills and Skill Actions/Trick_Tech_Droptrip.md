## Droptrip \[Specialization\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                              |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[Security\]          | Tech  | \[Cyber Rig\], \[Datajack\], \[Physical\], \[Two Hands\], \[Unaware\] |
