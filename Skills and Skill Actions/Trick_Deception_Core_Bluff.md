## Bluff \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Deception | \[Attitude\], \[Language\], \[Social\], \[Vocal\] |

*With a straight face, you tell a lie, obscure the truth, or omit key details to deceive the person you're talking to.*

| Outcome      | Description                                                    |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They do not believe you and their \[Attitude\] drops one level |
| Fade         | They believe you (but are unsure) and gain \[Suspicious\]      |
| Success      | They believe you                                               |
| Cool Success | They believe you and you are \[Inspired\]                      |
