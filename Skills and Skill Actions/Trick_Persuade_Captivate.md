## Captivate \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                          |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Persuade | \[Attitude\], \[Physical\], \[Social\], \[Vicinity\], \[Visible\] |

*With a simple change in posture, a strut in your step, or a flick of your hair, you can put on "the look" that makes everyone sit up and take notice.* 

If there are multiple onlookers, use the highest Wits amongst the \[Group\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                    |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They are not impressed and suffers a \[Break\]                 |
| Fade         | They are \[Distracted\] and their \[Attitude\] drops one level |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                                        |
| Cool Success | They are \[Bewildered\] and your \[Ally\]                      |
