## Frighten \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | \[Language\], \[Social\], \[Visual\], (\[Vocal\]) |

*You turn to an individual and look them in the eye. Maybe you say a few words, or you might just let your body language do the talking. Regardless, they are left broken from the brief exchange.* 

To use Frighten on someone, they must be \[Close\]. You cannot use this trick on someone who is currently suffering from a \[Break\].

| Outcome      | Description                                            |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | They are not scared of you and they gain \[Confident\] |
| Fade         | They \[Break\] and \[Attack\] you                      |
| Success      | They \[Break\] and flee                                |
| Cool Success | They faint and are rendered \[Unconscious\]            |
