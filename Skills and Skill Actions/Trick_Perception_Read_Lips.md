## Read Lips \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                             |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (10)         | Perception | \[Concentrate\], \[Language\], \[Range\], \[Visual\] |

*You focus on a person's lips as they speak and though you cannot hear them, you understand what they are saying.* 

To use Read Lips, the person speaking must be \[Close\] or \[Nearby\]. The scope of Read Lips is one sentence (or a few short sentences.) You must roll Read Lips again each time you want to decipher what the person said. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                          |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You don't know what they said and are \[Confused\]                   |
| Fade         | You know what they said and gain Stress vs. Moderate (10)            |
| Success      | You know what they said                                              |
| Cool Success | You automatically know what they're saying for the rest of the scene |
