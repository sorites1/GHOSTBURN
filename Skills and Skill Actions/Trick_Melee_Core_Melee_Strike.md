## Melee Strike \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                   |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| \[Opposed\]           | Melee | \[Attack\], \[Melee], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Visual\], (\[One Hand\] or \[Two Hands\]) |

*You attack your opponent by viciously swinging or thrusting your melee weapon at them.* 

To use Melee Strike, your opponent must be \[Close\]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You miss and are \[Off Balance\]                           |
| Fade         | You deal \[Minimum Damage\] to them                        |
| Success      | You deal \[Normal Damage\] to them                         |
| Cool Success | You deal \[Maximum Damage\] to them and they are \[Prone\] |
