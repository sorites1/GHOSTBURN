## Acrobatic Stunt \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                           |
| --------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Hard (14)             | Athletics | \[Move\], \[Physical\], \[Visual\] |

*You are capable of moving in way that seems almost supernatural.* 

Acrobatic Stunt is used to perform a \[Move\] you would otherwise be unable to attempt, When you use Acrobatic Stunt, state the desired outcome of your \[Move\], such as *I want to move across the wire like a tightrope walker or I want to scale the sheer surface of this building.*

| Outcome      | Description                                                    |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You fall \[Prone\] and may take falling damage (GM discretion) |
| Fade         | You get the desired outcome but you are \[Off Balance\]        |
| Success      | You get the desired outcome                                    |
| Cool Success | You get the desired outcome and recover 1 AP                   |
