## Intimidate \[Skill Action\]

| Target                | Base Difficulty Level | Duration      | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | --------------------- | ------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| A person or \[Group\] | FORT                  | A few seconds | \[Language\], \[Social\], \[Vocal\] |

With threats of damage to income, reputation, or bodily health, you force the targe to comply with your instructions. If your instructions put the target in harm's way, the DL is raised by one. The target will refuse to follow suicidal instructions.

| Failure          | Description                                                             |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Critical Failure | As Standard Failure but the target's \[Attitude\] also drops one level. |
| Standard Failure | The target is not intimidated and you suffer a \[Setback\].             |

| Success       | Description                                                                                                     |
| ------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Minor (6)     | The target is \[Compliant\] but you gain \[Stress\] vs. \[FORT\]. Failure means the target suffers a \[Break\]. |
| Moderate (10) | The target is \[Compliant\].                                                                                    |
| Major (14)    | The target is \[Compliant\] and the GM offers to make them your \[Follower\] or \[Reliable\].                   |
| Severe (18)   | The target is \[Compliant\] and you receive the both \[Follower\] and \[Reliable\].                             |
| Extreme (22)  | As Severe (18) but target becomes your \[Ally\].                                                                |
