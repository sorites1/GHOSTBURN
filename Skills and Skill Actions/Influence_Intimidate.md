## Intimidate \[Skill\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Ability  | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------- |
| FORT                  | Presence | \[Language\], \[Social\], \[Vocal\] |

With threats of damage to income, reputation, or bodily health, you force the targe to comply with your instructions. If your instructions put the target in harm's way, the DL is raised by one. The target will refuse to follow suicidal instructions.
