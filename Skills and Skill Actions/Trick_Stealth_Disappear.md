## Disappear \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Reaction\], \[Visual\] |

*By capitalizing on a sudden distraction, you slip away with not even a whisper.* 

When observers in your \[Vicinity\] become \[Distracted\], you may use a \[Reaction\] to Disappear. There must also be an exit \[Nearby\] that you can use to make your escape.

| Outcome      | Description                                                          |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You are discovered and they are \[Suspicious\]                       |
| Fade         | You escape, gain \[Undetected] and have \[Stress\] vs. Moderate (10) |
| Success      | You escape and gain \[Undetected\]                                   |
| Cool Success | You escape and gain \[Hidden\] or \[Far\] (your choice)              |
