## Silent Takedown \[Specialization\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | \[Attack\], \[Physical\], \[Two Hands\], (\[Blade\] or \[Hands\]) |

With a simple change in posture, a strut in your step, or a flick of your hair, you can put on "the look" that makes everyone sit up and take notice. If there are multiple onlookers, use the highest Wits amongst the \[Group\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Not impressed and \[Break\]                                 |
| Fade         | \[Distracted\] and \[Attitude\] drops one level             |
| Success      | \[Distracted\]                                              |
| Cool Success | \[Bewildered\]. \[Attitude\] becomes Friendly, and \[Ally\] |
