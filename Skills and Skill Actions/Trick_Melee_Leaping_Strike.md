## Leaping Strike \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                            |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[Opposed\]           | Melee | \[Attack\], \[Melee], \[Move\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Visual\] |

*You get a running start, fall from above, or swing in on a rope to perform a mid-air strike.* 

To use Leaping Strike, first make a basic Athletics roll with a DL of Hard (14). If you fail, you fall \[Prone\] and your action ends. Otherwise, you automatically \[Move\] through the air to a \[Nearby\] opponent by jumping, swinging, or falling, for example, and roll Leaping Strike. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You miss and fall \[Prone\]                                |
| Fade         | You deal \[Minimum Damage\] to them                        |
| Success      | You deal \[Normal Damage\] to them                         |
| Cool Success | You deal \[Maximum Damage\] to them and they are \[Prone\] |
