## Dodge \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                               |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Insight | \[Physical\], \[Reaction\], \[Visual\] |

*You sense the attack almost before you see it and deftly sidestep and duck out of harms way.* 

When you are hit by a \[Melee\] \[Attack\], you may use a \[Reaction\] to \[Dodge\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                    |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You take the hit and are \[Prone\]                             |
| Fade         | Their \[Attack\] is \[Downgraded\] and you are \[Off Balance\] |
| Success      | Their \[Attack\]  is \[Downgraded]                             |
| Cool Success | Their \[Attack\] is \[Downgraded\] and you \[Counterattack\]   |
