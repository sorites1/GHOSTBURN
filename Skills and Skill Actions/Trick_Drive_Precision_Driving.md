## Precision Driving \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                             |
| --------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Severe (18)           | Drive | \[Physical\], \[Two Hands\], \[Vehicle\], \[Visual\] |

*When you're behind the wheel, you are capable of pulling off daring high-speed stunts that threaten to break the laws of physics.* 

When you use Precision Driving, state your desired outcome, such as *I want to jump the draw bridge while it's opening* or *I want to tilt my car so it's almost on its side and I can go through a narrow alley.* If you use only one hand with Precision Driving, raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                     |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Your \[Vehicle\] \[Crashes\] and takes 5 damage                 |
| Fade         | You get the desired outcome but your \[Vehicle\] takes 2 damage |
| Success      | You get the desired outcome                                     |
| Cool Success | You get the desired outcome and recover 1 Luck Point            |
