## Burst Fire \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                             |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (14)             | Firearms | \[Gun\], \[Attack\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Visual\], (\[Move\]) |

*You grip your weapon and issue a three-round burst.* 

When you use Burst Fire, your target must be at the same range as the optimal range listed for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Burst Fire \[Damage Array\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                              |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again. |
| Fade         | You deal \[Minimum Damage\]                                              |
| Success      | You deal \[Normal Damage\]                                               |
| Cool Success | Choose to deal \[Maximum Damage\] and they are \[Suppressed\]            |
