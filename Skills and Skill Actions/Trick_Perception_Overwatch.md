## Overwatch \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (14)             | Perception | \[Auditory\], \[Concentrate\], \[Language\], \[Verbal\], \[Visual\] |

*You survey the combat zone and relay relevant information to your crew, noting the positions and movements of friends, enemies, and civilians.* 

To benefit from an Overwatch outcome, the crew member must have hands-free wireless comms (headset or cyberware). 

| Outcome      | Description                                                      |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They get no benefit and you are \[Distracted\]                   |
| Fade         | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\] and you are \[Distracted\] |
| Success      | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\]                            |
| Cool Success | Their next two \[Attack\] are \[Upgraded\]                       |
