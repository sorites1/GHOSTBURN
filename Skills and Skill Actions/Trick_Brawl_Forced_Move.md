## Forced Move \[Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[Opposed\]           | Brawl | \[Attack\], \[Move\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Visual\] |

*You grab your opponent and force them to move with your superior ability.* 

To use Forced Move, your opponent must be \[Close\]. If they are \[Restrained\], your Forced Move outcome is \[Upgraded\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                     |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You fail and are \[Off Balance\]                                |
| Fade         | You force them to \[Move\] and you are \[Off Balance\]          |
| Success      | You force them to \[Move\]                                      |
| Cool Success | You force them to \[Move\] and put them in \[Agony\] if desired |
