## Single Shot \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                             |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (10)         | Firearms | \[Gun\], \[Attack\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Visual\], (\[Move\]) |

*You point your gun and shoot.* 

When you use Single Shot, your target must be at the same range as the optimal range listed for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Single Shot \[Damage Array\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                          |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again.             |
| Fade         | You deal \[Minimum Damage\]                                                          |
| Success      | You deal \[Normal Damage\]                                                           |
| Cool Success | Choose to deal \[Maximum Damage\] or deal \[Normal Damage\] and make a free \[Move\] |
