## Influence \[Skill\]

The Influence skill is used to convince someone to help you, whether through honeyed words or threats of violence. It is also covers forms of expression ranging from singing and dancing to storytelling.

This skill is typically rolled with Presence, but the GM might decide to call for Strength, such as when you literally strong arm someone to get them to agree to your demands, or Intellect when you\'re impressing someone with your raw intellect.

### Skill Actions

Influence includes the following skill actions: 

| Skill Action | Description                                                              |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Entertain    | Singing, giving a rousing speech, telling jokes, or relating an anecdote |
| Intimidate   | Making someone do what you want through intimidation                     |
| Persuade     | Convincing someone to help you, potentially by improving their attitude  |
