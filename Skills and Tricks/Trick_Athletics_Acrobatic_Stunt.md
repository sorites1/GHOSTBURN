## Acrobatic Stunt \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                           |
| --------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Athletics | \[Move\], \[Physical\], \[Visual\] |

*You utilize your incredible balance and speed to execute a death defying climb, jump, or dive.* 

Use Acrobatic Stunt to \[Move\] beyond a significant physical obstacle or to gain access to a hard-to-reach area. When you use this trick, state the desired outcome of your \[Move\], such as *I want to move across the wire like a tightrope walker or I want to scale the sheer surface of this building.*

| Outcome      | Description                                         |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You do not \[Move\] and you are \[Off Balance\]     |
| Fade         | You \[Move\] as desired but you are \[Off Balance\] |
| Success      | You \[Move\] as desired                             |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] as desired and you are \[Inspired\]    |
