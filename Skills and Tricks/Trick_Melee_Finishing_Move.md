## Finishing Move \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                              |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[Opposed\]           | Melee | \[Attack\], \[Move\], \[Melee\], \[One Hand\], \[Physical\], \[Reaction\], \[Visual\] |

*You sense your opponent's weakness and use your weapon to finish them.* 

When an opponent who is \[Close\] or \[Nearby\] goes from \[Fine\] to \[Hurting\], you can react. You \[Move\] to the opponent, if they are not already \[Close\], and roll Finishing Move. 

| Outcome      | Description                      |
| ------------ | -------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You miss and are \[Off Balance\] |
| Fade         | You deal \[Minimum Damage\]      |
| Success      | You deal \[Normal Damage\]       |
| Cool Success | They are dead                    |
