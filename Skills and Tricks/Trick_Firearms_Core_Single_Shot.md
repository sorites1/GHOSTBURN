## Single Shot \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                         |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Firearms | \[[[Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Keyword_Gun\|Gun]]\], \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Keyword_One_Hand\|One Hand]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You point your gun and shoot.* 

When you use Single Shot, your target must be at the optimal range listed for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. If you use \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands|Two Hands]]\], lower the DL by one. Use your weapon's Single Shot \[[[Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                          |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again.             |
| Fade         | You deal \[[[Keyword_Minimum_Damage\|Minimum Damage]]\]                                                          |
| Success      | You deal \[[[Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\]                                                           |
| Cool Success | Choose to deal \[[[Keyword_Maximum_Damage\|Maximum Damage]]\] or deal \[Normal Damage\] and make a free \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] |
