## Athletic Move \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                            |
| --------------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Athletics | \[Move\], \[Physical\], \[Visual\], (\[Two Hands\]) |

*You engage in a physical activity like climbing, jumping, or swimming.* 

Athletic Move is used to \[Move\] by climbing, jumping, or swimming, When you use Physical Activity, state the desired outcome of your \[Move\], such as *I want to climb over the fence or I want jump from this rooftop to the next one.* If the desired outcome is unlikely, raise the DL by one, and if it is highly unlikely, raise the DL by two.

| Outcome      | Description                                                    |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You fall \[Prone\] and may take falling damage (GM discretion) |
| Fade         | You get the desired outcome but you are \[Off Balance\]        |
| Success      | You get the desired outcome                                    |
| Cool Success | You get the desired outcome and recover 1 AP                   |
