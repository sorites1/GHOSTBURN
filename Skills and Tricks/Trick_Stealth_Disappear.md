## Disappear \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Reaction\], \[Visual\] |

*By capitalizing on a sudden distraction, you slip away with not even a whisper.* 

When one or more observers \[Close\] to you become \[Distracted\], you may use a \[Reaction\] to Disappear. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You are seen and their \[Attitude\] becomes Hostile                               |
| Fade         | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\], gain \[Undetected] and have \[Stress\] vs. Moderate (14) |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and gain \[Undetected\]                                   |
| Cool Success | You escape and \[Move\] \[Far\] away                                              |
