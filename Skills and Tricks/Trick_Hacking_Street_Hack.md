## Street Hack \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                        |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[Security\]          | Tech  | \[Cyber Rig\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Two Hands\], \[Visual\], \[Wireless\] |

*Your type in the command and your cyber rig spins up, creating an AR overlay of the world around you. By selecting one of the color-coded devices in your field of view, you execute the hack.* 

To use Street Hack, the device must be \[Close\] or \[Nearby\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                  |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | You have no access and trigger a \[Security Alert\]          |
| Fade         | You are an \[Operator\] and have \[Stress\] vs. \[Security\] |
| Success      | You are an \[Operator\]                                      |
| Cool Success | You are an \[Admin\]                                         |
