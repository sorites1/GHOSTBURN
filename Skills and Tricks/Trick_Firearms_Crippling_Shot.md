## Crippling Shot \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | \[Attack\], \[Gun\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Two Hands\], \[Visual\] |

*You take careful aim and target one of your opponent's legs with your shot.* 

When you use Crippling Shot, your target must be at the same range as the optimal range listed for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Single Shot \[Damage Array\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                   |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again.      |
| Fade         | You deal \[Minimum Damage\]                                                   |
| Success      | You deal \[Normal Damage\] and they gain \[Movement Restricted\]              |
| Cool Success | You deal \[Maximum Damage\] and they gain \[Movement Impaired\] and \[Prone\] |
