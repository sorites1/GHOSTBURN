## Convince \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Persuade | \[Attitude\], \[Language\], \[Social\], \[Vocal\] |

*By appealing to the other person's sensibilities, you find a way to convince them to help you or to see things from your point of view.* 

| Outcome      | Description                                                     |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They are unconvinced and their \[Attitude\] drops one level     |
| Fade         | They agree (with some reservations) and they gain \[Confident\] |
| Success      | They agree                                                      |
| Cool Success | They agree and their \[Attitude\] goes up one level             |
