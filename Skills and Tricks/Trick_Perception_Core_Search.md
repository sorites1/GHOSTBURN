## Search \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                           |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | \[Move\], \[Physical\], \[Visual\] |

*You move about and study your surroundings for hidden people or objects.* 

When you use Search, define the scope of your search to the GM, such as *I want to search the desk* or *I want to scan the rooftops*. Then roll Search. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                             |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You find something unpleasant and you are \[Shaken\]                    |
| Fade         | The GM tells you what you find (if anything) and you are \[Distracted\] |
| Success      | The GM tells you what you find (if anything)                            |
| Cool Success | The GM tells you what you find (if anything) and you learn a \[Secret\] |
