## Strafing Run \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                              |
| --------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Piloting | \[Attack\], \[Control Deck\], \[Datajack\], \[Drone\], \[Fly\], \[Physical\], \[Two Hands\], \[Visual\], \[Wireless\] |

*You fly your drone low and fast, firing its automatic weapons to rain down lead on your opponents.*

To use Strafing Run, your \[Drone\] must be outfitted with an automatic weapon. You may target up to three opponents with Strafing Run, all of which must be \[Nearby\] your \[Drone\] and each other. Use the weapon's Full Auto \[Damage Array\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You miss, \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\], and are \[Distracted]                              |
| Fade         | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\]                           |
| Success      | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\] away                         |
| Cool Success | They take \[Normal Damage\] and are \[Suppressed\], and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\] away |
