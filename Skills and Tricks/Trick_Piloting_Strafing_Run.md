## Strafing Run \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                              |
| --------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Piloting | \[[[Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Keyword_Control_Deck\|Control Deck]]\], \[[[Keyword_Datajack\|Datajack]]\], \[[[Keyword_Drone\|Drone]]\], \[[[Keyword_Fly\|Fly]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\], \[[[Keyword_Wireless\|Wireless]]\] |

*You fly your drone low and fast, firing its automatic weapons to rain down lead on your opponents.*

To use Strafing Run, your \[[[Keyword_Drone|Drone]]\] must be outfitted with an automatic weapon. You may target up to three opponents with Strafing Run, all of which must be \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] your \[Drone\] and each other. Use the weapon's Full Auto \[[[Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You miss, \[[[Keyword_Fly\|Fly]]\] the \[[[Keyword_Drone\|Drone]]\] \[[[Keyword_Nearby\|Nearby]]\], and are \[Distracted]                              |
| Fade         | They take \[[[Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\]                           |
| Success      | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[[[Keyword_Far\|Far]]\] away                         |
| Cool Success | They take \[Normal Damage\] and are \[[[Condition_Suppressed\|Suppressed]]\], and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\] away |
