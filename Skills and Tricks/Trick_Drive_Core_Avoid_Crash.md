## Avoid Crash \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                 |
| --------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | \[[[Keyword_Drone\|Drone]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*In a split second, you react to an oncoming hazard and swerve to avoid a crash.* 

When you are driving a \[[[Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] at \[[[Keyword_High_Speed|High Speed]]\] and a sudden obstacle or hazard appears on the road ahead or if your vehicle \[[[Keyword_Crashes|Crashes]]\] for another reason, you can use a \[[[Keyword_Reaction|Reaction]]\] to use Avoid Crash. The \[[[Keyword_Handling|Handling]]\] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Avoid Crash roll. If you use only \[[[Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                         |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Your \[[[Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\] \[[[Keyword_Crashes\|Crashes]]\]                                        |
| Fade         | You avoid the obstacle or hazard (near miss) and you are \[[[Condition_Shaken\|Shaken]]\] |
| Success      | You avoid the obstacle or hazard                                    |
| Cool Success | You avoid the obstacle or hazard and are \[[[Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]               |
