## Silent Takedown \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Brawl | \[Attack\], \[Unarmed\], \[Physical\], \[Two Hands\] \[Visual\] |

*You swiftly and silently close in on your opponent and execute a maneuver to quietly subdue them before they have time to react.* 

To use Silent Takedown, you must be \[Undetected\], and your opponent must be \[Nearby\]. When you use this trick, you silently \[Move\] \[Close\] to them and roll Silent Takedown. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                           |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You fumble the maneuver, lose \[Undetected\], and are \[Off Balance\] |
| Fade         | They are \[Stunned\] but cry out, alerting anyone \[Nearby\]          |
| Success      | They are \[Stunned\]                                                  |
| Cool Success | They are \[Unconscious\]                                              |
