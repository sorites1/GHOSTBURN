## Mock \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Intimidate | \[Concentrate\], \[Language\], \[Range\], \[Social\], \[Vocal\] |

*You hone in on your opponent's insecurities and ruthlessly mock them, undermining their confidence.*

To use Mock on an opponent, they must be \[Close\] or \[Nearby\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become \[Confident\] |
| Fade         | You are both \[Distracted\]                                |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                                    |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are \[Inspired\]           |
