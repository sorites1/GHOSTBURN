## Mock \[Core Trick\]

*You hone in on your opponent's insecurities and ruthlessly mock them, undermining their confidence.*

To use Mock on an opponent, they must be \[[[Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become \[[[Condition_Confident\|Confident]]\]                        |
| Fade         | You are both \[[[Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] and their \[[[Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\] worsens |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\] and their \[[[Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\] worsens                               |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are \[[[Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                                   |
