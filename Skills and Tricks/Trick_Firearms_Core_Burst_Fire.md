## Burst Fire \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | \[Gun\], \[Attack\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Two Hands\], \[Visual\] |

*You grip your weapon and issue a three-round burst.* 

When you use Burst Fire, your target must be at the same range as the optimal range listed for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. If you use \[One Hand\], raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Burst Fire \[Damage Array\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                              |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again. |
| Fade         | You deal \[Minimum Damage\]                                              |
| Success      | Choose to either deal \[Normal Damage\] or make them \[Suppressed\]      |
| Cool Success | You deal \[Maximum Damage\] and they are \[Suppressed\]                  |
