## Burst Fire \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | \[[[Keyword_Gun\|Gun]]\], \[[[Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You grip your weapon and issue a three-round burst.* 

When you use Burst Fire, your target must be at the \[[[Keyword_Optimal_Range|Optimal Range]]\] for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. You may use \[[[Keyword_Spray|Spray]]\] to hit an additional target. If you use \[[[Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Burst Fire \[[[Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                              |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again. |
| Fade         | You deal \[[[Keyword_Minimum_Damage\|Minimum Damage]]\]                                              |
| Success      | Choose to either deal \[[[Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\] or make them \[[[Condition_Suppressed\|Suppressed]]\]      |
| Cool Success | You deal \[[[Keyword_Maximum_Damage\|Maximum Damage]]\] and they are \[Suppressed\]                  |
