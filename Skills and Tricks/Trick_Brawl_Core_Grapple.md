## Grapple \[Core Trick\]

Without this trick, 

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[[[Keyword_Opposed\|Opposed]]\]           | Brawl | \[[[Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You run after your opponent, grabbing them and tacking them to the ground.* 

When you use Grapple, \[[[Keyword_Move|Move]]\] to an opponent who is \[[[Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] and then roll Grapple.

| Outcome      | Description                                        |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You are \[[[Condition_Restrained\|Restrained]]\] and you are both \[[[Condition_Prone\|Prone]]\]  |
| Fade         | They are \[Restrained\] and you are both \[Prone\] |
| Success      | They are \[Restrained\] and \[Prone\]              |
| Cool Success | They are \[Restrained\], \[Prone\] and \[[[Condition_Shaken\|Shaken]]\]  |
