## Grapple \[Core Trick\]

Without this trick, 

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[Opposed\]           | Brawl | \[Attack\], \[Move\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Visual\] |

*You run after your opponent, grabbing them and tacking them to the ground.* 

When you use Grapple, \[Move\] to an opponent who is \[Close\] or \[Nearby\ and then roll Grapple.

| Outcome      | Description                                        |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You are \[Restrained\] and you are both \[Prone\]  |
| Fade         | They are \[Restrained\] and you are both \[Prone\] |
| Success      | They are \[Restrained\] and \[Prone\]              |
| Cool Success | They are \[Restrained\], \[Prone\] and \[Shaken\]  |
