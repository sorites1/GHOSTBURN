## Escape \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                     |
| --------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*When your opponent falls behind, you seize the opportunity and drive your car out of sight to lose them completely.* 

When you are driving a \[[[Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] that's the target of a Chase and your opponent falls behind, you may use a \[[[Keyword_Reaction|Reaction]]\] to Escape. The \[[[Keyword_Handling|Handling]]\] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Lose Them roll. If you use only \[[[Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Your \[[[Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\] takes 3 damage and the other \[Vehicle\] is \[[[Keyword_Close\|Close]]\] behind you |
| Fade         | You get away, the chase ends, and your \[Vehicle\] takes 3 damage                 |
| Success      | You get away and the chase ends                                                   |
| Cool Success | You get away, the chase ends, and you are \[[[Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                            |
