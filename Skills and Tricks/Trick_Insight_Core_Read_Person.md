## Read Person \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                    |
| --------------------- | ------- | --------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Insight | \[Concentrate\], \[Visual\] |

*You surmise information about a person by studying them, the way they speak, the clothes they're wearing, their facial expressions, and body language.* 

When you use Read Person, ask the GM to identify a person's current emotional state, immediate goals, or way of thinking. To use Read Person on someone, they must be \[Close\] or \[Nearby\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You do not learn any information and you are \[Distracted\] |
| Fade         | GM gives you information and you are \[Distracted\]         |
| Success      | GM gives you information                                    |
| Cool Success | GM gives you information and you gain a \[Secret\]          |
