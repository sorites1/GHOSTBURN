## Shakedown \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | \[Language\], \[Social\], \[Vocal\] |

*By making veiled threats to your victim, you make them see that it's in their best interests to give you a "gift."* 

You can suggest what you would like to receive, but the GM is not obligated to have the NPC grant you a specific \[Gift.\]

| Outcome      | Description                                           |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They are not intimidated and they \[Break\]           |
| Fade         | They give you a minor \[Gift\] and gain \[Confident\] |
| Success      | They give you a regular \[Gift\]                      |
| Cool Success | They give you an expensive \[Gift\] and a \[Secret\]  |
