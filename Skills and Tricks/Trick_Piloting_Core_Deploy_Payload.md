## Deploy Payload \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                              |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Piloting | \[Concentrate\], \[Control Deck\], \[Datajack\], \[Drone\], \[Fly\], \[Range\], \[Physical\], \[Two Hands\], \[Visual\], \[Wireless\] |

*Having piloted your drone to its destination, you carefully position it and deploy its payload.*

To use Deploy Payload, the spot where you want to drop your payload, called the mark, must be \[Nearby\]. When you use Deploy Payload, describe the mark, such as *I want to place the package in front of the door*, or *I want to drop the grenade on the truck*. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\] miss the mark, and are \[Distracted\] |
| Fade         | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\], hit the mark, and are \[Distracted\] |
| Success      | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\] and hit the mark                      |
| Cool Success | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\], hit the mark, and are \[Inspired\]   |
