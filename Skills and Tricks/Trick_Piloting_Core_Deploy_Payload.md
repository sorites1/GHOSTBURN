## Deploy Payload \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                              |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Piloting | \[[[Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Keyword_Control_Deck\|Control Deck]]\], \[[[Keyword_Datajack\|Datajack]]\], \[[[Keyword_Drone\|Drone]]\], \[[[Keyword_Fly\|Fly]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\], \[[[Keyword_Wireless\|Wireless]]\] |

*Having piloted your drone to its destination, you carefully position it and deploy its payload.*

To use Deploy Payload, the spot where you want to drop your payload, called the mark, must be \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. When you use Deploy Payload, describe the mark, such as *I want to place the package in front of the door*, or *I want to drop the grenade on the truck*. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You \[[[Keyword_Fly\|Fly]]\] the \[[[Keyword_Drone\|Drone]]\] \[[[Keyword_Nearby\|Nearby]]\] miss the mark, and are \[[[Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] |
| Fade         | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\], hit the mark, and are \[Distracted\] |
| Success      | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\] and hit the mark                      |
| Cool Success | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\], hit the mark, and are \[[[Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]   |
