## Misdirect \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Deception | \[Attitude\], \[Language\], \[One Hand\]. \[Social\], \[Vocal\] |

*Whether by shouting and pointing or simply by talking and using one hand, you momentarily distract your opponent.*

To use Misdirect on someone, they must have an \[Attitude\] of Friendly or Neutral towards you.

| Outcome      | Description                                       |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They become \[Suspicious\] and gain \[Confident\] |
| Fade         | They are \[Distracted\] and become \[Suspicious\] |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                           |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are \[Inspired\]  |
