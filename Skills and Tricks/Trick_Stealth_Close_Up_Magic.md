## Close Up Magic \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                                                         |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Stealth | \[Fine Control\], \[Physical\], \[Range\], \[Social\], \[Two Hands\], \[Visual\] |

*You dazzle a nearby audience with astounding sleight of hand, making small items vanish and reappear to entertain and bewilder them.* 

You may use \[Close Up Magic\] on one person who is \[Close\] to you or on a \[Group\] that is \[Nearby\]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                         |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Your poor execution makes them \[Confident\]        |
| Fade         | They are \[Entertained\] and you are \[Distracted\] |
| Success      | They are \[Entertained\]                            |
| Cool Success | They are \[Entertained\] and \[Bewildered\]         |
