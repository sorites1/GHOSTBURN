## Sneak \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                           |
| --------------------- | ------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | \[Move\], \[Physical\], \[Visual\] |

*By moving slowly and quietly and by staying behind cover and out of sight, you sneak to your destination.* 

To use Sneak, you must be \[Undetected\] by the people you're avoiding. If multiple observers are present, use the highest Wits amongst them.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                     |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You lose \[Undetected\] and their \[Attitude\] drops one level                  |
| Fade         | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\], remain \[Undetected], and have \[Stress\] vs. \[Wits\] |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and remain \[Undetected\]                               |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and are \[Hidden\]                                      |
