## First Aid \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                               |
| --------------------- | -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Stabilization DL      | Medicine | \[[[Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Supplies\|Supplies]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*When a friend falls in combat or suffers from another treatable condition, you rush to their side and use your medical knowledge to stabilize them.*

If you use First Aid without \[[[Keyword_Supplies|Supplies]]\] raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                              |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | They take 2 damage and gain \[[[Condition_Bleeding\|Bleeding]]\]                                 |
| Fade         | They are \[[[Condition_Stabilized\|Stabilized]]\] and you have \[[[Condition_Stress\|Stress]]\] vs. \[[[Keyword_Stabilization_DL\|Stabilization DL]]\] |
| Success      | They are \[Stabilized\] and gain 2 hp                                    |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stabilized\] and heal 2 hp + your Medicine bonus              |
