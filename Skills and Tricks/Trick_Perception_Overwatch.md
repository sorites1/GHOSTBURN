## Overwatch \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | \[[[Keyword_Auditory\|Auditory]]\], \[[[Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], [[Keyword_Language\|Language]], \[Verbal\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You survey the combat zone and relay relevant information to your crew, noting the positions and movements of friends, enemies, and civilians.* 

When you use Overwatch, pick one ally in the same battle as you. The ally must have hands-free wireless comms (headset or cyberware) to benefit from this trick. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                      |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They get no benefit and you are \[[[Condition_Confused\|Confused]]\]                                     |
| Fade         | Their next \[[[Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] is \[[[Keyword_Upgraded\|Upgraded]]\] and you are \[[[Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\]                 |
| Success      | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\]                                            |
| Cool Success | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\] and they gain 1 \[[[Keyword_Temporary\|Temporary]]\] \[[[Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\] |
