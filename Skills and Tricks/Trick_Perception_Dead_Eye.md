## Dead Eye \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                     |
| --------------------- | ---------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | \[Gun\], \[One Hand\] \[Ranged\], \[Visual\] |

*You zero in on your opponent and raise your gun. One heartbeat later, you send hot lead their way.* 

To use Dead Eye on an opponent, they must be \[Close\] or \[Nearby\]. If you are wielding a pistol, lower the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                        |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Your next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on anyone this turn is \[Downgraded\]                        |
| Fade         | Your next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is \[Upgraded\] and you are \[Distracted\] |
| Success      | The next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is \[Upgraded\]                             |
| Cool Success | The next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is is an automatic \[Cool Success\]         |
