# Shakedown \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\] |

*By making veiled threats to your victim, you make them see that it's in their best interests to give you a "gift."* 

You can suggest what you would like to receive, but the GM is not obligated to have the NPC grant you a specific \[Gift.\]

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They give you an expensive \[Gift\] and a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Secret\|Secret]]\]                                                                                                     |
| Success      | They give you a regular \[Gift\]                                                                                                                                                      |
| Fade         | They give you a minor \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Gift\|Gift]]\], their \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\] worsens, and they gain \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident\|Confident]]\] |
| Glitch       | They are not intimidated, their \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\] worsens, and they \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Break\|Break]]\]                                               |
