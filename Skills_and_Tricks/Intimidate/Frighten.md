# Frighten \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\] |

*You turn to an individual and look them in the eye. Maybe you say a few words, or you might just let your body language do the talking. Regardless, they are left broken from the brief exchange.* 

If you use Frighten without speaking, raise the DL by one. You cannot use this trick on someone who is currently suffering from a \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Break|Break]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They faint and are rendered \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Unconscious\|Unconscious]]\]              |
| Success      | They \[Break\] and flee                                                                        |
| Fade         | They \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Break\|Break]]\] and \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] you |
| Glitch       | They are not scared of you and they gain \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident\|Confident]]\]     |
