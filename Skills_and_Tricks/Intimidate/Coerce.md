# Coerce \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\] |

*With threats of damage to income, reputation, or bodily wellbeing, you force your victim to comply with your instructions.* 

If you use Coerce on a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far|Far]]\] away target or remotely, such as over a call, raise the DL by one. If your instructions would put the target or their loved ones in a dangerous situation, raise the DL by one. The target will outright refuse instructions that would result in serious injury or certain death. If you threaten the target with a deadly weapon as part of your Coerce attempt, lower the DL by one. If you use Coerce to threaten a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group|Group]]\], use the highest \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fort|Fort]]\] amongst them and apply the outcome to the \[Group\].
