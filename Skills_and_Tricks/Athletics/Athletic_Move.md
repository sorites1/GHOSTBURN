# Athletic Move \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                            |
| --------------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Athletics | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\], (\[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\]) |

*You engage in a physical activity like climbing, jumping, or swimming.* 

Athletic Move is used to \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move|Move]]\] by climbing, jumping, or swimming, When you use Physical Activity, state the desired outcome of your \[Move\], such as, "I want to climb over the fence or I want jump from this rooftop to the next one." If the desired outcome is unlikely, raise the DL by one, and if it is highly unlikely, raise the DL by two.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You get the desired outcome and recover 1 AP                                                   |
| Success      | You get the desired outcome                                                                    |
| Fade         | You get the desired outcome but you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\|Off Balance]]\]  |
| Glitch       | You fall \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone\|Prone]]\] and may take falling damage (GM discretion) |
