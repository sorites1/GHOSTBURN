# Read Person \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                    |
| --------------------- | ------- | --------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Insight | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You surmise information about a person by studying them, the way they speak, the clothes they're wearing, their facial expressions, and body language.* 

When you use Read Person, ask the GM to identify a person's current emotional state, immediate goals, or way of thinking. To use Read Person on someone, they must be \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | GM gives you information and you gain a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Secret\|Secret]]\]                  |
| Success      | GM gives you information                                                                         |
| Fade         | GM gives you information and you are \[Distracted\]                                              |
| Glitch       | You do not learn any information and you are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] |
