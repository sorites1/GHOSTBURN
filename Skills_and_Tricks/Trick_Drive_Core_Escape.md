# Escape \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                     |
| --------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*When your opponent falls behind, you seize the opportunity and drive your car out of sight to lose them completely.* 

When you are driving a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] that's the target of a Chase and your opponent falls behind, you may use a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Reaction|Reaction]]\] to Escape. The \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Handling|Handling]]\] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Lose Them roll. If you use only \[[[Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You get away, the chase ends, and you are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                                                   |
| Success      | You get away and the chase ends                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | You get away, the chase ends, and your \[Vehicle\] takes 3 damage                                                                           |
| Glitch       | Your \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\] takes 3 damage and the other \[Vehicle\] is \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Close\|Close]]\] behind you |
