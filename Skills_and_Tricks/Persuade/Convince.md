# Convince \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                             |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Wits                  | Persuade | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\] |

*By appealing to the other person's sensibilities, you find a way to convince them to help you or to see things from your point of view.* 

If you use Convince on a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far|Far]]\] away target or remotely, such as over a call, raise the DL by one. If you offer a suitable bribe, lower the DL by one. Some targets may be offended by the offer a bribe (GM's discretion), in which case the DL is raised by one instead. If you use Convince on a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group|Group]]\], use the highest \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Wits|Wits]]\] amongst them and apply the outcome to the \[Group\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They agree and their \[Attitude\] improves                                                                  |
| Success      | They agree                                                                                                  |
| Fade         | They agree (with some reservations) and they gain \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident\|Confident]]\] |
| Glitch       | They are unconvinced and they gain \[Confident\]                                                            |
