# Haggle \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                  |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Persuade | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\], |

*With a combination of confidence, market knowledge, and a bit of savvy, you negotiate over the price of goods or services.* 

In some situations, such as when you purchase from a fixed-price vendor, it is not possible to use Haggle. Attempting to Haggle with someone who has a Hostile \[Attitude\] towards you automatically fails.

| Outcome      | Description                                                        |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | You get 20% better price and a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Gift\|Gift]]\] |
| Success      | You get 20% better price                                           |
| Fade         | You get 20% better price and their \[Attitude\] worsens            |
| Glitch       | You get 20% worse price and their \[Attitude\] worsens             |
