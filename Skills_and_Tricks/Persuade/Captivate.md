# Captivate \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                       |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Persuade | \[Attitude\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visible\|Visible]]\] |

*With a simple change in posture, a strut in your step, or a flick of your hair, you can put on "the look" that makes everyone sit up and take notice.* 

To use this trick, the opponent(s) must be \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. If there are multiple onlookers, use the highest Wits amongst the \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group|Group]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                          |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Bewildered\|Bewildered]]\] and you gain an \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Ally\|Ally]]\] |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                                                                                              |
| Fade         | They are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] and their \[Attitude\] worsens                          |
| Glitch       | They are not impressed and suffer a \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Break\|Break]]\]                                        |
