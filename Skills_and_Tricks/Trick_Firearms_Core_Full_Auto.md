## Full Auto \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Severe (26)           | Firearms | \[[[Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Keyword_Gun\|Gun]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You hold the trigger and lay down a continuous stream of hot lead.* 

When you use Full Auto, your target must be at the \[[[Keyword_Optimal_Range|Optimal Range]]\] for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. You may use \[[[Keyword_Spray|Spray]]\] to hit up to four additional targets (five total). Use your weapon's Full Auto \[[[Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                              |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again. |
| Fade         | You deal \[[[Keyword_Minimum_Damage\|Minimum Damage]]\]                                              |
| Success      | You deal \[[[Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\] and they are \[[[Condition_Suppressed\|Suppressed]]\]                   |
| Cool Success | You deal \[[[Keyword_Maximum_Damage\|Maximum Damage]]\] and they are \[Suppressed\]                  |
