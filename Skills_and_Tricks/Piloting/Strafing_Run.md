# Strafing Run \[Signature Trick\]

*You fly your drone low and fast, firing its automatic weapons to rain down lead on your opponents.*

To use Strafing Run, your \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone|Drone]]\] must be outfitted with an automatic weapon. You may target up to three opponents with Strafing Run, all of which must be \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] your \[Drone\] and each other. Use the weapon's Full Auto \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] with this trick.
