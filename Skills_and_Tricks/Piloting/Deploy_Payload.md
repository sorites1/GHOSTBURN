# Deploy Payload \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                              |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Piloting | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Control_Deck\|Control Deck]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Datajack\|Datajack]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone\|Drone]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fly\|Fly]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Wireless\|Wireless]]\] |

*Having piloted your drone to its destination, you carefully position it and deploy its payload.*

To use Deploy Payload, describe the spot \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] where you want to drop your payload. This spot is called *the mark*. For example, *I want to place the package in front of the door*, or *I want to drop the grenade on the truck*. If the mark is in a spot that is hard to navigate, the the GM should raise the DL by one. If the mark is extremely hard to navigate, raise the DL by two.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\], hit the mark, and are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                                 |
| Success      | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\] and hit the mark                                                                                       |
| Fade         | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\] but fail to deploy your payload and gain \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stress\|Stress]]\] vs. Moderate (14) |
| Glitch       | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close\|Close]]\], miss the mark, and have a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Bad_Memory\|Bad Memory]]\]  |
