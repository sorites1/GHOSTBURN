# Full Auto \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Severe (26)           | Firearms | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Gun\|Gun]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You hold the trigger and lay down a continuous stream of hot lead.* 

When you use Full Auto, your target must be at the \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range|Optimal Range]]\] for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. You may use \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Spray|Spray]]\] to hit up to four additional targets (five total). Use your weapon's Full Auto \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] with this trick.

| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\|Maximum Damage]]\] and they are \[Suppressed\]                                                                |
| Success      | You deal \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\] and they are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suppressed\|Suppressed]]\]                             |

| Glitch       | Your weapon is \[Out of Ammo\]                                                                                                                              |
