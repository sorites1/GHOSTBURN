# Sense Lies \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                                |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Insight | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Auditory\|Auditory]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*By listening to the speaker's words and tone and reading their body language, you sense that they are lying.* 

When someone speaks, you may use a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Reaction|Reaction]]\] to Sense Lies.  If you are unable to see the speaker, raise the DL by one. The scope of Sense Lies is one statement You must roll Sense Lies again each time you want to determine if a statement is a lie. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You automatically know if they're lying for the rest of the scene                                                                               |
| Success      | You know if they lied                                                                                                                           |
| Fade         | You know if they lied and you are \[Distracted\]                                                                                                |
| Glitch       | You don't know if they lied and you are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] and \[[[Conditions/Condition_Confused\|Confused]]\] |
