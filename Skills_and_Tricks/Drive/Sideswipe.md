# Sideswipe \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                    |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Drive | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You pull up alongside another driver and then violently swerve into them, damaging both of your vehicles.* 

To use Sideswipe, your \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] must be \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] to the other \[Vehicle\]. The \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Handling|Handling]]\] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Sideswipe roll. If you use only \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                            |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Their \[Vehicle\] takes 20 damage and the other driver is \[Shaken\]                                                                   |
| Success      | Their \[Vehicle\] takes 15 damage and yours takes 5 damage                                                                             |
| Fade         | Both your and their \[Vehicle\] take 10 damage                                                                                         |
| Glitch       | You miss them, your \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\] takes 10 damage, and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Shaken\|Shaken]]\] |
