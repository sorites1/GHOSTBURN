# Chase \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You spot the car you're chasing up ahead and rapidly accelerate to catch up.* 

When you use Chase, you identify a target \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] ahead of you (\[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far|Far]]\]). If the other \[Vehicle\] is traveling at \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_High_Speed|High Speed]]\], you must do the same (which raises the DL by one). If the other \[Vehicle\] is \[Far\] away, raise the DL by one. The \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Handling|Handling]]\] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Chase roll. If you use only \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] \[Close\] to the other \[Vehicle\] and may overtake (get ahead) if desired                                                            |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Close\] to the other \[Vehicle\]                                                                                                    |
| Fade         | You \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close\|Close]]\] to the other \[Vehicle\] and you are \[Distracted\]                  |
| Glitch       | You fall behind and are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] and the other \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\] may use Escape |
