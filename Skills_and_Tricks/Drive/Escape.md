# Escape \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                     |
| --------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*When your opponent falls behind, you seize the opportunity and drive your car out of sight to lose them completely.* 

When you are driving a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] that's the target of a Chase and your opponent falls behind, you may use a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction|Reaction]]\] to Escape. The \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Handling|Handling]]\] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Escape roll. If you use only \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You get away, the chase ends, and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                     |
| Success      | You get away and the chase ends                                                                               |
| Fade         | You get away, the chase ends, and your \[Vehicle\] takes 3 damage                                             |
| Glitch       | Your \[Vehicle\] takes 3 damage and the other \[Vehicle\] is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close\|Close]]\] behind you |
