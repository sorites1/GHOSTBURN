*You hone in on your opponent's insecurities and ruthlessly mock them, undermining their confidence.*

To use Mock on an opponent, they must be \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                                            |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\] and their \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\] worsens                                          |
| Fade         | You are both \[[[Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] and their \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\] worsens |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become \[[[Conditions/Condition_Confident\|Confident]]\]                                 |
