# Corporate Culture \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords        |
| --------------------- | ------- | --------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Culture | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\] |

*You are plugged in to the corporate world of cutthroat business.* 

When you use Corporate Culture, you ask the GM a question related to corporations, what they provide, business processes, c-suite execs, scandals, lawsuits, etc. For example, you might ask, *What do I know about Knifepoint Capital?* or *What corporations have a beef with Knifepoint Capital?* 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | The GM answers the question and you gain a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Secret\|Secret]]\]         |
| Success      | The GM answers the question                                                                |
| Fade         | The GM answers the question and you gain a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Bad_Memory\|Bad Memory]]\] |
| Glitch       | You don't know and you gain a \[Bad Memory\]                                               |
