## Disguise \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                         |
| --------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Deception | \[[[Keyword_Advanced_Supplies\|Advanced Supplies]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Supplies\|Supplies]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\] |

*Using clothing, makeup, and props, you create a disguise that makes you unrecognizable.*

When you use Disguise, you use \[[[Keyword_Supplies|Supplies]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Advanced_Supplies|Advanced Supplies]]\] to create the desired look. \[Advanced Supplies\] allow you replicate another person's appearance and raise the DL by one. This trick takes one hour to complete. If you rush, raise the DL by one and it takes 30 minutes.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Unusable disguise, your time and \[[[Keyword_Supplies\|Supplies]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Advanced_Supplies\|Advanced Supplies]]\] are wasted               |
| Fade         | NPCs are automatically \[Suspicious\] and roll Threat vs. Moderate (14) DL to see through it    |
| Success      | A \[Suspicious\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Hard (20) DL to see through it                            |
| Cool Success | A \[Suspicious\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Severe (26) DL to see through it and you are \[[[Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\] |
