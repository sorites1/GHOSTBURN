## Captivate \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                       |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Persuade | \[Attitude\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visible\|Visible]]\] |

*With a simple change in posture, a strut in your step, or a flick of your hair, you can put on "the look" that makes everyone sit up and take notice.* 

To use this trick, the opponent(s) must be \[[[Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. If there are multiple onlookers, use the highest Wits amongst the \[[[Keyword_Group|Group]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They are not impressed and suffer a \[[[Condition_Break\|Break]]\]                             |
| Fade         | They are \[[[Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] and their \[Attitude\] worsens               |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                                                                        |
| Cool Success | They are \[[[Condition_Bewildered\|Bewildered]]\] and you gain an \[[[Condition_Ally\|Ally]]\] |
