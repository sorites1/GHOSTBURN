# Avoid Crash \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                 |
| --------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Drone\|Drone]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*In a split second, you react to an oncoming hazard and swerve to avoid a crash.* 

When you are driving a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] at \[[[Keywords/Keyword_High_Speed|High Speed]]\] and a sudden obstacle or hazard appears on the road ahead or if your vehicle \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Crashes|Crashes]]\] for another reason, you can use a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Reaction|Reaction]]\] to use Avoid Crash. The \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Handling|Handling]]\] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Avoid Crash roll. If you use only \[[[Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                          |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You avoid the obstacle or hazard and are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]             |
| Success      | You avoid the obstacle or hazard                                                                     |
| Fade         | You avoid the obstacle or hazard (near miss) and you are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Shaken\|Shaken]]\] |
| Glitch       | Your \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\] \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Crashes\|Crashes]]\]             |
