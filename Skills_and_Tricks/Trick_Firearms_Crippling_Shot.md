# Crippling Shot \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Gun\|Gun]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You take careful aim and target one of your opponent's legs with your shot.* 

When you use Crippling Shot, if the target is at a \[Range\] other than the weapon's \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range|Optimal Range]]\], raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Single Shot \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\|Maximum Damage]]\] and they gain \[[[Conditions/Condition_Movement_Impaired\|Movement Impaired]]\] and \[[[Conditions/Condition_Prone\|Prone]]\] |
| Success      | You deal \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\] and they gain \[[[Conditions/Condition_Movement_Restricted\|Movement Restricted]]\]                                             |
| Fade         | You deal \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage\|Minimum Damage]]\] and they gain a  \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Combat_Opportunity\|Combat Opportunity]]\]                                              |
| Glitch       | You miss the shot and they gain a \[Combat Opportunity\]                                                                                                                                       |
