*Having piloted your drone to its destination, you carefully position it and deploy its payload.*

To use Deploy Payload, the spot where you want to drop your payload, called the mark, must be \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. When you use Deploy Payload, describe the mark, such as *I want to place the package in front of the door*, or *I want to drop the grenade on the truck*. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\], hit the mark, and are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                                                                                        |
| Success      | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\] and hit the mark                                                                                                                                              |
| Fade         | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\], hit the mark, and are \[Distracted\]                                                                                                                         |
| Glitch       | You \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Fly\|Fly]]\] the \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Drone\|Drone]]\] \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Nearby\|Nearby]]\] miss the mark, and are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] |
