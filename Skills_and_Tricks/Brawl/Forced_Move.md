# Forced Move \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Opposed\|Opposed]]\]           | Brawl | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You grab your opponent and force them to move with your superior ability.* 

To use Forced Move, your opponent must be \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\]. If they are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Restrained|Restrained]]\], your Forced Move outcome is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded|Upgraded]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You force them to \[Move\] and put them in \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Agony\|Agony]]\] if desired |
| Success      | You force them to \[Move\]                                                                      |
| Fade         | You force them to \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] and you are \[Off Balance\]               |
| Glitch       | You fail and are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\|Off Balance]]\]                          |
