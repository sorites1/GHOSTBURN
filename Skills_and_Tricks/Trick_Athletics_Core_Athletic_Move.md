## Athletic Move \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                            |
| --------------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Athletics | \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\], (\[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\]) |

*You engage in a physical activity like climbing, jumping, or swimming.* 

Athletic Move is used to \[[[Keyword_Move|Move]]\] by climbing, jumping, or swimming, When you use Physical Activity, state the desired outcome of your \[Move\], such as *I want to climb over the fence or I want jump from this rooftop to the next one.* If the desired outcome is unlikely, raise the DL by one, and if it is highly unlikely, raise the DL by two.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                         |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You fall \[[[Condition_Prone\|Prone]]\] and may take falling damage (GM discretion) |
| Fade         | You get the desired outcome but you are \[[[Condition_Off_Balance\|Off Balance]]\]  |
| Success      | You get the desired outcome                                                         |
| Cool Success | You get the desired outcome and recover 1 AP                                        |
