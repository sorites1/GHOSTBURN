## Disappear \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*By capitalizing on a sudden distraction, you slip away with not even a whisper.* 

When one or more observers \[[[Keyword_Close|Close]]\] to you become \[[[Condition_Distracted|Distracted]]\], you may use a \[[[Keyword_Reaction|Reaction]]\] to Disappear. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You are seen and their \[Attitude\] becomes Hostile                               |
| Fade         | You \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] \[[[Keyword_Nearby\|Nearby]]\], gain \[Undetected] and have \[[[Condition_Stress\|Stress]]\] vs. Moderate (14) |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and gain \[[[Condition_Undetected\|Undetected]]\]                                   |
| Cool Success | You escape and \[Move\] \[[[Keyword_Far\|Far]]\] away                                              |
