# Sneak \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                                                                                                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*By moving slowly and quietly and by staying behind cover and out of sight, you sneak to your destination.* 

To use Sneak, you must be \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Undetected|Undetected]]\] by the people you're avoiding. If multiple observers are present, use the highest Wits amongst them.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Hidden\|Hidden]]\]                                                                                                                                   |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and remain \[Undetected\]                                                                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | You \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] somewhere \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby\|Nearby]]\], remain \[Undetected], and have \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stress\|Stress]]\] vs. \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Wits\|Wits]]\] |
| Glitch       | You lose \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Undetected\|Undetected]]\] and their \[Attitude\] drops one level                                                                                                           |
