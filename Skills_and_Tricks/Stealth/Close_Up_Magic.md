You may use \[Close Up Magic\] on one person who is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] to you or on a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group|Group]]\] that is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\].
