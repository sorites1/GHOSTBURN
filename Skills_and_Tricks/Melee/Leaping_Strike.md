# Leaping Strike \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                          |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Opposed\|Opposed]]\]           | Melee | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[Melee], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\|One Hand]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You get a running start, fall from above, or swing in on a rope to perform a mid-air strike.* 

To use Leaping Strike, first make a basic Athletics roll with a DL of Moderate (14). If you fail, you fall \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone|Prone]]\] and your action ends. Otherwise, you automatically \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move|Move]]\] through the air to a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] opponent by jumping, swinging, or falling, for example, and roll Leaping Strike. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\|Maximum Damage]]\] to them and they are \[Prone\] |
| Success      | You deal \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\] to them                          |
| Fade         | You deal \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage\|Minimum Damage]]\] to them                        |
| Glitch       | You miss and fall \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone\|Prone]]\]                                     |
