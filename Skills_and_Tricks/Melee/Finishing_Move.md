# Finishing Move \[Signature Trick\]

*You sense your opponent's weakness and use your weapon to finish them.* 

When an opponent who is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] goes from \[Fine\] to \[Hurting\], you can react. You \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move|Move]]\] to the opponent, if they are not already \[Close\], and roll Finishing Move. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                            |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are dead                                                          |
| Success      | You deal \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\|Maximum Damage]]\]       |
| Fade         | You deal \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\]         |
| Glitch       | You miss and are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\|Off Balance]]\] |
