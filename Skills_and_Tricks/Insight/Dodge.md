# Dodge \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                               |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Insight | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You sense the attack almost before you see it and deftly sidestep and duck out of harms way.* 

When you are the target of a [[Skills_and_Tricks/Brawl/Brawl_Skill|Brawl]] or [[Skills_and_Tricks/Melee/Melee_Skill|Melee]] \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack|Attack]]\], you may spend a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction|Reaction]]\] to roll Dodge. After you roll Dodge, the attacker rolls their Brawl or Melee \[Attack\] and modifies their \[Attack\] outcome based on your Dodge outcome.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Their  \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] outcome is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded\|Downgraded]]\] and if you would take \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Absolute_Damage\|Absolute Damage]]\], you instead take no damage and may immediately \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby\|Nearby]]\] |
| Success      | Their  \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] outcome  is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded\|Downgraded]]\]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Fade         | Their \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] outcome is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded\|Downgraded]]\] and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\]                                                                                                                                                              |
| Glitch       | You are unable to mitigate the \[Attack\] and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
