# Disguise \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                         |
| --------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Deception | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies\|Advanced Supplies]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies\|Supplies]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\] |

*Using clothing, makeup, or props, you create a disguise.*

When you use Disguise, you create a desired look using \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies|Supplies]]\], which may include clothing, makeup, and basic accessories. You can use Disguise to produce one of three effects. First, you can make yourself unrecognizable. Second, you can create a look that helps you 'fit in' to a specific environment. Third, if you have \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies|Advanced Supplies]]\], which include prosthetics, wigs, and so forth, you can replicate another person's physical appearance, though this raises the DL by one. You may combine this third use of Disguise with [[Impression|Impression]] to "become" the person you pretend to be. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | A \[Suspicious\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Severe (26) DL to see through the disguise, and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                                            |
| Success      | A \[Suspicious\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Hard (20) DL to see through the disguise                                                                                                                   |
| Fade         | A \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suspicous\|Suspicious]]\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Moderate (14) DL to see through the disguise                                                                   |
| Glitch       | Your disguise is unusable, and your \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies\|Supplies]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies\|Advanced Supplies]]\] may be wasted (GM's discretion) |
