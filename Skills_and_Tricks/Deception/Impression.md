# Impression \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                           |
| --------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Hard (20)             | Deception | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\] |

*By altering your voice, facial expressions, and even the way you sit or stand, you do a convincing impression of another person.*

You can use Impression to achieve three different effects. First, you can use it to make those around you \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Entertained|Entertained]]\]. Second, if you have only verbal communication with your opponent (such as with an audio-only call), you can trick the listener into believing you are someone else (by doing an Impression of that person's voice). Third, by combining this trick with [[Skills_and_Tricks/Deception/Disguise|Disguise]], you can fool people up close into thinking you are the person you pretend to be. When you use Impression to deceive others, \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suspicous|Suspicious]]\] opponents roll Threat vs. Hard (20) to see through it.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                          |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | You get the desired effect, and you gain an \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Ally\|Ally]]\]                                                                                          |
| Success      | You get the desired effect                                                                                                                                                           |
| Fade         | You get the desired effect, but they become \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suspicous\|Suspicious]]\] or you gain a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Bad_Memory\|Bad Memory]]\]         |
| Glitch       | You do not get the desired effect, and they become \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suspicous\|Suspicious]]\] or you gain a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Bad_Memory\|Bad Memory]]\]  |
