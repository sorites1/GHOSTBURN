# Misdirect \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                 |
| --------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Wits                  | Deception | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\|One Hand]]\]. \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\] |

*Whether by shouting and pointing or simply by talking and using one hand, you momentarily distract your opponent.*

To use Misdirect on an opponent, they must be \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. If the target has an \[Attitude\] of Hostile towards you, raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]         |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                                                                     |
| Fade         | They are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] and they become \[Suspicious\] |
| Glitch       | They become \[Suspicious\], and they gain \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident\|Confident]]\] |
