# Dead Eye \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                     |
| --------------------- | ---------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Gun\|Gun]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\|One Hand]]\] \[Ranged\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You zero in on your opponent and raise your gun. One heartbeat later, you send hot lead their way.* 

To use Dead Eye on an opponent, they must be \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. If you are wielding a pistol, lower the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | The next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is is an automatic \[Cool Success\]                                                                             |
| Success      | The next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is \[Upgraded\]                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | Your next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded\|Upgraded]]\] and you are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] |
| Glitch       | Your next \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Gun\|Gun]]\] \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] you make on anyone this turn is \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded\|Downgraded]]\]    |
