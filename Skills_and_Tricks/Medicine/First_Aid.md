# First Aid \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Stabilization DL      | Medicine | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close\|Close]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies\|Supplies]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*When a friend falls in combat or suffers from another treatable condition, you rush to their side and use your medical knowledge to stabilize them.*

To use First Aid on a target, they must be \[Bleeding\] or \[Dying\]. If you use First Aid without \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies|Supplies]]\] raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stabilized\] and heal HP equal to their Resilience score + 2 + your Medicine skill bonus                                                                                                                                                        |
| Success      | They are \[Stabilized\] and gain HP equal to their Resilience score + 2                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Fade         | They are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stabilized\|Stabilized]]\], gain HP equal to their Resilience score +2, and you have \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stress\|Stress]]\] vs. \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Stabilization_DL\|Stabilization DL]]\] |
| Glitch       | They lose 2 HP and gain \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Bleeding\|Bleeding]]\]                                                                                                                                                                            |
