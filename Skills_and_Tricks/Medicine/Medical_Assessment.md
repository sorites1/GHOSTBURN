# Medical Assessment \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                    |
| --------------------- | -------- | --------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Medicine | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You provide your medical opinion of a patient, a corpse, or a set of medical records.*

When you use Medical Assessment, ask the GM a medical question related to the subject, such as *What was the approximate time of death?* or *Do I know what this disease is or how to cure it?* Taking extra time to perform a thorough Medical Assessment can lower the DL by one (GM's discretion. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                        |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | The GM answers the question and you gain a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Secret\|Secret]]\] |
| Success      | The GM answers the question                                                        |
| Fade         | The GM answers the question and you are \[Confused]                                |
| Glitch       | You don't know and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confused\|Confused]]\]         |
