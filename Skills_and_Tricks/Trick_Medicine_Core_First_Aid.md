# First Aid \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                               |
| --------------------- | -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Stabilization DL      | Medicine | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Supplies\|Supplies]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*When a friend falls in combat or suffers from another treatable condition, you rush to their side and use your medical knowledge to stabilize them.*

If you use First Aid without \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Supplies|Supplies]]\] raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stabilized\] and heal 2 hp + your Medicine bonus                                                                                                                           |
| Success      | They are \[Stabilized\] and gain 2 hp                                                                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | They are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Stabilized\|Stabilized]]\] and you have \[[[Conditions/Condition_Stress\|Stress]]\] vs. \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Stabilization_DL\|Stabilization DL]]\] |
| Glitch       | They take 2 damage and gain \[[[Conditions/Condition_Bleeding\|Bleeding]]\]                                                                                                           |
