## Finishing Move \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                              |

*You sense your opponent's weakness and use your weapon to finish them.* 

When an opponent who is \[[[Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] goes from \[Fine\] to \[Hurting\], you can react. You \[[[Keyword_Move|Move]]\] to the opponent, if they are not already \[Close\], and roll Finishing Move. 

| Outcome      | Description                      |
| ------------ | -------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You miss and are \[[[Condition_Off_Balance\|Off Balance]]\] |
| Fade         | You deal \[[[Keyword_Minimum_Damage\|Minimum Damage]]\]      |
| Success      | You deal \[[[Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\]       |
| Cool Success | They are dead                    |
