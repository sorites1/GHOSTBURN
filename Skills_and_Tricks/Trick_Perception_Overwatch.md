# Overwatch \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Auditory\|Auditory]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], [[Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]], \[Verbal\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You survey the combat zone and relay relevant information to your crew, noting the positions and movements of friends, enemies, and civilians.* 

When you use Overwatch, pick one ally in the same battle as you. The ally must have hands-free wireless comms (headset or cyberware) to benefit from this trick. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\] and they gain 1 \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Temporary\|Temporary]]\] \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\|Reaction]]\]                   |
| Success      | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\]                                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | Their next \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] is \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded\|Upgraded]]\] and you are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] |
| Glitch       | They get no benefit and you are \[[[Conditions/Condition_Confused\|Confused]]\]                                                                                   |
