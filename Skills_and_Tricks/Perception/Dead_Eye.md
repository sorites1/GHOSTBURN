# Dead Eye \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Firearm\|Firearm]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\|One Hand]]\] \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You zero in on your opponent and raise your gun. One heartbeat later, you send hot lead their way.* 

To use Dead Eye, you must be wielding a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Firearm|Firearm]]\], and you must be able to see your target. If you are wielding a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Light_Pistol|Light Pistol]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Heavy_Pistol|Heavy Pistol]]\] and your target is at \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range|Optimal Range]]\], lower the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | If the next action you take this turn is to \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] the target with your \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Firearm\|Firearm]]\], the outcome is an automatic \[Cool Success\] |
| Success      | If the next action you take this turn is to \[Attack\] the target with your \[Firearm\], the outcome is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded\|Upgraded]]\]                                                      |
| Fade         | If the next action you take this turn is to \[Attack\] the target with your \[Firearm\], the outcome is \[Upgraded\]; regardless, you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\]            |
| Glitch       | You gain no benefit, and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\]                                                                                                                     |
