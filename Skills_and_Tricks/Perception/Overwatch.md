# Overwatch \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| --------------------- | ---------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Auditory\|Auditory]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You survey the combat zone and relay relevant information to your crew, noting the positions and movements of friends, enemies, and civilians.* 

When you use Overwatch, pick one ally in the same battle as you. The ally must be able to hear you to benefit from this trick. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                               |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\] and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                                                                                 |
| Success      | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\]                                                                                                                                                     |
| Fade         | Their next \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded\|Upgraded]]\] and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] |
| Glitch       | They get no benefit and you are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confused\|Confused]]\]                                                                                                   |
