*You use your drone's manipulator arm to open doors, use a keypad, or pick up an object, just like you would use your own.*

To use Manipulate Object, your \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Drone|Drone]]\] must be outfitted with a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Manipulator_Arm|Manipulator Arm]]\]. When you use Manipulate Object, state the action you want to perform, such as *I want use my drone to type the security code into the keypad*, or *I want my drone to pick up the pistol and put it in its cargo bay*.
