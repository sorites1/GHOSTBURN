# Spring-Loaded Pistol

The \[Spring-Loaded Pistol\] augment is a hidden compartment implanted in the forearm. With a gesture, the hatch on your inner forearm pops open, and a retractable arm pushes a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Light_Pistol|Light Pistol]]\] into your open hand. You can do this as a free action (0 AP) on your turn. Stowing the weapon costs 1 AP. Any \[Light Pistol\] on the \[[[Firearms/index|Firearms]]\] list can be mounted as a \[Spring-Loaded Pistol\] with a conversion kit (cheap and widely available).
