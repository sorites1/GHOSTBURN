# Comms \[Augment\]

*Implanted behind the ear at the base of the skull, the Comms tech array is powerful and ubiquitous. The single, most-installed augment worldwide, Comms is used for Layer 1 access to the Net. Voice and video calls, maps, music, entertainment, live broadcasts, and weather are easily accessed with Comms.*

Use Comms to wirelessly access the Net. The quality and quantity of content available to you is based on your Lifestyle. You can also use Comms to make and receive calls and messages. When speaking through \[Comms\], you may do so silently by forming the words with your tongue in your mouth (while keeping your lips closed).

Comms includes \[Security\] to protect you from hackers. The \[Security\] of Comms is based on its level. Comms (Level 1): Easy (8), Comms (Level 2): Moderate (14), Comms (Level 3): Hard (20), Comms (Level 4): Severe (26).
