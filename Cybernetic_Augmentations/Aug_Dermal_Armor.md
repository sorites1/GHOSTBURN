# Dermal Armor

\[Dermal Armor\] is metal plating inserted under your skin, typically over the rib cage and abdomen. This augmentation is obvious to anyone who observes you shirtless. \[Dermal Armor\] counts as Hard Armor. \[Dermal Armor\] and \[[[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Subdermal_Nanoweave|Subdermal Nanoweave]]\] are \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Incompatible|Incompatible]]\].

| Points | Augmentation | Mesh Cost | Reduces Psi | Description                   |
| :----: | ------------ | :-------: | ----------- | ----------------------------- |
|   3    | Dermal Armor |     3     | No          | Obvious, Counts as Hard Armor |
