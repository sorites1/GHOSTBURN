# Cybernetic Larynx \[Augment\]

*A Cybernetic Larynx is a replacement for a natural larynx, the organ in the throat that holds your vocal cords.*

|   Cost   | Augment                     | Mesh Cost | Mod Slots | Keywords |
| :------: | --------------------------- | :-------: | :-------: | :------- |
| 7,000 ⌆  | Cybernetic Larynx (Base)    |     1     |     0     | -        |
| 12,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Larynx (Level 1) |     1     |     1     | -        |

Cybernetic Larynx (Base) is a replacement only. It performs all the functions of a natural larynx. It can be crafted to restore your original voice, or you can configure it to use a new voice permanently.
