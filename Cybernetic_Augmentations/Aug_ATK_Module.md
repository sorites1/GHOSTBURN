# ATK Module

The \[ATK Module\] is an augment that works in conjunction with \[[[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_CAT_Reflexes|CAT Reflexes]]\] and \[[[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Gunwires|Gunwires]]\]. Both of these augments (at any level) must already be installed before the \[ATK Module\] can be implanted in the brain. ATK stands for Auto-Target-Kill. The \[ATK Module\] works by rapidly acquiring targets and queues up attacks against them so that when you \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Attack|Attack]]\] with a firearm that has a \[[[Weapon_Mods/Mods_Gunwire_Grip|Gunwire Grip]]\], it is much easier to hit your target.
