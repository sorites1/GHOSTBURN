TGR Claws are \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Incompatible|Incompatible]]\] with [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Flex_Blade|Flex Blade]] and [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_FNG_Razors|FNG Razors]] in the same arm.
