# Cybernetic Arm \[Augment\]

*A Cybernetic Arm is a full replacement of one of your existing arms and includes a Cybernetic Hand.*

|   Cost   | Augment                  | Mesh Cost | Mod Slots | Keywords |
| :------: | ------------------------ | :-------: | :-------: | -------- |
| 20,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Arm (Base)    |     5     |     0     | -        |
| 40,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Arm (Level 1) |     5     |     1     | -        |
| 60,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Arm (Level 2) |     5     |     2     | -        |
| 80,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Arm (Level 3) |     5     |     3     | -        |

Cybernetic Arm (Base) is a replacement only. It performs all the functions of a natural arm and hand. 

If you have two Cybernetic Arms, they may be of different levels and each has its own Mod Slots. 

## Augment Mods

The following augments have the \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Mod|Mod]]\] keyword and can be installed in a Cybernetic Arm with a free Mod Slot. Each \[Mod\] augment takes one Mod Slot. Mods: [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_FNGR_Razors|FNGR Razors]], [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Gunwires|Gunwires]], [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Multitool|Multitool]], [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_SLAP_Gun|SLAP Gun]], [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Spider_Fingers|Spider Fingers]], [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_TGR_Claws|TGR Claws]], and [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_VPR_Blade|VPR Blade]]

## Strength Mod

You can also install a Strength Mod in a free Mod Slot in a Cybernetic Arm to permanently raise your Strength, as shown below. You may install this \[Mod\] multiple times, and its effects are cumulative. Each time you install a Strength Mod, it uses one Mod Slot. 

|   Cost    | Mod               | Keywords                                                                               |
| :-------: | ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 100,000 ⌆ | +1 Strength (Aug) | \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Increase\|Aug Increase]]\], \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Mod\|Mod]]\] |

Recalculate your [[Health_and_Healing/HaH_Hit_Points_and_Conditions|Hit Points and Health Conditions]] using your increased Strength score and make the necessary adjustments to your character sheet. You should also update your Athletics, Brawl, and Melee skills to use your new Strength score.
