# Pheromonic Synthesizer

The \[Pheromonic Synthesizer\] is a small, biomechanical device that can be implanted amongst existing organs and connected to secretion zones at the epidermal layer of the body. The augment synthesizes modulator pheromones designed to positively affect the mood and emotion of anyone who is \[Close\] to you. \[Pheromonic Synthesizer\] comes in two levels: \[Pheromonic Synthesizer 1\] and \[Pheromonic Synthesizer 2\]. These levels represent an upgrade path; they do not stack.
