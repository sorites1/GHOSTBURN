# Flex Blade

The \[Flex Blade\] augment is implanted in your forearm. When activated, a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Long_Blade|Long Blade]]\] extends from a slot in your arm and acts as a \[[[Melee_Weapons/Melee_Katana|Katana]]\]. You can extend and retract the \[Arm Blade\] for free (0 AP).

| Points | Augmentation | Mesh Cost | Reduces Psi | Description      |
| :----: | ------------ | :-------: | ----------- | ---------------- |
|   7    | Arm Blade    |     7     | No          | Acts as a Katana |
