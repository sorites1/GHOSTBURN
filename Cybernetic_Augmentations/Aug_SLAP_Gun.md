# SLAP Gun \[Augment\]

*The SLAP (Spring-Loaded Auto-Pistol) Gun by Ranco is a hidden firearm compartment. With a gesture, a hatch in your forearm pops open, and a Ripper AP9 light pistol springs out and pushes into your open hand, ready to fire.*

Activating SLAP Gun is free (0 AP). Activation causes you to equip the \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Light_Pistol|Light Pistol]]\] hidden in your arm, and you may immediately use [[Skills_and_Tricks/Trick_Firearms_Core_Single_Shot|Single Shot]] or [[Skills_and_Tricks/Trick_Firearms_Core_Burst_Fire|Burst Fire]] at no additional AP cost. Stowing the weapon in its compartment costs 1 AP. 

SLAP Gun comes preloaded with a [[Firearms/Firearms_LP_Ranco_Ripper_AP9|Ranco Ripper AP9]], but any \[Light Pistol\] (which may contain its own mods) can be mounted in its compartment. It costs 1 AP to mount or unmount a \[Light Pistol\], a task which can be performed anywhere and without the need for special tools. 

SLAP Gun can be installed as a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Mod|Mod]]\] in the [[Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Cybernetic_Arm|Cybernetic Arm]] augment.
