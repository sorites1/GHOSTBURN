# About

**GHOSTBURN** was written by Justin Smith, © 2026.


> [!NOTE] GET -Author //auto-bio
> Hi, my name is Justin Smith, and I'm the person who wrote this game! I've been playing RPGs since the late '80's, starting with AD&D and M.E.R.P (Middle Earth Role-Playing), and of course, Shadowrun. I ran a lot of Shadowrun. I wrote **GHOSTBURN** because I love the cyberpunk genre (starting with William Gibson's **Neuromancer**). More than that, though, I am driven to create. I really loved writing this game, and I hope you enjoy it too.
