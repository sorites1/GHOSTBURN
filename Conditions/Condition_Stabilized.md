## Stabilized

**Cause:** When you are the target of a successful First Aid roll, or you succeed on a basic Resilience roll to avoid Dying, you gain the \[Stabilized\] condition.. 

**Effect:** You gain 1 HP and lose the following conditions: \[Bleeding\], \[Critical Condition\], \[Dying\], \[Traumatized\], and \[Unconscious\].

**Removal:** After \[Stabilized\] takes effect on you, you lose the \[Stabilized\] condition.
