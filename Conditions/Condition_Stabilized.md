# Stabilized

**Cause:** When you are the target of a successful First Aid roll, or you succeed on a basic Resilience roll to avoid Dying, you gain the \[Stabilized\] condition.. 

**Effect:** You gain 1 HP and lose the following conditions: \[[[Conditions/Condition_Bleeding|Bleeding]]\], \[[[Conditions/Condition_Critical_Condition|Critical Condition]]\], \[[[Conditions/Condition_Dying|Dying]]\], \[[[Conditions/Condition_Traumatized|Traumatized]]\], and \[[[Conditions/Condition_Unconscious|Unconscious]]\].

**Removal:** After \[Stabilized\] takes effect on you, you lose the \[Stabilized\] condition.
