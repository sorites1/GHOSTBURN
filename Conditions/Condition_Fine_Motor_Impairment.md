## Fine Motor Impairment

**Cause:** When you have difficulty doing things that use fine motor control, you have \[Fine Motor Impairment\].

**Effect:** Tricks with the \[Fine Control\] keyword have their DL raised by one.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused \[Fine Motor Impairment\] is removed, you lose the \[Fine Motor Impairment\] condition. In rare cases, this condition is permanent.
