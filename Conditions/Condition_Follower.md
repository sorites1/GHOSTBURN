## Follower

**Cause:** When you achieve a Major (14) Success or a Severe (18) Success on certain skill actions, the target becomes your \[Follower]\. If the target is a \[Group\], one representative individual fills this role.

**Effect:** As long as the target is a \[Follower\], they will follow your instructions and fulfill your requests. The target will only take actions as instructed (though they will always take steps to protect themselves and their loved ones).

**Removal:** When the target is no longer \[Compliant\] they lose \[Follower]\.
