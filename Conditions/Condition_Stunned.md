## Stunned

**Cause:** When a skill action, condition or effect causes you to have a momentary mental shutdown, you are \[Stunned\]. 

**Effect:** You are \[Immobilized\], \[Unaware\], and \[Incapable\]. 

**Removal:** At the start of your turn, make a basic Resilience roll with a DL of Easy (6). If you Succeed, you lose the \[Stunned\] condition.
