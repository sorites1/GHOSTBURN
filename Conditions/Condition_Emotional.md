## Emotional

**Cause:** A \[Social\] skill action can move you so deeply that you become \[Emotional\].

**Effect:** This condition manifests as an emotional change that matches the tone of whatever triggered it. You might become angry, melancholy, reverent, nostalgic, excited, or cheery. All \[Social\] skill actions used on you have their DL lowered by one.

**Removal:** When a new scene begins, you lose the \[Emotional\] condition.
