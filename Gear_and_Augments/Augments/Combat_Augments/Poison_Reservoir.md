# Poison Reservoir \[Augment\]

*Poison Reservoir is implanted in your lower lip, at the base of your gums. Using a bio-mechanical process, it naturally produces poison which can render an unsuspecting victim unconscious.*

Spend 1 AP to use Poison Reservoir to secrete poison onto your lips. The next person you kiss (skin contact is required) or with whom you share a drink must make a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Basic|Basic]]\] Resilience or Threat roll with a DL of Moderate (14). Failure means they gain the \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Drugged|Drugged]]\] condition.

Poison Reservoir naturally generates enough poison to be used once per scene. 

You are immune to the poison produced by your Poison Reservoir.

Poison Reservoir can be installed as a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod|Mod]]\] in the [[Cybernetic_Lips|Cybernetic Lips]] augment.
