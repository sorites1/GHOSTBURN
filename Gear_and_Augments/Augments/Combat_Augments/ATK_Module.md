# ATK Module \[Augment\]

*Auto-Target-Kill. The ATK Module by Ranco is a cranial implant that works by analyzing data from Cybernetic Eyes, Gunwires, and Glide-By-Wire Reflexes to provide computer-aided adjustments that improve combat outcomes.*

The ATK Module has the following effects: [[Gunwire_Grip|Gunwire Grip]] grants a +3 \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Gear_Bonus|Gear Bonus]]\] (instead of a +2) and [[Gunwires|Gunwires]] grant a +2 \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Bonus|Aug Bonus]]\] to Firearms (instead of a +1).
