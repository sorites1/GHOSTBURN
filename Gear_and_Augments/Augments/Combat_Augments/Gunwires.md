# Gunwires \[Augment\]

*Gunwires run from a contact pad installed in the palm of your hand, up the arm, to your brainstem. When paired with a compatible firearm, Gunwires use data from your weapon to provide aim assistance.*

Gunwires allows you to get the +2 \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Gear_Bonus|Gear Bonus]]\] to Firearms granted by a weapon's \[[[Gunwire_Grip|Gunwire Grip]]\]. 

If you have [[Cybernetic_Eye|Cybernetic Eyes]] installed, Gunwires grant a +1 \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Bonus|Aug Bonus]]\] to Firearms. This bonus is not dependent upon the weapon having a \[Gunwire Grip\].

Gunwires can be installed as a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod|Mod]]\] in the [[Cybernetic_Arm|Cybernetic Arm]] augment.
