# Cybernetic Hand \[Augment\]

*A Cybernetic Hand is a full replacement of one of your existing hands.*

|   Cost   | Augment                   | Mesh Cost | Mod Slots | Keywords |
| :------: | ------------------------- | :-------: | :-------: | -------- |
| 5,000 ⌆  | Cybernetic Hand (Base)    |     2     |     0     | -        |
| 10,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Hand (Level 1) |     2     |     1     | -        |
| 20,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Hand (Level 2) |     2     |     2     | -        |
| 30,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Hand (Level 3) |     2     |     3     | -        |

Cybernetic Hand (Base) is a replacement only. It performs all the functions of a natural hand. 

If you have two Cybernetic Hands, they may be of different levels and each has its own Mod Slots.

## Augment Mods

The following augments have the \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod|Mod]]\] keyword and can be installed in a Cybernetic Hand with a free Mod Slot. Each \[Mod\] augment takes one Mod Slot. Mods: [[FNGR_Razors|FNGR Razors]], [[Multitool|Multitool]], and [[Spider_Fingers|Spider Fingers]]
