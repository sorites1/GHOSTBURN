# Cybernetic Leg \[Augment\]

*A Cybernetic Leg is a full replacement of one of your existing legs and includes a Cybernetic Foot.*

|   Cost   | Augment                  | Mesh Cost | Mod Slots | Keywords |
| :------: | ------------------------ | :-------: | :-------: | -------- |
| 15,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Leg (Base)    |     4     |     0     | -        |
| 30,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Leg (Level 1) |     4     |     1     | -        |
| 50,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Leg (Level 2) |     4     |     2     | -        |
| 75,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Leg (Level 3) |     4     |     3     | -        |

Cybernetic Leg (Base) is a replacement only. It performs all the functions of a natural leg and foot. 

If you have two Cybernetic Legs, they may be of different levels and each has its own Mod Slots. 

## Concealed Holster Mod

You can install a Concealed Holster Mod in a free Mod Slot in a Cybernetic Leg, as shown below. You may install this \[Mod\] only once per leg.

|  Cost   | Mod               | Keywords                                                                             |
| :-----: | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 5,000 ⌆ | Concealed Holster | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concealable\|Concealable]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod\|Mod]]\] |

You may store a Light Pistol or Heavy Pistol in a Concealed Holster. 

Activating Concealed Holster is free (0 AP). Activation causes a panel in your leg to pop open and for the weapon within to be ejected so it is available to draw. It costs 1 AP to draw your weapon, as normal. It costs 1 AP to stow your weapon and return Concealed Holster to a closed position.

## Speed Mod

You can install the Speed Mod in a free Mod Slot in a Cybernetic Leg, as shown below. You may only install this \[Mod\] once. If you have one Cybernetic Leg, you must activate Speed Mod to use it. Activating Speed Mod costs 1 AP and causes you to gain \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fast|Fast]]\] for the remainder of the scene. If you have two Cybernetic Legs, then this \[Mod\] is always active and you permanently gain \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fast|Fast]]\].

|   Cost   | Mod       | Keywords                          |
| :------: | --------- | --------------------------------- |
| 30,000 ⌆ | Speed Mod | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod\|Mod]]\] |

## Hydraulic Mod

You can install the Hydraulic Mod in a free Mod Slot in a Cybernetic Leg, as shown below. When you \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move|Move]]\], you may spend an additional 1 AP to jump to a spot \[Nearby\] instead of moving normally and without the need for an [[Athletic_Move|Athletic Move]] roll.

|   Cost   | Mod           | Keywords                          |
| :------: | ------------- | --------------------------------- |
| 20,000 ⌆ | Hydraulic Mod | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod\|Mod]]\] |

## Balance Mod

You can install the Balance Mod in a free Mod Slot in a Cybernetic Leg, as shown below. You are steady on your feet. You gain \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Perfect_Balance|Perfect Balance]]\].

|   Cost   | Mod         | Keywords                          |
| :------: | ----------- | --------------------------------- |
| 10,000 ⌆ | Balance Mod | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod\|Mod]]\] |
