# Cybernetic Face \[Augment\]

*When Cybernetic Face is installed, your natural face is scraped away, and a replacement face is constructed.*

|   Cost   | Augment                   | Mesh Cost | Mod Slots | Keywords |
| :------: | ------------------------- | :-------: | :-------: | :------- |
| 5,000 ⌆  | Cybernetic Face (Base)    |     1     |     0     | -        |
| 10,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Face (Level 1) |     1     |     1     | -        |
| 40,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Face (Level 2) |     1     |     2     | -        |
| 80,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Face (Level 3) |     1     |     3     | -        |

Cybernetic Face (Base) is a replacement only. It performs all the functions of a natural face. A Cybernetic Face can replicate your original, natural face to make you look like "you" again. It can also be used to create a brand new face so you look nothing like your former self.

## Augment Mods

The following augments have the \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod|Mod]]\] keyword and can be installed in a Cybernetic Face with a free Mod Slot. Each \[Mod\] augment takes one Mod Slot. Mods: [[Faceshifter|Faceshifter]] and [[Poison_Reservoir|Poison Reservoir]]

## Presence Mod

You can install a Presence Mod in a free Mod Slot in Cybernetic Face to permanently raise your Presence, as shown below. You may install this \[Mod\] multiple times, and its effects are cumulative. Each time you install a Presence Mod, it uses one Mod Slot. 

|   Cost   | Mod               | Keywords                                                                               |
| :------: | ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 60,000 ⌆ | +1 Presence (Aug) | \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Increase\|Aug Increase]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod\|Mod]]\] |

You should update your Deception, Intimidate, and Persuade skills to use your new Presence score.
