## Backgrounds

Choose one **background** at character creation. Your background represents who your character used to be. Mechanically, it gives you a **culture** that your character is familiar with based on their past experience, as well as a favored skill, some skill action bonuses, or other benefits.

| Culture         | Knowledge about...                                                                                  |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Corporate**   | Corporations, what they provide, business processes, c-suite execs, scandals, lawsuits              |
| **Milsec**      | Military and security organizations, weapons, tactics, teamwork, security, crowd control, black ops |
| **Political**   | Political corporations and organizations, campaigning, political causes, notable figures, bribery   |
| **Street**      | Gangs (colors, territory, leadership, factions), squatting, scavenging, stealing, drugs             |
| **Underground** | Organized crime, Mafia, Yakuza, extortion, gambling, prostitution, smuggling, robbery, murder       |
