# Abilities

Abilities represent a character's body and mind. Each character is composed of the following abilities:

| Ability Name | Description                                                  |
| :----------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Presence     | Force of personality, social awareness, and artistic ability |
| Awareness    | Physical senses, insight, and abstract thinking              |
| Agility      | Quickness, nimbleness, balance, and coordination             |
| Intelligence | Education, reasoning, quick thinking, and general knowledge  |
| Strength     | Physical strength and general athleticism                    |
| Resilience   | Bodily fortitude, health, and willpower                      |

# Ability Scores

Each of your abilities has a number associated with it called an ability score. An ability score has a natural range of 1 to 5. Cybernetic augmentations and psionic mutations can increase your ability score (up to +3), for a maximum augmented ability score of 8. Each of your ability scores must be at least 1.

| Ability Score | Ability Level | Description                                         |
| :-----------: | :------------ | :-------------------------------------------------- |
|       0       | Incapable     | Completely incapable, non-functional                |
|       1       | Weak          | Underdeveloped, marginally capable                  |
|       2       | Below Average | Less capable than most, often struggles             |
|       3       | Average       | Human average, sufficient for most things in life   |
|       4       | Exceptional   | Better than most, a serious contender               |
|       5       | Peak Human    | The best of the best humankind has to offer         |
|       6       | Beyond Human  | Exceeds natural human limits                        |
|       7       | Superhuman    | Awe inspiring, breathtaking                         |
|       8       | Extraordinary | Nearing limits of the animal kingdom, apex predator |

Note: Ability scores above 8 are beyond player character capabilities and are provided to the GM to use as a basis for powerful enemies, such as constructs, and to show theoretical limits.

| Ability Score | Ability Level | Description                                          |

|      10       | Construct     | Beyond the limits of the animal kingdom; artificial  |

|      12       | Legendary     | Stories are told of such things, awe-inspiring       |
|      13       | Unfathomable  | Almost beyond comprehension, unimaginable            |
