# Chapter 2: Whiskey Head Bar

Read the following to the players: 

*You've lived in the Skags long enough that its grimy demeanor shouldn't affect you, but driving back from Panza, it's hard not to notice the stark contrast. You find the Whiskey Head without much trouble and pull into a nearby, curbside parking spot. A few motorcycles are lined up in front of the entrance to the bar.* 

*The Whiskey Head shares its outer walls with businesses on either side, just one in a long string down this city block. The bar's single front window is tinted dark black, making it opaque from the outside. A long crack runs down the center of the glass and has been patched with duct tape. A neon sign above the front door bears the name of the joint, and its colors, yellow and green, fill the puddles of rainwater that have collected on the pavement.*

## 2A: Bar Entrance

When the players enter the Whiskey Head Bar, read the following: 

*You open the door and walk in to a dimly lit room. Cigarette smoke hangs visibly in the air. The place is cramped. A long bar dominates the room with padded barstools positioned evenly along its front side. A few booths line the outer wall, and a hallway in the back appears to lead to the restroom.*

*The bartender, an older man with massive side burns, stands behind the bar, and three men are seated on the barstools, each wearing the same leather jackets as the gangers you encountered back at Apartment 416D. A middle-aged man with scraggily hair and wearing a worn-out suit sits in the back booth alone, apparently reading something on his tablet.*

## 2B: Restroom and Storage Room

In addition to the main bar area, there is a single restroom and a storage room, both accessible at the far rear. Neither room has any occupants or anything of real interest. The restroom is disgusting and amounts to a wide drain/trough and a clogged sit-down toilet (no stall or privacy shield). The storeroom contains boxes of alcohol and cheap, processed Nutty snacks.

## 2C: Backdoor

The players may want to see if they can find another way in to the Whiskey Head Bar, but there is no other way in except through the main entrance. The back of the bar abuts another business facing the opposite direction. When deliveries are made to the bar, they must be brought in through the front door.
