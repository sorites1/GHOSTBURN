## \[Speed\]

To calculate your character's \[Speed\], multiply your \[Half-Speed\] by two, as follows:

| Step | \[Speed\] Score Calculation                       |
|:----:|:--------------------------------------------------|
| 1    | \[Half-Speed\] 5 x 2 = \[Speed\] score in meters |
| 2    | 5 x 2                                             |
| 3    | 10                                                |
| 4    | \[Speed\]: 10 meters                              |
