## Classes

Choose one **class** during character creation. Your class represents the job your character has on a crew of ghosts. 

### Favored Skills

It costs less XP to increase the bonus for a Favored Skill. Mark your class's Favored Skills on your character by filling in the heart icon next to the name of the skill.

| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
