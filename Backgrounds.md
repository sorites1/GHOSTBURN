## Backgrounds

Choose one **background** at character creation. Your background represents who your character used to be. Mechanically, it gives you a **culture** that your character is familiar with based on their past experience. 

| Culture         | Knowledge about...                                                                                  |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Corporate**   | Corporations, what they provide, business processes, c-suite execs, scandals, lawsuits              |
| **Milsec**      | Military and security organizations, weapons, tactics, teamwork, security, crowd control, black ops |
| **Politics**    | Political corporations and organizations, campaigning, political causes, notable figures, bribery   |
| **Street**      | Gangs (colors, territory, leadership, factions), squatting, scavenging, stealing, drugs             |
| **Underground** | Organized crime, Mafia, Yakuza, extortion, gambling, prostitution, smuggling, robbery, murder       |

If the background you choose lists more than one culture, pick the one that best represents how your character experienced that time in their life. For example, Dock Worker offers both the Street and Underground cultures. If you picked Street, that might mean your character did their job and just tried to survive, whereas if you picked Underground, it could mean your character was part of a smuggling operation that brought in illegal merchandise through the docks.
