## Ghostburns

Choose one **ghostburn** at character creation. Your ghostburn represents the reason your character's old life got burned and helps to explain why they became a ghost. 

| Ghostburn         | Description                                                        | Ability Score Boost                   |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Awakened**      | The banality of life almost killed you, and then you woke up       | Choose one: Awareness or Psi          |
| **Betrayed**      | You were sold out or double-crossed by someone you trusted         | Choose one: Agility or Resilience     |
| **Compromised**   | Your secret identity was compromised, putting you at risk          | Choose one: Strength or Agility       |
| **Dark Secret**   | You learned a dark secret that cannot be ignored                   | Choose one: Intelligence or Presence  |
| **Escaped**       | You were held against your will, but you managed to escape         | Choose one: Agility or Psi            |
| **Fake Life**     | You discovered that your life was a carefully fabricated lie       | Choose one: Psi or Presence           |
| **Fed Up**        | You finally got fed up with the lies                               | Choose one: Presence or Strength      |
| **Framed**        | You were framed for a crime you didn't commit                      | Choose one: Strength or Resilience    |
| **Glitched**      | Your digital identity was swapped with that of a criminal          | Choose one: Agility or Intelligence   |
| **Left Behind**   | You were abandoned by people you thought you could trust           | Choose one: Strength or Awareness     |
| **Lost it All**   | You lost everything that was important to you                      | Choose one: Intelligence or Awareness |
| **Left for Dead** | You were part of a terrible accident or atrocity with no survivors | Choose one: Psi or Resilience         |
| **Made Enemies**  | You have made some powerful enemies through your actions           | Choose one: Strength or Intelligence  |
| **Manslaughter**  | You didn't know your own strength and someone died                 | Choose one: Strength or Psi           |
| **Microscope**    | You were subjected to constant monitoring and correction           | Choose one: Intelligence or Psi       |
| **Public Enemy**  | You were exposed of a wrongdoing in a very public way              | Choose one: Presence or Awareness     |
| **Ruined**        | Your professional reputation was destroyed by your enemies         | Choose one: Intelligence or Presence  |
| **Witness**       | You saw a bad thing, a murder, coverup, massive fraud, etc.        | Choose one: Awareness or Agility      |

## Hooks

Choose on **hook** for your ghostburn. The hook describes the extended outcome of your character's ghostburn and helps explain what motivated them to become a ghost.

| Hook    | Description                                                                                  |
| ------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hunted  | You are hunted by someone described in your ghostburn, such as the megacorp where you worked |
| Revenge | You lust for revenge against someone described in your ghostburn, such as those who hurt you |
| DNE     | You Do Not Exist in any system and are unable to function in normal society                  |
| Broken  | Your ghostburn left you broken and unable to fit into normal society                         |
| Legacy  | A ghost helped you through your ghostburn, and they showed you the way                       |
