# Clearview

\[Clearview\] is a quality that can be applied to goggles, helmets (with visor), video cameras, and cybernetic eyes. When \[Clearview\] is active, you ignore \[[[Conditions/Condition_Concealment_Partial|Concealment, Partial]]\] that would normally be granted to targets because it is \[[[Conditions/Condition_Raining|Raining]]\] or \[[[Conditions/Condition_Foggy|Foggy]]\].
