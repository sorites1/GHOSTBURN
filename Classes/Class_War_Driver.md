## War Driver

The allure of technology is irresistible. It factors into every facet of life in the sprawl, and you've learned that controlling it gives you an edge on the street. Eschewing autonomous vehicles used by the majority of citizens, you crave the freedom and speed that come with a steering wheel and a gear shift. With on-the-fly hacks, you flip traffic lights, override checkpoints, and pop open secure garage gates. Your skills don't stop at the pavement either. Your drones scout ahead, silently spying from above, ready to unleash sudden violence on your command.
