## Samurai

The Samurai description. 

**Favored Ability:**
* Agility

**Favored Skills:**
* Melee
* Firearms
* Stealth
* Athletics 
### Specializations
There are three Samurai specializations.
#### Street Samurai
Disciplined, deadly, follows a warrior code

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Melee (Punch / Kick)
* Melee (Slashing Strike)
* Melee (Finishing Move)
* Firearms (Single Shot)
* Firearms (Quick Shot)
* Firearms (Controlled Burst)
* Athletics (Dash)
* Athletics (Dodge)
* Athletics (Climb / Jump)
* Athletics (Swim)
#### Bushiki
An offshoot of the samurai way (the Boshido Code), the Bushiki channels psi energy (ki) to improve their physical form and their combat prowess; Special: You must have a Psi score of 1 or higher to take the Bushiki subclass.

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Melee (Punch / Kick)
* Melee (Slashing Strike)
* Melee (Bonebreaker)
* Melee (Finishing Move)
* Firearms (Single Shot)
* Firearms (Quick Shot)
* Firearms (Point Blank Shot)
* Firearms (Dual Wield Guns)
* Athletics (Dash)
* Athletics (Climb / Jump)
#### Shadow Ronin
Unpredictable, deadly, wanders without a code

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Melee (Punch / Kick)
* Melee (Slashing Strike)
* Melee (Fight Dirty)
* Melee (Finishing Move)
* Firearms (Dual Wield Guns)
* Stealth (Hide)
* Stealth (Move Silently)
* Stealth (Silent Takedown)
* Athletics (Dash)
* Athletics (Climb / Jump)
