## Merc

The Merc description. 

**Favored Ability:**
* Strength

**Favored Skills:**
* Melee
* Firearms
* Heavy Weapons
* Athletics 
### Specializations
There are two Merc specializations.
#### Metalneck
Relentless, tough, trained in modern warfare

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Melee (Punch / Kick)
* Firearms (Single Shot)
* Firearms (Controlled Burst)
* Firearms (Sustained Fire)
* Heavy Weapons (Machine Gun)
* Heavy Weapons (Flamethrower)
* Athletics (Climb / Jump)
* Athletics (Throw / Catch)
* Athletics (Swim)
#### Deadeye
Calculating, patient, precision over raw firepower

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Melee (Slashing Strike)
* Melee (Finishing Move)
* Firearms (Single Shot)
* Firearms (Called Shot)
* Heavy Weapons (Rail Gun)
* Heavy Weapons (Portable Laser)
* Athletics (Dash)
* Athletics (Climb / Jump)
* Athletics (Swim)
