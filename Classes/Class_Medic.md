## Medic

The Medic description. 

**Favored Ability:**
* Intelligence

**Favored Skills:**
* Medicine
* Perception
* Athletics
* Tech
* Insight
### Specializations
There are two Medic specializations.
#### Hacksaw
Field-ready, combat medic, thrives under pressure

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Medicine (Medical Exam)
* Medicine (First Aid)
* Perception (Notice)
* Perception (Listen)
* Perception (Alertness)
* Athletics (Dash)
* Athletics (Dodge)
* Tech (Run Diagnostics)
* Tech (Repair Device)
* Insight (Detect Lies)
* Insight (Sense Emotions)
#### Ripper
Doctor with formal training and surgical skills

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Medicine (Medical Exam)
* Medicine (Forensic Exam)
* Medicine (First Aid)
* Medicine (Extended Care)
* Medicine (Surgery)
* Medicine (Install Cyberware)
* Perception (Notice)
* Perception (Listen)
* Perception (Alertness)
* Perception (Scrutinize)
* Insight (Connect the Dots)
