## Fixer

The Fixer description. 

**Favored Ability:**
* Presence

**Favored Skills:**
* Influence
* Deception
* Perform
* Stealth
* Driving
* Culture
* Insight
### Specializations
There are three Fixer specializations.
#### Path of the Wolf
Dominant, commanding, clever, dangerous

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Influence (Command)
* Influence (Intimidate Person)
* Influence (Intimidate Group)
* Deception (Disguise)
* Deception (Forgery)
* Deception (Blend In)
* Perform (Taunt)
* Stealth (Shadow Target)
* Insight (Read the Room)
* Driving (Chase)
* Driving (Pit Maneuver)
#### Path of the Fox
Sly, manipulative, charming, deceptive

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Influence (Persuade)
* Influence (Haggle)
* Deception (Bluff)
* Deception (Misdirect)
* Deception (Street Hustle)
* Perform (Captivate)
* Perform (Adopt Persona)
* Stealth (Eavesdrop)
* Stealth (Slip Past)
* Insight (Discern Intent)
* Driving (Evade and Escape)
#### Path of the Dog
Loyal, charismatic, trustful, genuine

**Favored Skill Actions:**
* Influence (Persuade)
* Influence (Improve Attitude)
* Influence (Command)
* Perform (Entertain)
* Perform (Taunt)
* Perform (Call Attention)
* Insight (Detect Lies)
* Insight (Discern Intent)
* Insight (Sense Emotions)
* Insight (Connect the Dots)
* Driving (Precision Driving)
