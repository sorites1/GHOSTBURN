# Mute

**Cause:** When a condition, effect, or injury prevents you from speaking, you are \[Mute\]. This condition replaces \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Speech_Impairment|Speech Impairment]]\].

**Effect:** You cannot speak, though you may be able to make grunts or other sounds. You cannot use tricks with the \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal|Vocal]]\] keyword.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Mute\] is removed, you lose the \[Muted\] condition. If you gained \[Mute\] as the result of an injury, receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Recovery entry of the injury description. causes you to lose the \[Mute\] condition.
