# Unaware

**Cause:** When you gain \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Unconscious|Unconscious]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stunned|Stunned]]\], you also gain \[Unaware\]. You can also be rendered \[Unaware\] by other circumstances.

**Effect:** You are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Blinded|Blinded]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Deafened|Deafened]]\], and \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Guard|Off Guard]]\]. 

**Removal:** When you lose \[Blinded\] or \[Deafened\], you lose \[Unaware\].
