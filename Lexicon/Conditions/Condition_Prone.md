# Prone

**Cause:** When you lay down on the ground or are forced down by a condition or an attack, you gain \[Prone\].

**Effect:** Attacks made against you from a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range|Range]]\] of \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far|Far]]\] have their DL raised by one. \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Melee|Melee]]\] and \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Unarmed|Unarmed]]\]attacks made against you have their DL lowered by one. On your turn while \[Prone\], you may spend 1 AP to stand up., While you remain \[Prone\], you may only \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move|Move]]\] by using \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Crawl|Crawl]]\]. If you are swimming when forced to go \[Prone\], disregard the rest of this Effect and you begin \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Drowning|Drowning]]\].

**Removal:** When you stand up, you lose the \[Prone\] condition.
