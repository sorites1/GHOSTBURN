# Preoccupied

**Cause:** When a skill action, condition, or effect causes you to become unfocused and lost in thought, you are \[Preoccupied\].

**Effect:** Skills and tricks you use have their DL raised by one.

**Removal:** When \[Preoccupied\] was applied, a condition was given for its removal. Completing that condition causes you to lose \[Preoccupied\]. If you are still \[Preoccupied\] at the end of a session, you lose \[Preoccupied\].
