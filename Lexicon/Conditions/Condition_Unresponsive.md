# Unresponsive

**Cause:** When you gain the \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Unconscious|Unconscious]]\] condition, you also gain \[Unresponsive\]. You can also be rendered \[Unresponsive\] by certain special attacks.

**Effect:** You are \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Mute|Mute]]\] and cannot communicate in any way.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Unresponsive\] is removed, you lose the \[Unresponsive\] condition.
