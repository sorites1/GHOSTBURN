# Movement Restricted

**Cause:** When a condition or injury makes it nearly impossible to move, you are \[Movement Restricted\]. This condition replaces \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Movement_Impaired|Movement Impaired]]\].

**Effect:** It costs 3 AP to \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move|Move]]\] to an area \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] or to use a trick with the \[Move\] keyword. You cannot make Athletics rolls.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Movement Restricted\] is removed, you lose the \[Movement Restricted\] condition. If you gained \[Movement Restricted\] as the result of an injury, receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Recovery entry of the injury description. causes you to lose \[Movement Restricted\].
