# Agony

**Cause:** When a condition, effect, or injury causes you intense, unbearable pain, you are in \[Agony\].

**Effect:** All tricks you use have their DL raised by one. 

**Removal:** When the source of the \[Agony\] is removed, you lose the \[Agony\] condition. Taking painkillers also removes the effect of \[Agony\]. If you are in \[Agony\] due to an injury, receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the injury's Recovery entry, causes you to lose the \[Agony\] condition.
