# Servile

**Cause:** When you unnerve someone so much that they will do anything to please you, they are \[Servile]\. This condition replaces \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Compliant|Compliant]]\].

**Effect:** As long as the target is \[Servile\], they will follow your instructions and look for other ways to please you. 

**Removal:** To maintain the target's \[Servile\] condition, you must pay them a visit at least once a day. No roll is needed, but the visit must be in person. Otherwise, they lose the \[Servile\] condition. You may verbally release them from the \[Servile\] condition at any time.
