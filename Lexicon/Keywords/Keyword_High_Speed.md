# High Speed

A \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone|Drone]]\] traveling at 100 kph (about 60 mph) or higher. When you are moving at \[High Speed\] and are called upon to make a Drive roll, raise the DL by one. For each additional 100 kph, raise the DL by one again.
