# Weapon Jam

Some tricks result in a \[Weapon Jam\] on a Fade or Glitch. If you are using a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Firearm|Firearm]]\] and get a \[Weapon Jam\], you must spend 1 AP before you can use that weapon again. If you are controlling a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone|Drone]]\] and get a \[Weapon Jam\], you must recover the drone and then spend 1 AP to use its weapon again.
