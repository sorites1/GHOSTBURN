# Weapon Groups

A \[Weapon Group\] is a collection of \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Weapon_Types|Weapon Types]]\]. 

There are four \[Weapon Groups\]: \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Blades|Blades]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Blunt_Force|Blunt Force]]\], \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Small_Arms|Small Arms]]\], and \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Rifles|Rifles]]\]. 

Weapon Groups are used by [[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Armor|Armor]].
