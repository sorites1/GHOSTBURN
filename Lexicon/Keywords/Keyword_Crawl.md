# Crawl

You can use \[Crawl\] to \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move|Move]]\] by spending 2 AP to go somewhere \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. If you were not already \[[[Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone|Prone]]\], you automatically become \[Prone\] when you use \[Crawl\]. You cannot use \[Crawl\] to \[Move\] to a location \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far|Far]]\] from your original position.
