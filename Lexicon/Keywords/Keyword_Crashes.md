# Crashes

When a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] or \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone|Drone]]\] \[Crashes\], it collides with an immovable object or hazard and comes to a sudden stop. If the \[Vehicle\] or \[Drone\] was traveling at \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_High_Speed|High Speed]]\], it is totaled. If the driver was wearing a safety belt, they take 5 damage. Otherwise, they take 50 points of damage. At \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Moderate_Speed|Moderate Speed]]\], a \[Vehicle\] takes 30 damage and a \[Drone\] takes 15 damage. A \[Vehicle\] driver takes 3 damage with a safety belt or 30 damage without it. At \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Low_Speed|Low Speed]]\], the \[Vehicle\] or \[Drone\] takes 10 damage or 5 damage, respectively. A driver takes 1 damage with a safety belt or 10 damage without it.
