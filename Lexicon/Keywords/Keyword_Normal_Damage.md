# Normal Damage

In a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\], the middle number listed is the \[Normal Damage\] value. This number is 2 times the \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage|Minimum Damage]]] value. For example, in the \[Damage Array\], 5/10/25, the 10 is the \[Normal Damage\] value.
