# Reduced

To determine \[Reduced\] damage, use the next worse value from the weapon's \[[[Playing_the_Game/Rolling_the_Dice/Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] compared to the damage generated by the outcome of the roll. Damage cannot be \[Reduced\] below the \[Minimum Damage\] value of the \[Attack\].

In other words, \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage|Maximum Damage]]\] is \[Reduced\] to \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage|Normal Damage]]\], \[Normal Damage\] is \[Reduced\] to \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage|Minimum Damage]]\], and \[Minimum Damage\] cannot be \[Reduced\].
