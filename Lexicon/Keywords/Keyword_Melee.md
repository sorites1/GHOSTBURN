# Melee

An \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack|Attack]]\] made using a Melee Weapon, such as a knife, katana, or sledgehammer, against an opponent who is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close|Close]]\] to you. \[[Bladed]\] augments, such as [[Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/FNGR_Razors|FNGR Razors]] or [[Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/VPR_Blade|VPR Blade]] are also consider melee weapons. To make a \[Melee\] \[Attack\], use one of the tricks found under the [[Skills_and_Tricks/Melee/Melee_Skill|Melee Skill]].
