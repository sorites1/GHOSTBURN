# Move

When you use \[Move\], you spend 1 AP to go somewhere \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. If you keep going and immediately spend another 1 AP to use \[Move\] again, you somewhere that was \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far|Far]]\] from your original position.

For example, say you are in the living room. The font yard is \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. To \[Move\] from the living room to the front yard costs 1 AP. If you want to keep going, you can spend 1 AP again to \[Move\] somewhere \[Far\] away, like three blocks away.
