# Unarmed

\[Unarmed\] is a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Weapon_Types|Weapon Type]]\]. It belongs to the \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Blunt_Force|Blunt Force]]\] weapon group. The \[Unarmed\] weapon type includes the following keywords: \[Brawl\] and \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands|Two Hands]]\].
