# Handling

\[Handling\] represents how easy or difficult a \[[[Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] s to control. \[Handling\] can have one of three values: Good, Average, and Poor. When a Drive trick says to apply the \[Handling\] of a \[Vehicle\], do the following: Good \[Handling\] lowers the DL by one level; Poor \[Handling\] raises the DL by one; and Average \[Handling\] has no effect on the DL.
