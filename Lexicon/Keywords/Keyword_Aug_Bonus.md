# Aug Bonus

Cybernetic augments can grant an \[Aug Bonus\] to specific skills (up to +3). Multiple instances of an \[Aug Bonus\] on the same skill do not stack, and only the highest is applied. For example, if you have have a cybernetic augment that gives a +1 \[Aug Bonus\] to Persuade and another that gives a +2 \[Aug Bonus\] to Persuade, only the +2 \[Aug Bonus\] applies to your Persuade rolls.
