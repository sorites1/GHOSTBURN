# Gear Bonus

Your weapons and equipment can grant a \[Gear Bonus\] to specific skills (up to +3). 

Multiple instances of a \[Gear Bonus\] on the same skill do not stack, and only the highest \[Gear Bonus\] applies. For example, if you have have a piece of gear that gives a +1 \[Gear Bonus\] to Firearms and another that gives a +2 \[Gear Bonus\] to Firearms, only the +2 \[Gear Bonus\] applies to your Firearms rolls.
