## Stunned

**Cause:** When a skill action, condition or effect causes you to have a momentary mental shutdown, you are \[Stunned\]. 

**Effect:** You are \[[[ch2_Condition_Immobilized|Immobilized]]\], \[[[ch2_Condition_Unaware|Unaware]]\], and \[[[ch2_Condition_Incapable|Incapable]]\]. 

**Removal:** At the end of your next [[ch01_05_Types_of_Turns|turn]], you lose the \[Stunned\] condition.
