## Traumatized

**Cause:** When you sustain an injury by rolling on the **Injury Severity Chart**, you gain the \[Traumatized\] condition.

**Effect:** All skill actions have their DL is [[ch1_Adjusting_Difficulty_Level|raised by]] one. 

**Removal:** When you are \[[[ch2_Condition_Stabilized|Stabilized]]\], you lose the \[Traumatized\] condition. If you are still \[Traumatized\] at the end of the current scene, you lose the \[Traumatized\] condition.
