## Stabilized

**Cause:** When you are the target of a successful First Aid roll, or you succeed on a basic [[ch1_Abilities|Resilience]] roll to avoid Dying, you gain the \[Stabilized\] condition.. 

**Effect:** You lose the following conditions: \[[[ch2_Condition_Bleeding|Bleeding]]\], \[[[ch2_Condition_Critical_Condition|Critical Condition]]\], \[Dying\], \[[[ch2_Condition_Traumatized|Traumatized]]\], and \[[[ch2_Condition_Unconscious|Unconscious]]\].

**Removal:** After \[Stabilized\] takes effect on you, you lose the \[Stabilized\] condition.
