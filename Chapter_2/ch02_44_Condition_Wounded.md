## Wounded

**Cause:** When you are reduced to half your maximum hit points (or less), you gain the \[Wounded\] condition. this condition follows and replaces \[Healthy\].

**Effect:** All skill actions have their DL raised by one.

**Removal:** When you have more than half your maximum hit points, you lose the \[Wounded\] condition. You also lose the \[Wounded\] condition if you advance to \[Seriously Wounded\], \[Death's Door\], or \[Dying\].
