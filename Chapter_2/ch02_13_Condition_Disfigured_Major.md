## Disfigured, Major

**Cause:** When a condition or injury dramatically alters your appearance in an unnatural way, you gain \[Disfigured, Major\]. This condition stacks with itself and with \[Disfigured, Minor\].

**Effect:** The DL for the following skill actions is raised by **two**: Improve Attitude, Persuade, Bluff, and Blend In. The DL for the Intimidate skill action is  by lowered by **two**.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Disfigured, Major\] is removed, you lose the \[Disfigured, Major\] condition. If you gained \[Disfigured, Major\] as the result of an injury, receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Recovery entry of the injury description. causes you to lose \[Disfigured, Major\].
