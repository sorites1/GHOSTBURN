## Unconscious

**Cause:** When you gain the \[Dying\] condition, you also gain \[Unconscious\]. You can also be rendered \[Unconscious\] by certain special attacks.

**Effect:** You are \[Immobilized\], \[Incapable\], \[Prone\], \[Unaware\], and \[Unresponsive\].

**Removal:** When you gain \[Stabilized\] and have at least 1 hit point, you lose the \[Unconscious\] condition.
