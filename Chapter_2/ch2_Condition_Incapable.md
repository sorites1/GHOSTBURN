## Incapable

**Cause:** When a condition, effect, or injury renders you unable to act, you are \[Incapable\].

**Effect:** You cannot use skill actions or spend AP.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Incapable\] is removed, you lose the \[Incapable\] condition. If you gained \[Incapable\] as the result of an injury, receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Recovery entry of the injury description. causes you to lose \[Incapable\].
