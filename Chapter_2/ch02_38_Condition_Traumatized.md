## Traumatized

**Cause:** When you sustain an injury by rolling on the **Injury Severity Chart**, you gain the \[Traumatized\] condition.

**Effect:** All skill actions have their DL is raised by one. 

**Removal:** When you are \[Stabilized\], you lose the \[Traumatized\] condition. If you are still \[Traumatized\] at the end of the current scene, you lose the \[Traumatized\] condition.
