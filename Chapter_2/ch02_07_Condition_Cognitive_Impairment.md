## Cognitive Impairment

**Cause:** When a condition or effect makes it harder for you to think, you have a \[Cognitive Impairment\].

**Effect:** Skill actions roll with Intelligence have their DL raised by one.

**Removal:** When the condition or effect that caused \[Cognitive Impairment\] is removed, you lose the \[Cognitive Impairment\] condition. In rare cases, this condition is permanent.
