## Off Guard

**Cause:** When you are in a state of being unprepared for immediate danger, you are \[Off Guard\]. Most people are \[Off Guard\] by default.

**Effect:** Attacks made against a person who is \[Off Guard\] have their DL [[ch01_19_Adjusting_Difficulty_Level|lowered by]] one. 

**Removal:** When you observe immediate danger, you lose \[Off Guard\].
