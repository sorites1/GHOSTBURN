## Life Threatening

**Cause:** When you sustain an injury that requires emergency treatment, without which you could could die, you have a \[Life Threatening\] condition.

**Effect:** As determined by the injury you sustained. See **Sustaining an Injury** for specific effects.

**Removal:** Receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Recovery entry of the injury description. causes you to lose the \[Life Threatening\] condition.
