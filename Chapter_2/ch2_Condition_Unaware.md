## Unaware

**Cause:** When you gain \[[[ch2_Condition_Unconscious|Unconscious]]\] or \[[[ch2_Condition_Stunned|Stunned]]\], you also gain \[Unaware\]. You can also be rendered \[Unaware\] by certain special attacks.

**Effect:** You are \[[[ch2_Condition_Blinded|Blinded]]\], \[[[ch2_Condition_Deafened|Deafened]]\], and \[[[ch2_Condition_Off_Guard|Off Guard]]\]. 

**Removal:** When you lose \[[[ch2_Condition_Blinded|Blinded]]\] or \[[[ch2_Condition_Deafened|Deafened]]\], you lose \[Unaware\].
