## Disfigured, Minor

**Cause:** When a condition or injury alters your appearance in an unnatural way, you gain \[Disfigured, Minor\]. This condition stacks with itself and with \[[[ch2_Condition_Disfigured_Major|Disfigured, Major]]\].

**Effect:** The DL for the following skill actions is [[ch01_19_Adjusting_Difficulty_Level|raised by]] one: Improve Attitude, Persuade, Bluff, and Blend In. The DL for the Intimidate skill action s lowered by one.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Disfigured, Minor\] is removed, you lose the \[Disfigured, Minor\] condition. If you gained \[Disfigured, Minor\] as the result of an injury, receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Recovery entry of the injury description. causes you to lose \[Disfigured, Minor\].
