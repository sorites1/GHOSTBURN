A techno-mysticism has been slowly gaining roots in the lowest levels of the sprawl. The bitter, the deranged, the worker who is dead inside and on the verge of collapse, literally and figuratively. These broken individuals are "made whole" by coming together to worship and have guidance imparted to them. They form a cult, a religion, that preaches harmony and oneness. But they perform their own dark rituals in the dead of night. They too are being manipulated by the AI, using a persona that speaks to them in riddles and cyphers.
