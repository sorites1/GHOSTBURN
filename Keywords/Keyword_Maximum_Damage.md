# Maximum Damage

In a \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\], the last number listed is the \[Maximum Damage\] value. This number is 5x the \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage|Minimum Damage]]] value. For example, in the \[Damage Array\], 5/10/25, the 25 is the \[Maximum Damage\] value.
