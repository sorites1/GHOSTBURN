# Stabilization DL

Your \[Stabilization DL\] is based on your Resilience score. Resilience of 5 (or higher) means you have a \[Stabilization DL\] of Easy (8). Resilience of 4 means you have a \[Stabilization DL\] of Moderate (14). Resilience of 3 means you have a \[Stabilization DL\] of Hard (20). Resilience of 2 means you have a \[Stabilization DL\] of Severe (26).  Resilience of 1 means you have a \[Stabilization DL\] of Extreme (32).
