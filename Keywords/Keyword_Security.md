## Security

\[Security\] is a measure of how secure something is. It can be used to define the difficulty of hacking a device or system, but it can also refer to the overall defense posture of a defined area, like an office building or a region of airspace. \[Security\] is expressed as a [[Difficulty_Level|Difficulty Level]]. Retail is Easy (8), Small Corp Grade is Moderate (14), Middle Corp Grade is Hard (20), Megacorp Grade is Severe (26), and Milsec Grade is Extreme (32).
