## Unarmed Strike

Indicates an \[[[Keyword_Attack|Attack]]\] made without weapons, such as a punch or kick. See the \[[[Trick_Brawl_Core_Unarmed_Strike|Unarmed Strike]]\] trick for more information. 

The \[[[Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] for \[Unarmed Strike\] uses your Strength score for \[[[Keyword_Minimum_Damage|Minimum Damage]]\], Strength x 2 for \[[[Keyword_Normal_Damage|Normal Damage]]\], and Strength x 5 for \[[[Keyword_Maximum_Damage|Maximum Damage]]\].
