# Unarmed Strike

Indicates an \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Attack|Attack]]\] made without weapons, such as a punch or kick. See the \[[[Skills_and_Tricks/Trick_Brawl_Core_Unarmed_Strike|Unarmed Strike]]\] trick for more information. 

The \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] for \[Unarmed Strike\] uses your Strength score for \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage|Minimum Damage]]\], Strength x 2 for \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage|Normal Damage]]\], and Strength x 5 for \[[[Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage|Maximum Damage]]\].
