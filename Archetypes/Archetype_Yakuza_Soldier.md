## Yakuza Soldier

**Keywords:** \[Uncommon\], \[Underground\], \[Street\]

The Yakuza Soldier belongs to the powerful, Japanese criminal organization by the same name and serves the local *oyabun*, the crime boss. The Yakuza Soldier is an efficient enforcer skilled in firearms, as well as the *katana*. Responsible for carrying out the *oyabun''s* orders, the Yakuza Solder is decorated with tattoos commemorating past kills. The Yakuza Soldier is loyal to the *oyabun* because to disobey is to accept great dishonor and certain death. 
### Background (3 points)

If you purchase Yakuza Soldier as a Background during character creation, you get: 

 **Free Contact:** 
	* Choose one: (Gun Tech, Street Ganger, Underground Brawler) 
	* You gain the selected contact. This is person might also be in the Yakuza or could be an outsider you know. The nature of your relationship is up to you to describe.  

* **Warrior:**
	* Trained in Firearms
	* Firearms (Controlled Burst) +1
	* Trained in Melee Combat
	* Melee Combat (Slashing Strike) +1

* **Lifestyle:** You start the game with 1 month Low Lifestyle paid

* **Resources:** Add 50,000 cred to your starting money
### Contact

If you select Yakuza Soldier as a contact, they are able to assist with: 

* **Knowledge:*** Yakuza, Mafia, melee weapons, Japanese (language) 

* **Resources:*** This contact grants access to Black Market goods and services

* **Quid Pro Quo:*** The Yakuza Soldier is willing to share information that will damage the Yakuza's enemies, especially the Mafia, at no cost. However, Black Market goods and services tend to be quite expensive. See the Equipment section.
