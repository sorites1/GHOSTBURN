## Underground Brawler

**Keywords:** \[Rare\], \[Underground\]

The Underground Brawler is a bareknuckle fighter employed by one of the organized crime rackets in the city. In a world of cybernetic enhancement, people are inexplicably drawn to the spectacle of two, unaugmented boxers beating the absolute hell out of one another until only one of them is left standing. The life of the Underground Brawler is raucous, bloody, and brutal.
### Background (2 points)

If you purchase Underground Brawler as a Background during character creation, you get: 

* **Free Contact:** 
	* Choose one (Mafia Enforcer, Yakuza Soldier, or Vice Merchant)
	* You gain the selected contact. This is someone you encountered regularly in your dealings with organized crime or at your fights. More than a mere acquaintance, this person is your friend or is someone with whom you've had business dealings. 

* **Brawler:***
	* Trained in Melee Combat
	* Melee Combat (Punch / Kick) +1 bonus
	* Choose one more Melee Combat skill action and gain a +1 bonus to it

* **Lifestyle:** You start the game with 1 month Low Lifestyle paid

* **Resources:** Add 1,000 cred to your starting money
### Contact

If you select Underground Brawler as a contact, they are able to assist with: 

* **Knowledge:*** Underground fighting, gambling, organized crime, street docs

* **Resources:*** Nothing

* **Quid Pro Quo:*** Will help you for free
