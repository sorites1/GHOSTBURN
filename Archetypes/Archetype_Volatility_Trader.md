## Volatility Trader

**Keywords:** \[Uncommon\], \[Professional\]

Employed by one of the big, financial firms, the Volatility Trader makes a living by buying and selling digital assets on the Net, choosing trades specifically designed to capitalize on market volatility. The idea of money and wealth has all but lost meaning to these virtual robber barrens who seem to have unlocked an "infinite money glitch" in real life.
### Background (5 points)

If you purchase Volatility Trader as a Background during character creation, you get: 

* **Keen Mind:**  
	* Trained in Insight
	* Choose one Insight skill action and gain a +1 bonus to it
	* Chose one skill (other than Insight); you are Trained in the chosen skill

* **Lifestyle:** You start the game with 3 months Luxury Lifestyle paid

* **Resources:** Add 250,000 cred to your starting money
### Contact

If you select Volatility Trader as a contact, they are able to assist with: 

* **Knowledge:*** Corporations, the financial industry, markets

* **Resources:*** keys to a second apartment, spare vehicle, impeccable clothes, and insider information; willing to loan up to 25,000 cred with zero advance notice

* **Quid Pro Quo:*** Information about the job you're working right now or one you have recently completed, specifically related to corporate interests or commodities, like rare earths, metals (gold, copper, etc.), or gasses, such as those mined in Spinebreak
