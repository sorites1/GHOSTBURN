## Bartender

**Keywords:** \[Common\], \[Street\]

The Bartender works in a tavern or bar where they serve drinks and listen to people's stories. Ostensibly a straightforward job, the Bartender has learned how to be available without being obtrusive, and how to deal with someone who's had too much to drink. Understanding the bar's clientele is crucial to providing good service, and the Bartender tends to display a surprising level of cultural awareness. 
### Background (1 point)

If you purchase Bartender as a Background during character creation, you get: 

* **Free Contact:** Choose one \[Common\] or \[Street\] contact. This is someone who was a patron of the bar where you worked. You decide what kind of a relationship you developed with this person, whether a friendship or something else. 

* **Lifestyle:** You start the game with 1 month Low Lifestyle paid

* **Resources:** Add 3,000 cred to your starting money
### Contact

If you purchase Underground Brawler as a contact, they are able to assist with: 

* **Knowledge:*** Underground fighting, gambling, organized crime, street docs

* **Resources:*** Nothing

* **Quid Pro Quo:*** Will help you for free
