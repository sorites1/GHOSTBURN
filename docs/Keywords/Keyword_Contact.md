## Contact

A \[Contact\] is a person you know and who has a Friendly \[[[Keyword_Attitude|Attitude]]\] towards you. A \[Contact\] is someone who will help you (not necessarily for free) when you call upon them. A \[Contact\] is a permanent part of your character and they remain available to you as a resource from one mission to the next. You can call upon any given \[Contact\] no more than once per session.
