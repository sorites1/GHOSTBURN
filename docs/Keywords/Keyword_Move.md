# Move

When you use \[Move\], you spend 1 AP to go somewhere [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. If you keep going and immediately spend another 1 AP to use \[Move\] again, you somewhere that was [[Far](../Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] from your original position.

For example, say you are in the living room. The font yard is [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. To \[Move\] from the living room to the front yard costs 1 AP. If you want to keep going, you can spend 1 AP again to \[Move\] somewhere \[Far\] away, like three blocks away.
