# Character Creation

In this game, each player creates a character to play. As the player, you make all the decisions about your character, including their background, natural abilities, skills, and so on. 

To create a character in ***GHOSTBURN***, follow the steps below.

| Step                                                                   | Seven Questions                                      |
| :--------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| [1. Choose a Background](/../Characters/Char_CC1_Background)             | What was your old life?                              |
| [2. Choose a Burn and Hook](/../Characters/Char_CC2_Burn_and_Hook)       | How did you get burned, and what drives you forward? |
| [3. Choose a Contact](/../Characters/Char_CC3_Contacts)                  | Who do you still know from your old life?            |
| [4. Buy Ability Scores](/../Characters/Char_CC4_Ability_Scores)          | What are your natural abilities?                     |
| [5. Distribute Skill Bonuses](/../Characters/Char_CC5_Additional_Skills) | What are your skills?                                |
| [6. Starting Package](/../Characters/Char_CC6_Starting_Package)          | What gear and augments do you have?                  |
| [7. Finishing Touches](/../Characters/Char_CC7_Finishing_Touches)        | What is your name?                                   |
