# 5. Additional Skill Bonuses

Choose three skills, and write +1 in the skill bonus box for each. You cannot choose your favored skill, and no skill can have a skill bonus higher than +1 at the start of the game.

The chart below includes a list of skills, along with each skill's associated ability and a basic description. For details about a skill and the tricks it includes, refer to [Skills and Tricks](../Skills_and_Tricks.md).

| Skill      | Ability      | Description                              |
| ---------- | ------------ | ---------------------------------------- |
| Athletics  | Strength     | You know how to climb, jump, and run     |
| Brawl      | Strength     | You know how to fight with your fists    |
| Deception  | Presence     | You know how to lie and manipulate       |
| Drive      | Agility      | You know how to drive vehicles           |
| Firearms   | Agility      | You know how to use firearms             |
| Hacking    | Intelligence | You know how to hack systems and devices |
| Insight    | Awareness    | You know how to read people              |
| Intimidate | Presence     | You know how to unnerve people           |
| Medicine   | Intelligence | You know how to treat wounds             |
| Melee      | Strength     | You know how to use blades and clubs     |
| Perception | Awareness    | You how to be aware of your surroundings |
| Piloting   | Awareness    | You know how to pilot drones             |
| Persuade   | Presence     | You know how to persuade others          |
| Stealth    | Agility      | You know how to be sneaky                |
