In this game, each player creates a character to play. As the player, you make all the decisions about your character, including their background, natural abilities, skills, and so on. 

To create a character in ***GHOSTBURN***, follow the steps below.

| Step                                                            | Seven Questions                                      |

| [3. Contacts](../../Characters/Character_Creation/Contacts.md)                   | Who do you still know from your old life?            |
| [4. Ability Scores](../../Characters/Character_Creation/Ability_Scores.md)       | What are your natural abilities?                     |
| [5. Additional Skills](../../Characters/Character_Creation/Additional_Skills.md) | What are your skills?                                |

| [7. Finishing Touches](../../Characters/Character_Creation/Finishing_Touches.md) | What is your name?                                   |
