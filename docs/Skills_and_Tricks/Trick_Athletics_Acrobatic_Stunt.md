# Acrobatic Stunt \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                           |
| --------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Athletics | [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You utilize your incredible balance and speed to execute a death defying climb, jump, or dive.* 

Use Acrobatic Stunt to [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] beyond a significant physical obstacle or to gain access to a hard-to-reach area. When you use this trick, state the desired outcome of your \[Move\], such as *I want to move across the wire like a tightrope walker or I want to scale the sheer surface of this building.*

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] as desired and you are [[Inspired](../Conditions/Condition_Inspired\.md)]                              |
| Success      | You \[Move\] as desired                                                                                          |
| Fade         | You \[Move\] as desired but you are \[Off Balance\]                                                              |
| Glitch       | You do not [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)] and you are [[Off Balance](../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\.md)] |
