## Acrobatic Stunt \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                           |
| --------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Athletics | \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You utilize your incredible balance and speed to execute a death defying climb, jump, or dive.* 

Use Acrobatic Stunt to \[[[Keyword_Move|Move]]\] beyond a significant physical obstacle or to gain access to a hard-to-reach area. When you use this trick, state the desired outcome of your \[Move\], such as *I want to move across the wire like a tightrope walker or I want to scale the sheer surface of this building.*

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                  |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You do not \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] and you are \[[[Condition_Off_Balance\|Off Balance]]\] |
| Fade         | You \[Move\] as desired but you are \[Off Balance\]                                          |
| Success      | You \[Move\] as desired                                                                      |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] as desired and you are \[[[Condition_Inspired\|Inspired]]\]                     |
