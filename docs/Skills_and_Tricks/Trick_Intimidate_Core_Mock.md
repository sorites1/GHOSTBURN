# Mock \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Intimidate | [[Concentrate](/../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate)], [[Language](/../Keywords/Keyword_Language)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Social](/../Keywords/Keyword_Social)], [[Vocal](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal)] |

*You hone in on your opponent's insecurities and ruthlessly mock them, undermining their confidence.*

To use Mock on an opponent, they must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] or [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are [[Inspired](/../Conditions/Condition_Inspired)]                                            |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\] and their [[Attitude](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude)] worsens                                          |
| Fade         | You are both [[Distracted](/../Conditions/Condition_Distracted)] and their [[Attitude](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude)] worsens |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become [[Confident](/../Conditions/Condition_Confident)]                                 |
