# Mock \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Intimidate | [[Concentrate](../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\.md)], [[Language](../Keywords/Keyword_Language\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Social](../Keywords/Keyword_Social\.md)], [[Vocal](../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\.md)] |

*You hone in on your opponent's insecurities and ruthlessly mock them, undermining their confidence.*

To use Mock on an opponent, they must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are [[Inspired](../Conditions/Condition_Inspired\.md)]                                            |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\] and their [[Attitude](../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\.md)] worsens                                          |
| Fade         | You are both [[Distracted](../Conditions/Condition_Distracted\.md)] and their [[Attitude](../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\.md)] worsens |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become [[Confident](../Conditions/Condition_Confident\.md)]                                 |
