# Burst Fire \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | [[Gun](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Gun.md)], [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You grip your weapon and issue a three-round burst.* 

When you use Burst Fire, your target must be at the [[Optimal Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range.md)] for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. You may use [[Spray](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Spray.md)] to hit an additional target. If you use [[One Hand](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Burst Fire [[Damage Array](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array.md)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage.md)] and they are \[Suppressed\]                                                      |
| Success      | Choose to either deal [[Normal Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage.md)] or make them [[Suppressed](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suppressed.md)]      |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage.md)] and they gain a  [[Combat Opportunity](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Combat_Opportunity.md)] |
| Glitch       | Out of ammo. Spend 1 AP to use this weapon again.                                                                                                 |
