# Crippling Shot \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[Gun](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Gun.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You take careful aim and target one of your opponent's legs with your shot.* 

When you use Crippling Shot, if the target is at a \[Range\] other than the weapon's [[Optimal Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range.md)], raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Single Shot [[Damage Array](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array.md)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage.md)] and they gain [[Movement Impaired](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Movement_Impaired.md)] and [[Prone](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone.md)] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage.md)] and they gain [[Movement Restricted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Movement_Restricted.md)]                                             |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage.md)] and they gain a  [[Combat Opportunity](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Combat_Opportunity.md)]                                              |
| Glitch       | You miss the shot and they gain a \[Combat Opportunity\]                                                                                                                                       |
