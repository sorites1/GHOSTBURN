# Entertain \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                             |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Hard (20)             | Persuade | [[Attitude](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude)], [[Language](/../Keywords/Keyword_Language)], [[Social](/../Keywords/Keyword_Social)], [[Vocal](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal)] |

*With a well-timed joke, an unexpected song, or a captivating story, you entertain those around you..* 

When you use Entertain, you can focus on one person or perform for a larger audience. Describe what you are doing to capture their attention and then roll Entertain. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Entertained\], \[Emotional\], and your [[Ally](/../Conditions/Condition_Ally)]                                             |
| Success      | They are \[Entertained\] and [[Emotional](/../Conditions/Condition_Emotional)]                                                        |
| Fade         | They are [[Entertained](/../Conditions/Condition_Entertained)] and you have [[Stress](/../Conditions/Condition_Stress)] vs. Hard (20) |
| Glitch       | They are not entertained and their \[Attitude\] worsens                                                                               |
