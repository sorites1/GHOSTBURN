# Sideswipe \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                    |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Drive | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You pull up alongside another driver and then violently swerve into them, damaging both of your vehicles.* 

To use Sideswipe, your [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle.md)] must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to the other \[Vehicle\]. The [[Handling](../Keywords/Keyword_Handling.md)] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Sideswipe roll. If you use only [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                            |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Their \[Vehicle\] takes 20 damage and the other driver is \[Shaken\]                                                                   |
| Success      | Their \[Vehicle\] takes 15 damage and yours takes 5 damage                                                                             |
| Fade         | Both your and their \[Vehicle\] take 10 damage                                                                                         |
| Glitch       | You miss them, your [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\.md)] takes 10 damage, and you are [[Shaken](../Conditions/Condition_Shaken\.md)] |
