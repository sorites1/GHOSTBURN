## Crippling Shot \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | \[[[Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\], \[[[Keyword_Gun\|Gun]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You take careful aim and target one of your opponent's legs with your shot.* 

When you use Crippling Shot, your target must be at the same range as the optimal range listed for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Single Shot \[[[Keyword_Damage_Array|Damage Array]]\] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                   |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again.      |
| Fade         | You deal \[[[Keyword_Minimum_Damage\|Minimum Damage]]\]                                                   |
| Success      | You deal \[[[Keyword_Normal_Damage\|Normal Damage]]\] and they gain \[[[Condition_Movement_Restricted\|Movement Restricted]]\]              |
| Cool Success | You deal \[[[Keyword_Maximum_Damage\|Maximum Damage]]\] and they gain \[[[Condition_Movement_Impaired\|Movement Impaired]]\] and \[[[Condition_Prone\|Prone]]\] |
