# Dazzling Strike \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Opposed.md)]           | Melee | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], \[Melee], [[One Hand](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You strike at your opponent's eyes, temporarily obscuring their vision.* 

To use Dazzling Strike, your opponent must be [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal \[Minimum Damage\] to them and they are [[Blinded](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Blinded.md)]              |
| Success      | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage.md)] to them and they are \[Visually Impaired\] |
| Fade         | They are \[Visually Impaired\]                                                                              |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance.md)]                                      |
