# Leaping Strike \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| --------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Melee | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[Melee](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Melee.md)], [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)], [[One Hand](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You get a running start, fall from above, or swing in on a rope to perform a mid-air strike.* 

To use Leaping Strike, first make a basic Athletics roll with a DL of Moderate (14). If you fail, you fall [[Prone](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone.md)] and your action ends. Otherwise, you automatically [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] through the air to a [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] opponent by jumping, swinging, or falling, for example, and roll Leaping Strike. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                              |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage.md)] to them and they fall \[Prone\] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage.md)] to them                           |
| Fade         | You deal \[Normal Damage\] to them and then fall \[Prone\]                                               |
| Glitch       | You miss and fall [[Prone](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone.md)]                                      |
