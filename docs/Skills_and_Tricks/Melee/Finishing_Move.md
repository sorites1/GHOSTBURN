# Finishing Move \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                              |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Opposed.md)]           | Melee | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)], [[Melee](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Melee.md)], [[One Hand](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You sense your opponent's weakness and use your weapon to finish them.* 

When an opponent who is [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] goes from \[Fine\] to \[Hurting\], you can react. You [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] to the opponent, if they are not already \[Close\], and roll Finishing Move. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                            |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are dead                                                          |
| Success      | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage.md)]       |
| Fade         | You deal [[Normal Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage.md)]         |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance.md)] |
