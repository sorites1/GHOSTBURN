# Burst Fire \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | [[Gun](../Keywords/Keyword_Gun\.md)], [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You grip your weapon and issue a three-round burst.* 

When you use Burst Fire, your target must be at the [[Optimal Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range.md)] for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. You may use [[Spray](../Keywords/Keyword_Spray.md)] to hit an additional target. If you use [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Burst Fire [[Damage Array](../Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array.md)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\.md)] and they are \[Suppressed\]                                                      |
| Success      | Choose to either deal [[Normal Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\.md)] or make them [[Suppressed](../Conditions/Condition_Suppressed\.md)]      |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage\.md)] and they gain a  [[Combat Opportunity](../Keywords/Keyword_Combat_Opportunity\.md)] |
| Glitch       | Out of ammo. Spend 1 AP to use this weapon again.                                                                                                 |
