# Burst Fire \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Firearms | [[Gun](/../Keywords/Keyword_Gun)], [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You grip your weapon and issue a three-round burst.* 

When you use Burst Fire, your target must be at the [[Optimal Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range)] for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. You may use [[Spray](/../Keywords/Keyword_Spray)] to hit an additional target. If you use [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], raise the DL by one. Use your weapon's Burst Fire [[Damage Array](/../Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage)] and they are \[Suppressed\]                                                      |
| Success      | Choose to either deal [[Normal Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage)] or make them [[Suppressed](/../Conditions/Condition_Suppressed)]      |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage)] and they gain a  [[Combat Opportunity](/../Keywords/Keyword_Combat_Opportunity)] |
| Glitch       | Out of ammo. Spend 1 AP to use this weapon again.                                                                                                 |
