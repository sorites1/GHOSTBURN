## Chase \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You spot the car you're chasing up ahead and rapidly accelerate to catch up.* 

When you use Chase, you identify a target \[[[Keyword_Vehicle|Vehicle]]\] ahead of you (\[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Far|Far]]\]). If the other \[Vehicle\] is traveling at \[[[Keyword_High_Speed|High Speed]]\], you must do the same (which raises the DL by one). If the other \[Vehicle\] is \[Far\] away, raise the DL by one. The \[[[Keyword_Handling|Handling]]\] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Chase roll. If you use only \[[[Keyword_One_Hand|One Hand]]\], raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                             |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You fall behind and are \[[[Condition_Distracted\|Distracted]]\] and the other \[[[Keyword_Vehicle\|Vehicle]]\] may use Escape         |
| Fade         | You \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] \[[[Keyword_Close\|Close]]\] to the other \[Vehicle\] and you are \[Distracted\]              |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Close\] to the other \[Vehicle\]                                         |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] \[Close\] to the other \[Vehicle\] and may overtake (get ahead) if desired |
