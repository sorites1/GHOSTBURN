# Chase \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Vehicle](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You spot the car you're chasing up ahead and rapidly accelerate to catch up.* 

When you use Chase, you identify a target [[Vehicle](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle)] ahead of you ([[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)] or [[Far](/../Keywords/Keyword_Far)]). If the other \[Vehicle\] is traveling at [[High Speed](/../Keywords/Keyword_High_Speed)], you must do the same (which raises the DL by one). If the other \[Vehicle\] is \[Far\] away, raise the DL by one. The [[Handling](/../Keywords/Keyword_Handling)] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Chase roll. If you use only [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] \[Close\] to the other \[Vehicle\] and may overtake (get ahead) if desired                                                            |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Close\] to the other \[Vehicle\]                                                                                                    |
| Fade         | You [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)] [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] to the other \[Vehicle\] and you are \[Distracted\]                  |
| Glitch       | You fall behind and are [[Distracted](/../Conditions/Condition_Distracted)] and the other [[Vehicle](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle)] may use Escape |
