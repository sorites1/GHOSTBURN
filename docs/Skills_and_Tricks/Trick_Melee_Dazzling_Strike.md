# Dazzling Strike \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](/../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed)]           | Melee | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], \[Melee], [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You strike at your opponent's eyes, temporarily obscuring their vision.* 

To use Dazzling Strike, your opponent must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal \[Minimum Damage\] to them and they are [[Blinded](/../Conditions/Condition_Blinded)]              |
| Success      | You deal [[Minimum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage)] to them and they are \[Visually Impaired\] |
| Fade         | They are \[Visually Impaired\]                                                                              |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](/../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance)]                                      |
