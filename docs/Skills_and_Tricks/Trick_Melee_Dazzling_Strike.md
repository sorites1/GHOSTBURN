# Dazzling Strike \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed\.md)]           | Melee | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], \[Melee], [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You strike at your opponent's eyes, temporarily obscuring their vision.* 

To use Dazzling Strike, your opponent must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal \[Minimum Damage\] to them and they are [[Blinded](../Conditions/Condition_Blinded\.md)]              |
| Success      | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage\.md)] to them and they are \[Visually Impaired\] |
| Fade         | They are \[Visually Impaired\]                                                                              |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\.md)]                                      |
