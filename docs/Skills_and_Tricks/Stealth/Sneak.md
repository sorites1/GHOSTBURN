# Sneak \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                                                                                                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*By moving slowly and quietly and by staying behind cover and out of sight, you sneak to your destination.* 

To use Sneak, you must be [[Undetected](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Undetected.md)] by the people you're avoiding. If multiple observers are present, use the highest Wits amongst them.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and are [[Hidden](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Hidden.md)]                                                                                                                                   |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and remain \[Undetected\]                                                                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | You [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] somewhere [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)], remain \[Undetected], and have [[Stress](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stress.md)] vs. [[Wits](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Wits.md)] |
| Glitch       | You lose [[Undetected](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Undetected.md)] and their \[Attitude\] drops one level                                                                                                           |
