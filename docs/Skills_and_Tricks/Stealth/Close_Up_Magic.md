# Close Up Magic \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                                                         |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Stealth | [[Fine Control](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fine_Control.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Social](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You dazzle a nearby audience with astounding sleight of hand, making small items vanish and reappear to entertain and bewilder them.* 

You may use \[Close Up Magic\] on one person who is [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to you or on a [[Group](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group.md)] that is [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are \[Entertained\] and [[Bewildered](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Bewildered.md)]                                               |
| Success      | They are \[Entertained\]                                                                                                       |
| Fade         | They are [[Entertained](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Entertained.md)] and you are [[Distracted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)] |
| Glitch       | Your poor execution makes them [[Confident](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident.md)]                                               |
