# Disappear \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*By capitalizing on a sudden distraction, you slip away with not even a whisper.* 

When one or more observers [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to you become [[Distracted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)], you may use a [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)] to Disappear. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You escape and \[Move\] [[Far](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] away                                                                                                             |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and gain [[Undetected](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Undetected.md)]                                                                                       |
| Fade         | You [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)], gain \[Undetected] and have [[Stress](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stress.md)] vs. Moderate (14) |
| Glitch       | You are seen and their \[Attitude\] becomes Hostile                                                                                                                        |
