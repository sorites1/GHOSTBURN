# First Aid \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                               |
| --------------------- | -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Stabilization DL      | Medicine | [[Concentrate](/../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Supplies](/../Keywords/Keyword_Supplies)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*When a friend falls in combat or suffers from another treatable condition, you rush to their side and use your medical knowledge to stabilize them.*

If you use First Aid without [[Supplies](/../Keywords/Keyword_Supplies)] raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stabilized\] and heal 2 hp + your Medicine bonus                                                                                                                           |
| Success      | They are \[Stabilized\] and gain 2 hp                                                                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | They are [[Stabilized](/../Conditions/Condition_Stabilized)] and you have [[Stress](/../Conditions/Condition_Stress)] vs. [[Stabilization DL](/../Keywords/Keyword_Stabilization_DL)] |
| Glitch       | They take 2 damage and gain [[Bleeding](/../Conditions/Condition_Bleeding)]                                                                                                           |
