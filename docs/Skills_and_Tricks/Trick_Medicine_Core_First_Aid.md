# First Aid \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                               |
| --------------------- | -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Stabilization DL      | Medicine | [[Concentrate](../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Supplies](../Keywords/Keyword_Supplies\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*When a friend falls in combat or suffers from another treatable condition, you rush to their side and use your medical knowledge to stabilize them.*

If you use First Aid without [[Supplies](../Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)] raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stabilized\] and heal 2 hp + your Medicine bonus                                                                                                                           |
| Success      | They are \[Stabilized\] and gain 2 hp                                                                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | They are [[Stabilized](../Conditions/Condition_Stabilized\.md)] and you have [[Stress](../Conditions/Condition_Stress\.md)] vs. [[Stabilization DL](../Keywords/Keyword_Stabilization_DL\.md)] |
| Glitch       | They take 2 damage and gain [[Bleeding](../Conditions/Condition_Bleeding\.md)]                                                                                                           |
