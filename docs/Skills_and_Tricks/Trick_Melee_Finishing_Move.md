# Finishing Move \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                              |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](/../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed)]           | Melee | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)], [[Melee](/../Keywords/Keyword_Melee)], [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You sense your opponent's weakness and use your weapon to finish them.* 

When an opponent who is [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] or [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)] goes from \[Fine\] to \[Hurting\], you can react. You [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)] to the opponent, if they are not already \[Close\], and roll Finishing Move. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                            |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are dead                                                          |
| Success      | You deal [[Maximum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage)]       |
| Fade         | You deal [[Normal Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage)]         |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](/../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance)] |
