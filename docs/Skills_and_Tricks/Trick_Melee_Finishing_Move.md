# Finishing Move \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                              |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed\.md)]           | Melee | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)], [[Melee](../Keywords/Keyword_Melee\.md)], [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Reaction](../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You sense your opponent's weakness and use your weapon to finish them.* 

When an opponent who is [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] goes from \[Fine\] to \[Hurting\], you can react. You [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] to the opponent, if they are not already \[Close\], and roll Finishing Move. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                            |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are dead                                                          |
| Success      | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\.md)]       |
| Fade         | You deal [[Normal Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\.md)]         |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\.md)] |
