# Strafing Run \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                              |
| --------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Piloting | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], [[Control Deck](../Keywords/Keyword_Control_Deck\.md)], [[Datajack](../Keywords/Keyword_Datajack\.md)], [[Drone](../Keywords/Keyword_Drone\.md)], [[Fly](../Keywords/Keyword_Fly\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)], [[Wireless](../Keywords/Keyword_Wireless\.md)] |

*You fly your drone low and fast, firing its automatic weapons to rain down lead on your opponents.*

To use Strafing Run, your [[Drone](../Keywords/Keyword_Drone.md)] must be outfitted with an automatic weapon. You may target up to three opponents with Strafing Run, all of which must be [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] your \[Drone\] and each other. Use the weapon's Full Auto [[Damage Array](../Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array.md)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They take [[Normal Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\.md)] and are [[Suppressed](../Conditions/Condition_Suppressed\.md)], and you [[Fly](../Keywords/Keyword_Fly\.md)] the \[Drone\] [[Far](../Keywords/Keyword_Far\.md)] away |
| Success      | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Far](../Keywords/Keyword_Far\.md)] away                                                                                                                            |
| Fade         | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\]                                                                                                                                                        |
| Glitch       | You miss, \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby\.md)], and are [[Out of Ammo](../Keywords/Keyword_Out_of_Ammo\.md)]                                                                                          |
