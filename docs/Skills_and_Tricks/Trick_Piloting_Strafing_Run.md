# Strafing Run \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                              |
| --------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Piloting | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], [[Control Deck](/../Keywords/Keyword_Control_Deck)], [[Datajack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Datajack)], [[Drone](/../Keywords/Keyword_Drone)], [[Fly](/../Keywords/Keyword_Fly)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)], [[Wireless](/../Keywords/Keyword_Wireless)] |

*You fly your drone low and fast, firing its automatic weapons to rain down lead on your opponents.*

To use Strafing Run, your [[Drone](/../Keywords/Keyword_Drone)] must be outfitted with an automatic weapon. You may target up to three opponents with Strafing Run, all of which must be [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)] your \[Drone\] and each other. Use the weapon's Full Auto [[Damage Array](/../Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They take [[Normal Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage)] and are [[Suppressed](/../Conditions/Condition_Suppressed)], and you [[Fly](/../Keywords/Keyword_Fly)] the \[Drone\] [[Far](/../Keywords/Keyword_Far)] away |
| Success      | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Far](/../Keywords/Keyword_Far)] away                                                                                                                            |
| Fade         | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\]                                                                                                                                                        |
| Glitch       | You miss, \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)], and are [[Out of Ammo](/../Keywords/Keyword_Out_of_Ammo)]                                                                                          |
