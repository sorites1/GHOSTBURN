# Full Auto \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Severe (26)           | Firearms | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], [[Gun](../Keywords/Keyword_Gun\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You hold the trigger and lay down a continuous stream of hot lead.* 

When you use Full Auto, your target must be at the [[Optimal Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range.md)] for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. You may use [[Spray](../Keywords/Keyword_Spray.md)] to hit up to four additional targets (five total). Use your weapon's Full Auto [[Damage Array](../Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array.md)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\.md)] and they are \[Suppressed\]                                                                |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\.md)] and they are [[Suppressed](../Conditions/Condition_Suppressed\.md)]                             |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage\.md)], they are \[Suppressed\], your weapon is [[Out of Ammo](../Keywords/Keyword_Out_of_Ammo\.md)] |
| Glitch       | Your weapon is \[Out of Ammo\]                                                                                                                              |
