# Full Auto \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Severe (26)           | Firearms | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], [[Gun](/../Keywords/Keyword_Gun)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You hold the trigger and lay down a continuous stream of hot lead.* 

When you use Full Auto, your target must be at the [[Optimal Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Optimal_Range)] for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. You may use [[Spray](/../Keywords/Keyword_Spray)] to hit up to four additional targets (five total). Use your weapon's Full Auto [[Damage Array](/../Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage)] and they are \[Suppressed\]                                    |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage)] and they are [[Suppressed](/../Conditions/Condition_Suppressed)] |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage)]                                                                |
| Glitch       | Weapon jam or out of ammo. You must spend 1 AP to use this weapon again.                                                        |
