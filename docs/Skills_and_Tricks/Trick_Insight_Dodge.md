# Dodge \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                               |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Insight | [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Reaction](../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You sense the attack almost before you see it and deftly sidestep and duck out of harms way.* 

When you are hit by a [[Melee](../Keywords/Keyword_Melee.md)] [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], you may use a [[Reaction](../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)] to \[Dodge\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | Their \[Attack\] is \[Downgraded\] and you [[Counterattack](../Keywords/Keyword_Counterattack\.md)]                                                                   |
| Success      | Their \[Attack\]  is \[Downgraded]                                                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | Their [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)] is [[Downgraded](../Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded\.md)] and you are [[Off Balance](../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\.md)] |
| Glitch       | You take the hit and are [[Prone](../Conditions/Condition_Prone\.md)]                                                                                                 |
