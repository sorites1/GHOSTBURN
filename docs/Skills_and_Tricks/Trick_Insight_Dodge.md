# Dodge \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                               |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Insight | [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You sense the attack almost before you see it and deftly sidestep and duck out of harms way.* 

When you are hit by a [[Melee](/../Keywords/Keyword_Melee)] [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], you may use a [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)] to \[Dodge\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | Their \[Attack\] is \[Downgraded\] and you [[Counterattack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Counterattack)]                                                                   |
| Success      | Their \[Attack\]  is \[Downgraded]                                                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | Their [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)] is [[Downgraded](/../Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded)] and you are [[Off Balance](/../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance)] |
| Glitch       | You take the hit and are [[Prone](/../Conditions/Condition_Prone)]                                                                                                 |
