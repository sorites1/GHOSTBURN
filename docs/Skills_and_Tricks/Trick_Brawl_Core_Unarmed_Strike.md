# Unarmed Strike \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](/../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed)]           | Brawl | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], \[Unarmed], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You square off against your opponent and hit them with a punch.* 

To use Unarmed Strike, your opponent must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage)] to them and they are \[Off Balance\] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage)] to them                                |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage)] to them                              |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](/../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance)]                                |
