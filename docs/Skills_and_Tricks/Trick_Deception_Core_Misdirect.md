# Misdirect \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                 |
| --------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Wits                  | Deception | [[Attitude](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude)], [[Language](/../Keywords/Keyword_Language)], [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)]. [[Social](/../Keywords/Keyword_Social)], [[Vocal](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal)] |

*Whether by shouting and pointing or simply by talking and using one hand, you momentarily distract your opponent.*

To use Misdirect on an opponent, they must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] or [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)]. If the target has an \[Attitude\] of Hostile towards you, raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are [[Inspired](/../Conditions/Condition_Inspired)]         |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                                                                     |
| Fade         | They are [[Distracted](/../Conditions/Condition_Distracted)] and they become \[Suspicious\] |
| Glitch       | They become \[Suspicious\], and they gain [[Confident](/../Conditions/Condition_Confident)] |
