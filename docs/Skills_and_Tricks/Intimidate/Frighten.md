# Frighten \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | [[Language](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language.md)], [[Social](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)], [[Vocal](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal.md)] |

*You turn to an individual and look them in the eye. Maybe you say a few words, or you might just let your body language do the talking. Regardless, they are left broken from the brief exchange.* 

If you use Frighten without speaking, raise the DL by one. You cannot use this trick on someone who is currently suffering from a [[Break](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Break.md)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They faint and are rendered [[Unconscious](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Unconscious.md)]              |
| Success      | They \[Break\] and flee                                                                        |
| Fade         | They [[Break](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Break.md)] and [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)] you |
| Glitch       | They are not scared of you and they gain [[Confident](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident.md)]     |
