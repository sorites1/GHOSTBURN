# Shred \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Intimidate | [[Concentrate](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate.md)], [[Language](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Social](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social.md)], [[Vocal](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal.md)] |

*You have perfected the art of the verbal takedown, and your words cut to the bone.*

To use Shred on an opponent, they must be [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stunned\] and [[Shaken](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Shaken.md)]                                                                                                               |
| Success      | They are \[Stunned\] and their [[Attitude](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude.md)] worsens                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | They are [[Stunned](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stunned.md)], their [[Attitude](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude.md)] worsens, and you are [[Distracted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)] |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become [[Confident](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident.md)]                                                                                     |
