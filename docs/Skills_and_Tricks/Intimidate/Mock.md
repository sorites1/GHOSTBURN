# Mock \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Intimidate | [[Concentrate](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate.md)], [[Language](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Social](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social.md)], [[Vocal](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal.md)] |

*You hone in on your opponent's insecurities and ruthlessly mock them, undermining their confidence.*

To use Mock on an opponent, they must be [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are \[Distracted\] and you are [[Inspired](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired.md)]                                            |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\] and their [[Attitude](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude.md)] worsens                                          |
| Fade         | You are both [[Distracted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)] and their [[Attitude](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude.md)] worsens |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become [[Confident](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident.md)]                                 |
