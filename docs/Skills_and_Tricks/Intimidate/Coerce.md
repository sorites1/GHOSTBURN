# Coerce \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | [[Language](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language.md)], [[Social](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social.md)], [[Vocal](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal.md)] |

*With threats of damage to income, reputation, or bodily wellbeing, you force your victim to comply with your instructions.* 

If you use Coerce on a [[Far](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] away target or remotely, such as over a call, raise the DL by one. If your instructions would put the target or their loved ones in a dangerous situation, raise the DL by one. The target will outright refuse instructions that would result in serious injury or certain death. If you threaten the target with a deadly weapon as part of your Coerce attempt, lower the DL by one. If you use Coerce to threaten a [[Group](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group.md)], use the highest [[Fort](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fort.md)] amongst them and apply the outcome to the \[Group\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                             |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are [[Servile](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Servile.md)]                                                                                  |
| Success      | They are \[Compliant\]                                                                                                                  |
| Fade         | They are [[Compliant](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Compliant.md)] and you have [[Stress](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stress.md)] vs. Fort            |
| Glitch       | They are not intimidated, their [[Attitude](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude.md)] worsens, and they [[Break](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Break.md)] |
