# Read Person \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                    |
| --------------------- | ------- | --------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Insight | [[Concentrate](/../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You surmise information about a person by studying them, the way they speak, the clothes they're wearing, their facial expressions, and body language.* 

When you use Read Person, ask the GM to identify a person's current emotional state, immediate goals, or way of thinking. To use Read Person on someone, they must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] or [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | GM gives you information and you gain a [[Secret](/../Keywords/Keyword_Secret)]                  |
| Success      | GM gives you information                                                                         |
| Fade         | GM gives you information and you are \[Distracted\]                                              |
| Glitch       | You do not learn any information and you are [[Distracted](/../Conditions/Condition_Distracted)] |
