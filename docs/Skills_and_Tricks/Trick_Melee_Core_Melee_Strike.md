# Melee Strike \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](/../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed)]           | Melee | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], \[Melee], [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You attack your opponent by viciously swinging or thrusting your melee weapon at them.* 

To use Melee Strike, your opponent must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage)] to them and they are [[Prone](/../Conditions/Condition_Prone)] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage)] to them                                                          |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage)] to them                                                        |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](/../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance)]                                                          |
