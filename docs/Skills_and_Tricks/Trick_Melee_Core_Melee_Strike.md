# Melee Strike \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                |
| --------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed\.md)]           | Melee | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], \[Melee], [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You attack your opponent by viciously swinging or thrusting your melee weapon at them.* 

To use Melee Strike, your opponent must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\.md)] to them and they are [[Prone](../Conditions/Condition_Prone\.md)] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\.md)] to them                                                          |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage\.md)] to them                                                        |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\.md)]                                                          |
