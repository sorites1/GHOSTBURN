# Understanding Skills and Tricks

There are fifteen skills in this game, three for each of the core five [[Archive/Abilities_and_Ability_Scores|abilities]] (Presence, Awareness, Agility, Intelligence, and Strength). For example, the three skills associated with Agility are [Drive](../Skills_and_Tricks/Drive/Drive_Skill.md), [Firearms](../Skills_and_Tricks/Firearms/Firearms_Skill.md), and [Stealth](../Skills_and_Tricks/Stealth/Stealth_Skill.md).

If your character knows a skill, they will have a [[Skill Bonus](../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Skill_Bonus.md)] ranging from +1 to +3. When you [Make a Roll](../Playing_the_Game/Rolling_the_Dice/Making_a_Roll.md) with a skill, you add your \[Skill Bonus\] and the skill's associated [[Ability Score](../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Ability_Score.md)] to the roll. 

Each skill has associated [[Tricks](../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Trick.md)], which are specific uses of the skill. For example, the [Medicine](../Skills_and_Tricks/Medicine/Medicine_Skill.md) skill includes a trick called [First Aid](../Skills_and_Tricks/Medicine/First_Aid.md) \[Core Trick\] and another called [Surgery](../Skills_and_Tricks/Medicine/Surgery.md) \[Signature Trick\].

Each trick is categorized as being either a [[Core Trick](../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Core_Trick.md)] or a [[Signature Trick](../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Signature_Trick.md)]. You can use any \[Core Trick\] found in these rules, even if you don't have a \[Skill Bonus\] in that skill, but you can only use a \[Signature\] trick if your character knows it.

When you want to try something not covered by these rules, the GM can allow you to perform it as an [[Improvised Trick](../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Improvised_Trick.md)].
