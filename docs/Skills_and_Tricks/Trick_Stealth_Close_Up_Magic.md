# Close Up Magic \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                                                         |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Stealth | [[Fine Control](../Keywords/Keyword_Fine_Control\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Social](../Keywords/Keyword_Social\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You dazzle a nearby audience with astounding sleight of hand, making small items vanish and reappear to entertain and bewilder them.* 

You may use \[Close Up Magic\] on one person who is [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to you or on a [[Group](../Keywords/Keyword_Group.md)] that is [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are \[Entertained\] and [[Bewildered](../Conditions/Condition_Bewildered\.md)]                                               |
| Success      | They are \[Entertained\]                                                                                                       |
| Fade         | They are [[Entertained](../Conditions/Condition_Entertained\.md)] and you are [[Distracted](../Conditions/Condition_Distracted\.md)] |
| Glitch       | Your poor execution makes them [[Confident](../Conditions/Condition_Confident\.md)]                                               |
