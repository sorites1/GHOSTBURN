# Close Up Magic \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                                                         |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Stealth | [[Fine Control](/../Keywords/Keyword_Fine_Control)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Social](/../Keywords/Keyword_Social)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You dazzle a nearby audience with astounding sleight of hand, making small items vanish and reappear to entertain and bewilder them.* 

You may use \[Close Up Magic\] on one person who is [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] to you or on a [[Group](/../Keywords/Keyword_Group)] that is [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are \[Entertained\] and [[Bewildered](/../Conditions/Condition_Bewildered)]                                               |
| Success      | They are \[Entertained\]                                                                                                       |
| Fade         | They are [[Entertained](/../Conditions/Condition_Entertained)] and you are [[Distracted](/../Conditions/Condition_Distracted)] |
| Glitch       | Your poor execution makes them [[Confident](/../Conditions/Condition_Confident)]                                               |
