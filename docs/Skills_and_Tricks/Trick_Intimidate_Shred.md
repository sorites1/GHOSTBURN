# Shred \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Intimidate | [[Concentrate](/../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate)], [[Language](/../Keywords/Keyword_Language)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Social](/../Keywords/Keyword_Social)], [[Vocal](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal)] |

*You have perfected the art of the verbal takedown, and your words cut to the bone.*

To use Shred on an opponent, they must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] or [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stunned\] and [[Shaken](/../Conditions/Condition_Shaken)]                                                                                                               |
| Success      | They are \[Stunned\] and their [[Attitude](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude)] worsens                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | They are [[Stunned](/../Conditions/Condition_Stunned)], their [[Attitude](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude)] worsens, and you are [[Distracted](/../Conditions/Condition_Distracted)] |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become [[Confident](/../Conditions/Condition_Confident)]                                                                                     |
