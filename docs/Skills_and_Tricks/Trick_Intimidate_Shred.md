# Shred \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Intimidate | [[Concentrate](../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\.md)], [[Language](../Keywords/Keyword_Language\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Social](../Keywords/Keyword_Social\.md)], [[Vocal](../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\.md)] |

*You have perfected the art of the verbal takedown, and your words cut to the bone.*

To use Shred on an opponent, they must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stunned\] and [[Shaken](../Conditions/Condition_Shaken\.md)]                                                                                                               |
| Success      | They are \[Stunned\] and their [[Attitude](../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\.md)] worsens                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | They are [[Stunned](../Conditions/Condition_Stunned\.md)], their [[Attitude](../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\.md)] worsens, and you are [[Distracted](../Conditions/Condition_Distracted\.md)] |
| Glitch       | They are unaffected by your words and become [[Confident](../Conditions/Condition_Confident\.md)]                                                                                     |
