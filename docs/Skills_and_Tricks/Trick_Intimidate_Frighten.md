## Frighten \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | \[[[Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\], \[[[Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\] |

*You turn to an individual and look them in the eye. Maybe you say a few words, or you might just let your body language do the talking. Regardless, they are left broken from the brief exchange.* 

If you use Frighten without speaking, raise the DL by one. You cannot use this trick on someone who is currently suffering from a \[[[Condition_Break|Break]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                            |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Glitch       | They are not scared of you and they gain \[[[Condition_Confident\|Confident]]\] |
| Fade         | They \[[[Condition_Break\|Break]]\] and \[[[Keyword_Attack\|Attack]]\] you                      |
| Success      | They \[Break\] and flee                                |
| Cool Success | They faint and are rendered \[[[Condition_Unconscious\|Unconscious]]\]            |
