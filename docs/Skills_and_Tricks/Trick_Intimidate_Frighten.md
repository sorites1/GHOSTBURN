# Frighten \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                        |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | [[Language](/../Keywords/Keyword_Language)], [[Social](/../Keywords/Keyword_Social)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)], [[Vocal](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal)] |

*You turn to an individual and look them in the eye. Maybe you say a few words, or you might just let your body language do the talking. Regardless, they are left broken from the brief exchange.* 

If you use Frighten without speaking, raise the DL by one. You cannot use this trick on someone who is currently suffering from a [[Break](/../Conditions/Condition_Break)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They faint and are rendered [[Unconscious](/../Conditions/Condition_Unconscious)]              |
| Success      | They \[Break\] and flee                                                                        |
| Fade         | They [[Break](/../Conditions/Condition_Break)] and [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)] you |
| Glitch       | They are not scared of you and they gain [[Confident](/../Conditions/Condition_Confident)]     |
