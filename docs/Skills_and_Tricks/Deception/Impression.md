# Impression \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                           |
| --------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Hard (20)             | Deception | [[Concentrate](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate.md)], [[Language](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Social](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social.md)], [[Vocal](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal.md)] |

*By altering your voice, facial expressions, and even the way you sit or stand, you do a convincing impression of another person.*

You can use Impression to achieve three different effects. First, you can use it to make those around you [[Entertained](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Entertained.md)]. Second, if you have only verbal communication with your opponent (such as with an audio-only call), you can trick the listener into believing you are someone else (by doing an Impression of that person's voice). Third, by combining this trick with [Disguise](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Deception/Disguise.md), you can fool people up close into thinking you are the person you pretend to be. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                          |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | You get the desired effect, and you gain an [[Ally](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Ally.md)]                                                                                          |
| Success      | You get the desired effect                                                                                                                                                           |
| Fade         | You get the desired effect, but they become [[Suspicious](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suspicous.md)] or you gain a [[Bad Memory](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Bad_Memory.md)]         |
| Glitch       | You do not get the desired effect, and they become [[Suspicious](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suspicous.md)] or you gain a [[Bad Memory](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Bad_Memory.md)]  |
