# Disguise \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                         |
| --------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Deception | [[Advanced Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)] |

*Using clothing, makeup, or props, you create a disguise.*

When you use Disguise, you create a desired look using [[Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)], which may include clothing, makeup, and basic accessories. You can use Disguise to produce one of three effects. First, you can make the target (yourself or a willing subject) unrecognizable. Second, you can create a look that helps the target 'fit in' to a specific environment. Third, if you have [[Advanced Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies.md)], which include prosthetics, wigs, and so forth, you can replicate another person's physical appearance, though this raises the DL by one. The target may combine this third use of Disguise with [Impression](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Deception/Impression.md) to "become" the person they pretend to be. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | A \[Suspicious\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Hard (20) DL to see through the disguise, and you are [[Inspired](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired.md)], or NPCs roll Threat vs Severe (26), your choice |
| Success      | A \[Suspicious\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Hard (20) DL to see through the disguise                                                                                                                       |
| Fade         | When you interact with an NPC, they automatically become [[Suspicious](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suspicous.md)] and roll Threat vs. Hard (20) DL to see through the disguise                     |
| Glitch       | Your disguise is unusable, and your [[Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)] or [[Advanced Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies.md)] may be wasted (GM's discretion)     |
