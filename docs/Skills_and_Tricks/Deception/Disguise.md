# Disguise \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill     | Keywords                                                         |
| --------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Deception | [[Advanced Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)] |

*Using clothing, makeup, or props, you create a disguise.*

When you use Disguise, you create a desired look using [[Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)], which may include clothing, makeup, and basic accessories. You can use Disguise to produce one of three effects. First, you can make yourself unrecognizable. Second, you can create a look that helps you 'fit in' to a specific environment. Third, if you have [[Advanced Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies.md)], which include prosthetics, wigs, and so forth, you can replicate another person's physical appearance, including their face. You may combine this third use of Disguise with [Impression](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Deception/Impression.md) to "become" the person you pretend to be. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | A \[Suspicious\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Severe (26) DL to see through it and you are [[Inspired](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired.md)]                                                       |
| Success      | A \[Suspicious\] NPC rolls Threat vs. Hard (20) DL to see through the disguise                                                                                                                   |
| Fade         | A [[Suspicious](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suspicous.md)] NPC rolls Threat vs. Moderate (14) DL to see through the disguise                                                                   |
| Glitch       | Your disguise is unusable, and your [[Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)] or [[Advanced Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Advanced_Supplies.md)] may be wasted (GM's discretion) |
