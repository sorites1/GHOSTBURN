# Street Culture \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords        |
| --------------------- | ------- | --------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Culture | [[Concentrate](/../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate)] |

*You know where to go and what to do to survive on the street.* 

When you use Street Culture, you ask the GM a question related to gangs (colors, territory, leadership, factions), squatting, scavenging, stealing, and drugs. For example, *Where is the nearest homeless camp* or *Do I recognize those gang colors? What do I know about the Sick Vultures gang?*

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | The GM answers the question and you gain a [[Secret](/../Keywords/Keyword_Secret)]         |
| Success      | The GM answers the question                                                                |
| Fade         | The GM answers the question and you gain a [[Bad Memory](/../Keywords/Keyword_Bad_Memory)] |
| Glitch       | You don't know and you gain a \[Bad Memory\]                                               |
