# Shakedown \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | [[Language](../Keywords/Keyword_Language\.md)], [[Social](../Keywords/Keyword_Social\.md)], [[Vocal](../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal\.md)] |

*By making veiled threats to your victim, you make them see that it's in their best interests to give you a "gift."* 

You can suggest what you would like to receive, but the GM is not obligated to have the NPC grant you a specific \[Gift.\]

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They give you an expensive \[Gift\] and a [[Secret](../Keywords/Keyword_Secret\.md)]                                                                                                     |
| Success      | They give you a regular \[Gift\]                                                                                                                                                      |
| Fade         | They give you a minor [[Gift](../Keywords/Keyword_Gift\.md)], their [[Attitude](../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\.md)] worsens, and they gain [[Confident](../Conditions/Condition_Confident\.md)] |
| Glitch       | They are not intimidated, their [[Attitude](../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude\.md)] worsens, and they [[Break](../Conditions/Condition_Break\.md)]                                               |
