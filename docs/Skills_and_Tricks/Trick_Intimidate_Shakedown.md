## Shakedown \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | \[[[Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Keyword_Social\|Social]]\], \[[[Keyword_Vocal\|Vocal]]\] |

*By making veiled threats to your victim, you make them see that it's in their best interests to give you a "gift."* 

You can suggest what you would like to receive, but the GM is not obligated to have the NPC grant you a specific \[Gift.\]

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                              |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | They are not intimidated, their \[[[Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\] worsens, and they \[[[Condition_Break\|Break]]\]                                      |
| Fade         | They give you a minor \[[[Keyword_Gift\|Gift]]\], their \[[[Keyword_Attitude\|Attitude]]\] worsens, and they gain \[[[Condition_Confident\|Confident]]\] |
| Success      | They give you a regular \[Gift\]                                                                                                                         |
| Cool Success | They give you an expensive \[Gift\] and a \[[[Keyword_Secret\|Secret]]\]                                                                                 |
