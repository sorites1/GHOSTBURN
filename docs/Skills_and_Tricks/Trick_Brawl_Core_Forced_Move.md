# Forced Move \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed\.md)]           | Brawl | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You grab your opponent and force them to move with your superior ability.* 

To use Forced Move, your opponent must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)]. If they are [[Restrained](../Conditions/Condition_Restrained.md)], your Forced Move outcome is [[Upgraded](../Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded.md)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You force them to \[Move\] and put them in [[Agony](../Conditions/Condition_Agony\.md)] if desired |
| Success      | You force them to \[Move\]                                                                      |
| Fade         | You force them to [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)] and you are \[Off Balance\]               |
| Glitch       | You fail and are [[Off Balance](../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\.md)]                          |
