# Forced Move \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](/../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed)]           | Brawl | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You grab your opponent and force them to move with your superior ability.* 

To use Forced Move, your opponent must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)]. If they are [[Restrained](/../Conditions/Condition_Restrained)], your Forced Move outcome is [[Upgraded](/../Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You force them to \[Move\] and put them in [[Agony](/../Conditions/Condition_Agony)] if desired |
| Success      | You force them to \[Move\]                                                                      |
| Fade         | You force them to [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)] and you are \[Off Balance\]               |
| Glitch       | You fail and are [[Off Balance](/../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance)]                          |
