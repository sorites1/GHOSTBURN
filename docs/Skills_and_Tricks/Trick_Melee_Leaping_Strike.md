# Leaping Strike \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                          |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](/../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed)]           | Melee | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], \[Melee], [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)], [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You get a running start, fall from above, or swing in on a rope to perform a mid-air strike.* 

To use Leaping Strike, first make a basic Athletics roll with a DL of Moderate (14). If you fail, you fall [[Prone](/../Conditions/Condition_Prone)] and your action ends. Otherwise, you automatically [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)] through the air to a [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)] opponent by jumping, swinging, or falling, for example, and roll Leaping Strike. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage)] to them and they are \[Prone\] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage)] to them                          |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage)] to them                        |
| Glitch       | You miss and fall [[Prone](/../Conditions/Condition_Prone)]                                     |
