# Leaping Strike \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                          |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](../Keywords/Keyword_Opposed\.md)]           | Melee | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], \[Melee], [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)], [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You get a running start, fall from above, or swing in on a rope to perform a mid-air strike.* 

To use Leaping Strike, first make a basic Athletics roll with a DL of Moderate (14). If you fail, you fall [[Prone](../Conditions/Condition_Prone.md)] and your action ends. Otherwise, you automatically [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] through the air to a [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] opponent by jumping, swinging, or falling, for example, and roll Leaping Strike. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\.md)] to them and they are \[Prone\] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\.md)] to them                          |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage\.md)] to them                        |
| Glitch       | You miss and fall [[Prone](../Conditions/Condition_Prone\.md)]                                     |
