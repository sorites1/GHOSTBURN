# Disappear \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Reaction](../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*By capitalizing on a sudden distraction, you slip away with not even a whisper.* 

When one or more observers [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to you become [[Distracted](../Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)], you may use a [[Reaction](../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)] to Disappear. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You escape and \[Move\] [[Far](../Keywords/Keyword_Far\.md)] away                                                                                                             |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and gain [[Undetected](../Conditions/Condition_Undetected\.md)]                                                                                       |
| Fade         | You [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)] [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby\.md)], gain \[Undetected] and have [[Stress](../Conditions/Condition_Stress\.md)] vs. Moderate (14) |
| Glitch       | You are seen and their \[Attitude\] becomes Hostile                                                                                                                        |
