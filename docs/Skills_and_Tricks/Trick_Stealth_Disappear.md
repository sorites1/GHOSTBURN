# Disappear \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                                          |
| --------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*By capitalizing on a sudden distraction, you slip away with not even a whisper.* 

When one or more observers [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] to you become [[Distracted](/../Conditions/Condition_Distracted)], you may use a [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)] to Disappear. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You escape and \[Move\] [[Far](/../Keywords/Keyword_Far)] away                                                                                                             |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and gain [[Undetected](/../Conditions/Condition_Undetected)]                                                                                       |
| Fade         | You [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)] [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)], gain \[Undetected] and have [[Stress](/../Conditions/Condition_Stress)] vs. Moderate (14) |
| Glitch       | You are seen and their \[Attitude\] becomes Hostile                                                                                                                        |
