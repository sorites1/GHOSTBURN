# Avoid Crash \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Vehicle](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*In a split second, you react to an oncoming hazard and swerve to avoid a crash.* 

When you are driving a [[Vehicle](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle)] at [[High Speed](/../Keywords/Keyword_High_Speed)] and a sudden obstacle or hazard appears on the road ahead or if your vehicle [[Crashes](/../Keywords/Keyword_Crashes)] for another reason, you can use a [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)] to use Avoid Crash. The [[Handling](/../Keywords/Keyword_Handling)] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Avoid Crash roll. If you use only [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You avoid the obstacle or hazard and are [[Inspired](/../Conditions/Condition_Inspired)]                                          |
| Success      | You avoid the obstacle or hazard                                                                                                  |
| Fade         | You avoid the obstacle or hazard (near miss) and you are [[Shaken](/../Conditions/Condition_Shaken)]                              |
| Glitch       | Your [[Vehicle](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle)] collides with the obstacle or hazard and [[Crashes](/../Keywords/Keyword_Crashes)] |
