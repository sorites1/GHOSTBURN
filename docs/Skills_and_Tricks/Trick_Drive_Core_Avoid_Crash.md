# Avoid Crash \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Reaction](../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*In a split second, you react to an oncoming hazard and swerve to avoid a crash.* 

When you are driving a [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle.md)] at [[High Speed](../Keywords/Keyword_High_Speed.md)] and a sudden obstacle or hazard appears on the road ahead or if your vehicle [[Crashes](../Keywords/Keyword_Crashes.md)] for another reason, you can use a [[Reaction](../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)] to use Avoid Crash. The [[Handling](../Keywords/Keyword_Handling.md)] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Avoid Crash roll. If you use only [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You avoid the obstacle or hazard and are [[Inspired](../Conditions/Condition_Inspired\.md)]                                          |
| Success      | You avoid the obstacle or hazard                                                                                                  |
| Fade         | You avoid the obstacle or hazard (near miss) and you are [[Shaken](../Conditions/Condition_Shaken\.md)]                              |
| Glitch       | Your [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\.md)] collides with the obstacle or hazard and [[Crashes](../Keywords/Keyword_Crashes\.md)] |
