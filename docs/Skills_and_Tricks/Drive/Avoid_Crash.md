# Avoid Crash \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Hard (20)             | Drive | [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], [[Vehicle](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*In a split second, you react to an oncoming hazard and swerve to avoid a crash.* 

When you are driving a [[Vehicle](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle.md)] at [[High Speed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_High_Speed.md)] and a sudden obstacle or hazard appears on the road ahead or if your vehicle [[Crashes](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Crashes.md)] for another reason, you can use a [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)] to use Avoid Crash. The [[Handling](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Handling.md)] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Avoid Crash roll. If you use only [[One Hand](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You avoid the obstacle or hazard and are [[Inspired](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Inspired.md)]                                          |
| Success      | You avoid the obstacle or hazard                                                                                                  |
| Fade         | You avoid the obstacle or hazard (near miss) and you are [[Shaken](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Shaken.md)]                              |
| Glitch       | Your [[Vehicle](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle.md)] collides with the obstacle or hazard and [[Crashes](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Crashes.md)] |
