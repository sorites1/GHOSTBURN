# Dead Eye \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                     |
| --------------------- | ---------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | [[Gun](../Keywords/Keyword_Gun\.md)], [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\.md)] \[Ranged\], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You zero in on your opponent and raise your gun. One heartbeat later, you send hot lead their way.* 

To use Dead Eye on an opponent, they must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. If you are wielding a pistol, lower the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | The next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is is an automatic \[Cool Success\]                                                                             |
| Success      | The next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is \[Upgraded\]                                                                                                 |
| Fade         | Your next \[Gun\] \[Attack\] you make on them this turn is [[Upgraded](../Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded\.md)] and you are [[Distracted](../Conditions/Condition_Distracted\.md)] |
| Glitch       | Your next [[Gun](../Keywords/Keyword_Gun\.md)] [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)] you make on anyone this turn is [[Downgraded](../Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded\.md)]    |
