# Read Lips \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                             |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | [[Concentrate](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate.md)], [[Language](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You focus on a person's lips as they speak and though you cannot hear them, you understand what they are saying.* 

To use Read Lips, the person speaking must be [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. The scope of Read Lips is one sentence (or a few short sentences.) You must roll Read Lips again each time you want to decipher what the person said. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                         |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Read Lips is an automatic Success on them for the rest of the scene |
| Success      | You know what they said                                             |
| Fade         | You know what they said and they become \[Suspicious\]              |
| Glitch       | You don't know what they said and they become \[Suspicious\]        |
