# Overwatch \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | [[Auditory](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Auditory.md)], [[Concentrate](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate.md)], [Language](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language.md), \[Verbal\], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You survey the combat zone and relay relevant information to your crew, noting the positions and movements of friends, enemies, and civilians.* 

When you use Overwatch, pick one ally in the same battle as you. The ally must have hands-free wireless comms (headset or cyberware) to benefit from this trick. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\] and they gain 1 [[Temporary](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Temporary.md)] [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)]                   |
| Success      | Their next \[Attack\] is \[Upgraded\]                                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | Their next [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)] is [[Upgraded](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Upgraded.md)] and you are [[Distracted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)] |
| Glitch       | They get no benefit and you are [[Confused](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confused.md)]                                                                                   |
