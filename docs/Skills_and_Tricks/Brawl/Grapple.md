# Grapple \[Core Trick\]

Without this trick, 

| Difficulty Level (DL)            | Skill | Keywords                                                                                                                                                     |
| -------------------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| [[Opposed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Opposed.md)] | Brawl | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You run after your opponent, grabbing them and tacking them to the ground.* 

When you use Grapple, [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] to an opponent who is [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] and then roll Grapple.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                            |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Restrained\], \[Prone\] and [[Shaken](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Shaken.md)]                                     |
| Success      | They are \[Restrained\] and \[Prone\]                                                                                  |
| Fade         | They are \[Restrained\] and you are both \[Prone\]                                                                     |
| Glitch       | You are [[Restrained](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Restrained.md)] and you are both [[Prone](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone.md)] |
