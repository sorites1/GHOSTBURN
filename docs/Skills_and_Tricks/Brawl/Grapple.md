# Grapple \[Core Trick\]

Without this trick, 

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| --------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Brawl | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], [[Unarmed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Unarmed.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You run after your opponent, grabbing them and tacking them to the ground.* 

When you use Grapple, if you target a [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] opponent, you may [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to that opponent for free (0 AP) and then roll Grapple.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Restrained\] and \[Prone\] or in [[Agony](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Agony.md)], your choice |
| Success      | They are \[Restrained\]                                                                                    |
| Fade         | They are [[Restrained](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Restrained.md)] and you are both \[Prone\]            |
| Glitch       | You are [[Prone](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone.md)]                                                  |
