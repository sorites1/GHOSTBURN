# Unarmed Strike \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                  |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Opposed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Opposed.md)]           | Brawl | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[One Hand](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], \[Unarmed], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You square off against your opponent and hit them with a punch.* 

To use Unarmed Strike, your opponent must be [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)]. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage.md)] to them and they are \[Off Balance\] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage.md)] to them                                |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage.md)] to them                              |
| Glitch       | You miss and are [[Off Balance](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance.md)]                                |
