# Unarmed Strike \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| --------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Brawl | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[One Hand](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Unarmed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Unarmed.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You square off against your opponent and hit them with a punch.* 

To use Unarmed Strike, your opponent must be [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)]. Use your Unarmed [[Damage Array](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array.md)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You deal [[Maximum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage.md)] or deal \[Normal Damage\]and they are [[Off Balance](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance.md)] |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage.md)]                                                                                                       |
| Fade         | You deal \[Normal Damage\] and your target gains a \[Combat Opportunity\]                                                                                                    |
| Glitch       | Your target gains a [[Combat Opportunity](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Combat_Opportunity.md)]                                                                                  |
