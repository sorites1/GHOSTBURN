# Silent Takedown \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Brawl | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], \[Unarmed\], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)] [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You swiftly and silently close in on your opponent and execute a maneuver to quietly subdue them before they have time to react.* 

To use Silent Takedown, you must be [[Undetected](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Undetected.md)], and your opponent must be [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. When you use this trick, you silently [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to them and roll Silent Takedown. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are [[Unconscious](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Unconscious.md)]                                                                                   |
| Success      | They are \[Stunned\]                                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | They are [[Stunned](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stunned.md)] but cry out, alerting anyone [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]                      |
| Glitch       | You fumble the maneuver, lose [[Undetected](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Undetected.md)], and are [[Off Balance](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance.md)] |
