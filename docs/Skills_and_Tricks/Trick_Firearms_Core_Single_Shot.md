# Single Shot \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                         |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Firearms | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], [[Gun](/../Keywords/Keyword_Gun)], [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)], [[One Hand](/../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You point your gun and shoot.* 

When you use Single Shot, your target must be at the optimal range listed for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. If you use [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], lower the DL by one. Use your weapon's Single Shot [[Damage Array](/../Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Choose to deal [[Maximum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage)] or deal \[Normal Damage\] and make a free [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)]       |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage)]                                                                                             |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](/../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage)] and your opponent gains a [[Combat Opportunity](/../Keywords/Keyword_Combat_Opportunity)] |
| Glitch       | You miss and your opponent gains a \[Combat Opportunity\]                                                                                                  |
