# Single Shot \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                         |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Firearms | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], [[Gun](../Keywords/Keyword_Gun\.md)], [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)], [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You point your gun and shoot.* 

When you use Single Shot, your target must be at the optimal range listed for your weapon. If they are at a different range, raise the DL by one. If you use [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], lower the DL by one. Use your weapon's Single Shot [[Damage Array](../Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array.md)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Choose to deal [[Maximum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Maximum_Damage\.md)] or deal \[Normal Damage\] and make a free [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)]       |
| Success      | You deal [[Normal Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage\.md)]                                                                                             |
| Fade         | You deal [[Minimum Damage](../Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage\.md)] and your opponent gains a [[Combat Opportunity](../Keywords/Keyword_Combat_Opportunity\.md)] |
| Glitch       | You miss and your opponent gains a \[Combat Opportunity\]                                                                                                  |
