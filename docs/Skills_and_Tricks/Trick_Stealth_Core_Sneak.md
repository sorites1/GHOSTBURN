# Sneak \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                           |
| --------------------- | ------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*By moving slowly and quietly and by staying behind cover and out of sight, you sneak to your destination.* 

To use Sneak, you must be [[Undetected](../Conditions/Condition_Undetected.md)] by the people you're avoiding. If multiple observers are present, use the highest Wits amongst them.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and are [[Hidden](../Conditions/Condition_Hidden\.md)]                                                                                                                                   |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and remain \[Undetected\]                                                                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | You [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)] somewhere [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby\.md)], remain \[Undetected], and have [[Stress](../Conditions/Condition_Stress\.md)] vs. [[Wits](../Keywords/Keyword_Wits\.md)] |
| Glitch       | You lose [[Undetected](../Conditions/Condition_Undetected\.md)] and their \[Attitude\] drops one level                                                                                                           |
