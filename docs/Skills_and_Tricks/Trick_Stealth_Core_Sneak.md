## Sneak \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                           |
| --------------------- | ------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Stealth | \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*By moving slowly and quietly and by staying behind cover and out of sight, you sneak to your destination.* 

To use Sneak, you must be \[[[Condition_Undetected|Undetected]]\] by the people you're avoiding. If multiple observers are present, use the highest Wits amongst them.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                     |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You lose \[[[Condition_Undetected\|Undetected]]\] and their \[Attitude\] drops one level                  |
| Fade         | You \[[[Keyword_Move\|Move]]\] \[[[Keyword_Nearby\|Nearby]]\], remain \[Undetected], and have \[[[Condition_Stress\|Stress]]\] vs. \[[[Keyword_Wits\|Wits]]\] |
| Success      | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and remain \[Undetected\]                               |
| Cool Success | You \[Move\] \[Nearby\] and are \[[[Condition_Hidden\|Hidden]]\]                                      |
