*You inspect the device's electronic guts and make a calculated choice to snip one of the wires. A second later, the maglock disengages, and the door opens with a hiss.* 

Disable Device is a hands-on activity that requires you to directly interact with the target's hardware. If you do not have proper \[[[Keyword_Tools|Tools]]\], raise the DL by one.
