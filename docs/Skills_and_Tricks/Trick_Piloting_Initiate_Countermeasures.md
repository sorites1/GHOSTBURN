# Initiate Countermeasures \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                        |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Security](/../Keywords/Keyword_Security)]          | Piloting | [[Concentrate](/../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate)], [[Control Deck](/../Keywords/Keyword_Control_Deck)], [[Datajack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Datajack)], [[Drone](/../Keywords/Keyword_Drone)], [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)], [[Wireless](/../Keywords/Keyword_Wireless)] |

*Your control deck lights up with alerts. Someone is trying to jam your signal. You react by engaging your drone's electronic countermeasures and manually calibrating them to thwart the jam attempt*

When your drone is [[Jammed](/../Conditions/Condition_Jammed)], you may use a [[Reaction](/../Keywords/Keyword_Reaction)] to Initiate Countermeasures.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | The \[Jammed\] condition is removed and you are [[Lucky](/../Keywords/Keyword_Lucky)]                                                        |
| Success      | The \[Jammed\] condition is removed                                                                                                          |
| Fade         | The \[Jammed\] condition is removed and you have [[Stress](/../Conditions/Condition_Stress)] vs. [[Security](/../Keywords/Keyword_Security)] |
| Glitch       | Your drone is [[Jammed](/../Conditions/Condition_Jammed)] and you are [[Confused](/../Conditions/Condition_Confused)]                        |
