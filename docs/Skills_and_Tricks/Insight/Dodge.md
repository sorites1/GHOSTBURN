# Dodge \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                               |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Insight | [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You sense the attack almost before you see it and deftly sidestep and duck out of harms way.* 

When you are the target of a [Brawl](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Brawl/Brawl_Skill.md) or [Melee](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Melee/Melee_Skill.md) [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], you may spend a [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)] to roll Dodge. After you roll Dodge, the attacker rolls their Brawl or Melee \[Attack\] and modifies their \[Attack\] outcome based on your Dodge outcome.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | Their  [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)] outcome is [[Downgraded](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded.md)] and if you would take [[Absolute Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Absolute_Damage.md)], you instead take no damage and may immediately [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] |
| Success      | Their  [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)] outcome  is [[Downgraded](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded.md)]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Fade         | Their [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)] outcome is [[Downgraded](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded.md)] and you are [[Distracted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)]                                                                                                                                                              |
| Glitch       | You are unable to mitigate the \[Attack\] and you are [[Distracted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
