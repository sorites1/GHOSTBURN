# Dodge \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                               |
| --------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Insight | [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You sense the attack almost before you see it and deftly sidestep and duck out of harms way.* 

When you are hit by a [[Melee](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Melee.md)] [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], you may use a [[Reaction](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reaction.md)] to \[Dodge\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | Their \[Attack\] is \[Downgraded\] and you [[Counterattack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Counterattack.md)]                                                                                   |
| Success      | Their \[Attack\]  is \[Downgraded]                                                                                                                                                         |
| Fade         | Their [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)] is [[Downgraded](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded.md)] and you are [[Off Balance](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance.md)] |
| Glitch       | You take the hit and are [[Prone](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Prone.md)]                                                                                                                 |
