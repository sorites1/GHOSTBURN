# Read Person \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill   | Keywords                    |
| --------------------- | ------- | --------------------------- |
| Moderate (14)         | Insight | [[Concentrate](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*You surmise information about a person by studying them, the way they speak, the clothes they're wearing, their facial expressions, and body language.* 

When you use Read Person, ask the GM to identify a person's current emotional state, immediate goals, or way of thinking. To use Read Person on someone, they must be [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | GM gives you information and you gain a [[Secret](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Secret.md)]                  |
| Success      | GM gives you information                                                                         |
| Fade         | GM gives you information and you are \[Distracted\]                                              |
| Glitch       | You do not learn any information and you are [[Distracted](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Distracted.md)] |
