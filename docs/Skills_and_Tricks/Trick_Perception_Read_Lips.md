## Read Lips \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                                             |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Perception | \[[[Keyword_Concentrate\|Concentrate]]\], \[[[Keyword_Language\|Language]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*You focus on a person's lips as they speak and though you cannot hear them, you understand what they are saying.* 

To use Read Lips, the person speaking must be \[[[Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\]. The scope of Read Lips is one sentence (or a few short sentences.) You must roll Read Lips again each time you want to decipher what the person said. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                         |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You don't know what they said and they become \[Suspicious\]        |
| Fade         | You know what they said and they become \[Suspicious\]              |
| Success      | You know what they said                                             |
| Cool Success | Read Lips is an automatic Success on them for the rest of the scene |
