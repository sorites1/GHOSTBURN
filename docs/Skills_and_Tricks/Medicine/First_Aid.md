# First Aid \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Stabilization DL      | Medicine | [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)], [[Concentrate](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)] |

*When a friend falls in combat or suffers from another treatable condition, you rush to their side and use your medical knowledge to stabilize them.*

To use First Aid on a target, they must be \[Bleeding\] or \[Dying\]. If you use First Aid without [[Supplies](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Supplies.md)] raise the DL by one. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are \[Stabilized\] and heal HP equal to their Resilience score + 2 + your Medicine skill bonus                                                                                                                                                        |
| Success      | They are \[Stabilized\] and gain HP equal to their Resilience score + 2                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Fade         | They are [[Stabilized](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stabilized.md)], gain HP equal to their Resilience score +2, and you have [[Stress](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stress.md)] vs. [[Stabilization DL](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Stabilization_DL.md)] |
| Glitch       | They lose 2 HP and gain [[Bleeding](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Bleeding.md)]                                                                                                                                                                            |
