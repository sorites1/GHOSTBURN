# Pit Maneuver \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL)                                          | Skill | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| -------------------------------------------------------------- | ----- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Target Vehicle's [[Stability](../Keywords/Keyword_Stability\.md)] | Drive | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*Pit Maneuver is an aggressive driving move designed to disable your opponent's vehicle and bring it to a stop.* 

To use Pit Maneuver, your [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle.md)] must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to the other \[Vehicle\]. The [[Handling](../Keywords/Keyword_Handling.md)] of your \[Vehicle\] applies to the DL of your Pit Maneuver roll. If you use only [[One Hand](../Keywords/Keyword_One_Hand.md)], raise the DL by one.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | Their \[Vehicle\] [[Crashes](../Keywords/Keyword_Crashes\.md)]                                      |
| Success      | Their \[Vehicle\] takes 10 damage, spins out, and stops                                          |
| Fade         | Both your and their [[Vehicle](../Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle\.md)] take 10 damage, spin out, and stop |
| Glitch       | You miss and they [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move\.md)] [[Far](../Keywords/Keyword_Far\.md)] away     |

### Pit Maneuver Sidebar

*You maneuver your vehicle alongside so your front is lined up with the other vehicle's rear end. Then you use your vehicle to strike the rear quarter panel of the other vehicle with the intent of causing it to spin out and for the other driver to lose control.*
