# Drone Recon \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL)                       | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Security](/../Keywords/Keyword_Security)] | Piloting | [[Concentrate](/../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate)], [[Control Deck](/../Keywords/Keyword_Control_Deck)], [[Drone](/../Keywords/Keyword_Drone)], [[Fly](/../Keywords/Keyword_Fly)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)], [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)], [[Wireless](/../Keywords/Keyword_Wireless)] |

*Sitting on the side of the road, you remotely pilot a drone and use it to collect intel on your target.*

When you use Drone Recon, describe a location [[Far](/../Keywords/Keyword_Far)] away and state the scope of your search. For example, *I want to fly up to the 38th floor and see if I can find the room we're looking for*, or *I want to do a flyover of the junkyard and see where the gang's guards are stationed*. The Difficulty Level is based on the [[Security](/../Keywords/Keyword_Security)] of the airspace you are entering to perform your search. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\], the GM tells you what you find, and you learn a [[Secret](/../Keywords/Keyword_Secret)]                                                                   |
| Success      | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\] and the GM tells you what you find                                                                                                                         |
| Fade         | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\], the GM tells you what you find, and you have [[Stress](/../Conditions/Condition_Stress)] vs. [[Security](/../Keywords/Keyword_Security)]                  |
| Glitch       | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)], it triggers a [[Security Alert](/../Keywords/Keyword_Security_Alert)], and is [[Jammed](/../Conditions/Condition_Jammed)] |
