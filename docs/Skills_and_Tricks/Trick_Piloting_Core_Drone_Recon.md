# Drone Recon \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL)                       | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Security](../Keywords/Keyword_Security\.md)] | Piloting | [[Concentrate](../Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate\.md)], [[Control Deck](../Keywords/Keyword_Control_Deck\.md)], [[Drone](../Keywords/Keyword_Drone\.md)], [[Fly](../Keywords/Keyword_Fly\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)], [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)], [[Wireless](../Keywords/Keyword_Wireless\.md)] |

*Sitting on the side of the road, you remotely pilot a drone and use it to collect intel on your target.*

When you use Drone Recon, describe a location [[Far](../Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] away and state the scope of your search. For example, *I want to fly up to the 38th floor and see if I can find the room we're looking for*, or *I want to do a flyover of the junkyard and see where the gang's guards are stationed*. The Difficulty Level is based on the [[Security](../Keywords/Keyword_Security.md)] of the airspace you are entering to perform your search. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\], the GM tells you what you find, and you learn a [[Secret](../Keywords/Keyword_Secret\.md)]                                                                   |
| Success      | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\] and the GM tells you what you find                                                                                                                         |
| Fade         | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\], the GM tells you what you find, and you have [[Stress](../Conditions/Condition_Stress\.md)] vs. [[Security](../Keywords/Keyword_Security\.md)]                  |
| Glitch       | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby\.md)], it triggers a [[Security Alert](../Keywords/Keyword_Security_Alert\.md)], and is [[Jammed](../Conditions/Condition_Jammed\.md)] |
