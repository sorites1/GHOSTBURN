## Cybernetic Hack \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                                        |
| --------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[[[Keyword_Security\|Security]]\]          | Tech  | \[[[Keyword_Cyber_Rig\|Cyber Rig]]\], \[[[Keyword_Physical\|Physical]]\], \[[[Keyword_Range\|Range]]\], \[[[Keyword_Wireless\|Wireless]]\], \[[[Keyword_Two_Hands\|Two Hands]]\], \[[[Keyword_Visual\|Visual]]\] |

*With the right module installed, peoples' cyberware can be added to your cyber rig's AR overlay. Selecting one of the cybernetic implants in your field of view runs the module and attempts to disable the target.* 

To use Cybernetic Hack on an opponent, they must be \[[[Keyword_Close|Close]]\] or \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                         |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Glitch       | You have no access and trigger a \[[[Keyword_Security_Alert\|Security Alert]]\]                                 |
| Fade         | Their implant is \[[[Keyword_Disabled\|Disabled]]\] and you have \[[[Condition_Stress\|Stress]]\] vs. \[[[Keyword_Security\|Security]]\]              |
| Success      | Their implant is \[Disabled\]                                                       |
| Cool Success | Choose for their implant to be \[Disabled\] or \[[[Keyword_Enabled\|Enabled]]\] and you are an \[[[Keyword_Admin\|Admin]]\] |
