# Convince \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                             |
| --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Wits                  | Persuade | [[Attitude](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude.md)], [[Language](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Language.md)], [[Social](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social.md)], [[Vocal](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vocal.md)] |

*By appealing to the other person's sensibilities, you find a way to convince them to help you or to see things from your point of view.* 

If you use Convince on a [[Far](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] away target or remotely, such as over a call, raise the DL by one. If you offer a suitable bribe, lower the DL by one. Some targets may be offended by the offer a bribe (GM's discretion), in which case the DL is raised by one instead. If you use Convince on a [[Group](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group.md)], use the highest [[Wits](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Wits.md)] amongst them and apply the outcome to the \[Group\].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They agree and their \[Attitude\] improves                                                                  |
| Success      | They agree                                                                                                  |
| Fade         | They agree (with some reservations) and they gain [[Confident](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Confident.md)] |
| Glitch       | They are unconvinced and they gain \[Confident\]                                                            |
