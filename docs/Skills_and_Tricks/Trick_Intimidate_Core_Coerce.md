# Coerce \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill      | Keywords                            |
| --------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Fort                  | Intimidate | [[Language](/../Keywords/Keyword_Language)], [[Social](/../Keywords/Keyword_Social)], [[Vocal](/../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal)] |

*With threats of damage to income, reputation, or bodily health, you force your victim to comply with your instructions.* 

If your instructions would put the target or their loved ones in a dangerous situation, raise the DL by one. They will outright refuse instructions that would result in certain death or serious injury.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                             |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are [[Servile](/../Conditions/Condition_Servile)]                                                                                  |
| Success      | They are \[Compliant\]                                                                                                                  |
| Fade         | They are [[Compliant](/../Conditions/Condition_Compliant)] and you have [[Stress](/../Conditions/Condition_Stress)] vs. Fort            |
| Glitch       | They are not intimidated, their [[Attitude](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attitude)] worsens, and they [[Break](/../Conditions/Condition_Break)] |
