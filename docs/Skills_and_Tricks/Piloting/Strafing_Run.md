# Strafing Run \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                              |
| --------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hard (20)             | Piloting | [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)], [[Control Deck](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Control_Deck.md)], [[Datajack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Datajack.md)], [[Drone](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone.md)], [[Fly](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fly.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)], [[Wireless](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Wireless.md)] |

*You fly your drone low and fast, firing its automatic weapons to rain down lead on your opponents.*

To use Strafing Run, your [[Drone](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone.md)] must be outfitted with an automatic weapon. You may target up to three opponents with Strafing Run, all of which must be [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] your \[Drone\] and each other. Use the weapon's Full Auto [[Damage Array](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Damage_Array.md)] with this trick.

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They take [[Normal Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Normal_Damage.md)] and are [[Suppressed](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Suppressed.md)], and you [[Fly](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fly.md)] the \[Drone\] [[Far](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] away |
| Success      | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Far](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] away                                                                                                                            |
| Fade         | They take \[Normal Damage\] and you \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Nearby\]                                                                                                                                                        |
| Glitch       | You miss, \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)], and are [[Out of Ammo](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Out_of_Ammo.md)]                                                                                          |
