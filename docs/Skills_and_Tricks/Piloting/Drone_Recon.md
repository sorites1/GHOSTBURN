# Drone Recon \[Core Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL)                       | Skill    | Keywords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Security](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Security.md)] | Piloting | [[Concentrate](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Concentrate.md)], [[Control Deck](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Control_Deck.md)], [[Drone](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone.md)], [[Fly](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Fly.md)], [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)], [[Physical](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Two Hands](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)], [[Visual](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Visual.md)], [[Wireless](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Wireless.md)] |

*Sitting on the side of the road, you remotely pilot a drone and use it to collect intel on your target.*

When you use Drone Recon, describe a location [[Far](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] away and state the scope of your search. For example, *I want to fly up to the 38th floor and see if I can find the room we're looking for*, or *I want to do a flyover of the junkyard and see where the gang's guards are stationed*. The Difficulty Level is based on the [[Security](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Security.md)] of the airspace you are entering to perform your search. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\], the GM tells you what you find, and you learn a [[Secret](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Secret.md)]                                                                   |
| Success      | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\] and the GM tells you what you find                                                                                                                         |
| Fade         | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] \[Far\], the GM tells you what you find, and you have [[Stress](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stress.md)] vs. [[Security](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Security.md)]                  |
| Glitch       | You \[Fly\] the \[Drone\] [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)], it triggers a [[Security Alert](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Security_Alert.md)], and is [[Jammed](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Jammed.md)] |
