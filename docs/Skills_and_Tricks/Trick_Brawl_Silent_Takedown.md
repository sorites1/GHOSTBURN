# Silent Takedown \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Brawl | [[Attack](/../Keywords/Keyword_Attack)], \[Unarmed\], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Two Hands](/../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands)] [[Visual](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visual)] |

*You swiftly and silently close in on your opponent and execute a maneuver to quietly subdue them before they have time to react.* 

To use Silent Takedown, you must be [[Undetected](/../Conditions/Condition_Undetected)], and your opponent must be [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)]. When you use this trick, you silently [[Move](/../Keywords/Keyword_Move)] [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] to them and roll Silent Takedown. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are [[Unconscious](/../Conditions/Condition_Unconscious)]                                                                                   |
| Success      | They are \[Stunned\]                                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | They are [[Stunned](/../Conditions/Condition_Stunned)] but cry out, alerting anyone [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)]                      |
| Glitch       | You fumble the maneuver, lose [[Undetected](/../Conditions/Condition_Undetected)], and are [[Off Balance](/../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance)] |
