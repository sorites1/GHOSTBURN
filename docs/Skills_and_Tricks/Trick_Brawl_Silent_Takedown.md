# Silent Takedown \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill | Keywords                                                        |
| --------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Brawl | [[Attack](../Keywords/Keyword_Attack\.md)], \[Unarmed\], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Two Hands](../Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands\.md)] [[Visual](../Keywords/Keyword_Visual\.md)] |

*You swiftly and silently close in on your opponent and execute a maneuver to quietly subdue them before they have time to react.* 

To use Silent Takedown, you must be [[Undetected](../Conditions/Condition_Undetected.md)], and your opponent must be [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. When you use this trick, you silently [[Move](../Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to them and roll Silent Takedown. 

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Cool Success | They are [[Unconscious](../Conditions/Condition_Unconscious\.md)]                                                                                   |
| Success      | They are \[Stunned\]                                                                                                                             |
| Fade         | They are [[Stunned](../Conditions/Condition_Stunned\.md)] but cry out, alerting anyone [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby\.md)]                      |
| Glitch       | You fumble the maneuver, lose [[Undetected](../Conditions/Condition_Undetected\.md)], and are [[Off Balance](../Conditions/Condition_Off_Balance\.md)] |
