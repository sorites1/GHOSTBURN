# Captivate \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                       |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Persuade | \[Attitude\], [[Physical](/../Keywords/Keyword_Physical)], [[Range](/../Keywords/Keyword_Range)], [[Social](/../Keywords/Keyword_Social)], [[Visible](/../Keywords/Keyword_Visible)] |

*With a simple change in posture, a strut in your step, or a flick of your hair, you can put on "the look" that makes everyone sit up and take notice.* 

To use this trick, the opponent(s) must be [[Close](/../Keywords/Keyword_Close)] or [[Nearby](/../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby)]. If there are multiple onlookers, use the highest Wits amongst the [[Group](/../Keywords/Keyword_Group)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                          |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are [[Bewildered](/../Conditions/Condition_Bewildered)] and you gain an [[Ally](/../Conditions/Condition_Ally)] |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                                                                                              |
| Fade         | They are [[Distracted](/../Conditions/Condition_Distracted)] and their \[Attitude\] worsens                          |
| Glitch       | They are not impressed and suffer a [[Break](/../Conditions/Condition_Break)]                                        |
