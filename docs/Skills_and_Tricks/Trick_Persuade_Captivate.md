# Captivate \[Signature Trick\]

| Difficulty Level (DL) | Skill    | Keywords                                                       |
| --------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wits                  | Persuade | \[Attitude\], [[Physical](../Keywords/Keyword_Physical\.md)], [[Range](../Keywords/Keyword_Range\.md)], [[Social](../Keywords/Keyword_Social\.md)], [[Visible](../Keywords/Keyword_Visible\.md)] |

*With a simple change in posture, a strut in your step, or a flick of your hair, you can put on "the look" that makes everyone sit up and take notice.* 

To use this trick, the opponent(s) must be [[Close](../Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] or [[Nearby](../Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]. If there are multiple onlookers, use the highest Wits amongst the [[Group](../Keywords/Keyword_Group.md)].

| Outcome      | Description                                                                                                          |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cool Success | They are [[Bewildered](../Conditions/Condition_Bewildered\.md)] and you gain an [[Ally](../Conditions/Condition_Ally\.md)] |
| Success      | They are \[Distracted\]                                                                                              |
| Fade         | They are [[Distracted](../Conditions/Condition_Distracted\.md)] and their \[Attitude\] worsens                          |
| Glitch       | They are not impressed and suffer a [[Break](../Conditions/Condition_Break\.md)]                                        |
