# Cybernetic Arm

*A Cybernetic Arm is a full replacement of one of your existing arms and includes a Cybernetic Hand.*

|   Cost   | Points | Augmentation             | Mesh Cost | Reduces Psi | Mod Slots |
| :------: | :----: | ------------------------ | :-------: | :---------: | :-------: |
| 20,000 ⌆ |   2    | Cybernetic Arm (Base)    |     6     |     No      |     0     |
| 40,000 ⌆ |   4    | Cybernetic Arm (Level 1) |     6     |     No      |     1     |
| 60,000 ⌆ |   6    | Cybernetic Arm (Level 2) |     6     |     No      |     2     |
| 80,000 ⌆ |   8    | Cybernetic Arm (Level 3) |     6     |     No      |     3     |

A base \[Cybernetic Arm\] performs all the functions of a natural arm and hand. It does not include any [[Mod Slots](/../Keywords/Keyword_Mod_Slots)]. Higher level versions allow you to install one or more [[Mod](/../Keywords/Keyword_Mod)] features in the augment. A \[Cybernetic Arm\] of one level is [[Incompatible](/../Keywords/Keyword_Incompatible)] with a \[Cybernetic Arm\] of another level (only the lower of the two levels is applied).
