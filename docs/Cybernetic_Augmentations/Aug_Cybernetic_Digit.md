# Cybernetic Digit \[Augment\]

*A Cybernetic Digit is a replacement for one of your natural digits, a finger or toe.*

|  Cost   | Augment                    | Mesh Cost | Mod Slots | Keywords |
| :-----: | -------------------------- | :-------: | :-------: | :------- |
| 1,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Digit (Base)    |     1     |     0     | -        |
| 2,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Digit (Level 1) |     1     |     1     | -        |

Cybernetic Digit (Base) is a replacement only. It performs all the functions of a natural finger or toe. 

## Augment Mods

The following augments have the [[Mod](/../Keywords/Keyword_Mod)] keyword and can be installed in a Cybernetic Digit with a free Mod Slot. Each \[Mod\] augment takes one Mod Slot. Mods: [Multitool](/../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Multitool)
