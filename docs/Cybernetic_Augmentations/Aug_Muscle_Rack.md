# Muscle Rack

The \[Muscle Rack\] augment strengthens muscles in your arms, legs, neck, back, and abdomen, providing an increase to your natural Strength. \[Muscle Rack\] comes in two levels: \[Muscle Rack 1\] and \[Muscle Rack 2\]. These levels represent an upgrade path; they do not stack.

| Points | Augmentation  | Mesh Cost | Reduces Psi | Description          |
| :----: | ------------- | :-------: | ----------- | -------------------- |
|   4    | Muscle Rack 1 |     4     | Yes         | +1 to Strength (Aug) |
|   6    | Muscle Rack 2 |     6     | Yes         | +2 to Strength (Aug) |
