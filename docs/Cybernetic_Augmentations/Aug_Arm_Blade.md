# Arm Blade

The \[Arm Blade\] augment is implanted in the forearm. When activated, a long blade extends from a slot in the arm and acts as a [[Katana](/../Melee_Weapons/Melee_Katana)]. You can extend and retract the \[Arm Blade\] for free (0 AP).

| Points | Augmentation | Mesh Cost | Reduces Psi | Description      |
| :----: | ------------ | :-------: | ----------- | ---------------- |
|   7    | Arm Blade    |     7     | No          | Acts as a Katana |
