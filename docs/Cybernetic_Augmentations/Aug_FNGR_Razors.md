# FNGR Razors \[Augment\]

*Flick-Nick-Gouge-Retract. FNGR Razors by Ranco are implanted under the fingernails on one hand. They can be extended or retracted with a flick of the fingers.*

FNGR Razors have a Base Value of 2 for its Melee [Damage Array](/../Gear_and_Augments/GaA_Damage_Array). FNGR Razors cannot be used to perform [Finishing Move](/../Skills_and_Tricks/Trick_Melee_Finishing_Move).

You can extend and retract FNGR Razors for free (0 AP).

FNGR Razors are [[Incompatible](/../Keywords/Keyword_Incompatible)] with [VPR Blade](/../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_VPR_Blade) and [TGR Claws](/../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_TGR_Claws) in the same arm. 

FNGR Razors can be installed as a [[Mod](/../Keywords/Keyword_Mod)] in the [Cybernetic Arm](/../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Cybernetic_Arm) augment.
