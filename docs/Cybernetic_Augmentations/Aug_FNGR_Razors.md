# FNGR Razors \[Augment\]

*Flick-Nick-Gouge-Retract. FNGR Razors by Ranco are implanted under the fingernails on one hand. They can be extended or retracted with a flick of the fingers.*

FNGR Razors have a Base Value of 2 for its Melee [Damage Array](../Gear_and_Augments/GaA_Damage_Array.md). FNGR Razors cannot be used to perform [Finishing Move](../Skills_and_Tricks/Trick_Melee_Finishing_Move.md).

You can extend and retract FNGR Razors for free (0 AP).

FNGR Razors are [[Incompatible](../Keywords/Keyword_Incompatible.md)] with [VPR Blade](../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_VPR_Blade.md) and [TGR Claws](../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_TGR_Claws.md) in the same arm. 

FNGR Razors can be installed as a [[Mod](../Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] in the [Cybernetic Arm](../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Cybernetic_Arm.md) augment.
