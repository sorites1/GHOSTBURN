# Gunwires \[Augment\]

*Gunwires run from a contact pad installed in the palm of your hand, up the arm, to your brainstem. When paired with a compatible firearm, Gunwires use data from your weapon to provide aim assistance.*

Gunwires allows you to get the +2 [[Gear Bonus](../Keywords/Keyword_Gear_Bonus.md)] to Firearms granted by a weapon's [[Gunwire Grip](../Mod_Gunwire_Grip.md)]. 

If you have [Cybernetic Eyes](../Aug_Cybernetic_Eye.md) installed, Gunwires grant a +1 [[Aug Bonus](../Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Bonus.md)] to Firearms. This bonus is not dependent upon the weapon having a \[Gunwire Grip\].

Gunwires can be installed as a [[Mod](../Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] in the [Cybernetic Arm](../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Cybernetic_Arm.md) augment.
