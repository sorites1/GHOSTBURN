# FNG Razors

The \[Finger Razors\] augment involves implanting retractable razors underneath each fingernail on one hand. FNG stands for Flick-Nick-Gouge . When activated, the razors extend from under the fingernails. Together, these razors act a [[Butterfly Knife](/../Melee_Weapons/Melee_Butterfly_Knife)] but you use \[Brawl\] instead of \[Melee\]. You can extend and retract \[Finger Razors\] for free (0 AP).
