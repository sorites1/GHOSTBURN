# Dermal Armor

\[Dermal Armor\] is metal plating inserted under your skin, typically over the rib cage and abdomen. This augmentation is obvious to anyone who observes you shirtless. \[Dermal Armor\] counts as Hard Armor. \[Dermal Armor\] and [[Subdermal Nanoweave](/../Cybernetic_Augmentations/Aug_Subdermal_Nanoweave)] are [[Incompatible](/../Keywords/Keyword_Incompatible)].

| :----: | ------------ | :-------: | ----------- | ----------------------------- |
|   3    | Dermal Armor |     3     | No          | Obvious, Counts as Hard Armor |
