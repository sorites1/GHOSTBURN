# Cybernetic Leg

A \[Cybernetic Leg\] is a full replacement of one of your existing legs. If you replace both of your legs and pay for the Fast Upgrade, you gain \[Fast\]. You can install up to 4 Mesh worth of augments into a Cybernetic Leg without paying the Mesh Cost of those augments.

| Points | Augmentation                             | Mesh Cost | Reduces Psi | Description                                 |
| :----: | ---------------------------------------- | :-------: | ----------- | ------------------------------------------- |
|   2    | Cybernetic Leg (One)                     |     6     | No          | No change to movement                       |
|   4    | Cybernetic Legs (Both)                   |    12     | No          | No change to movement                       |
|   6    | Cybernetic Legs (Both with Fast Upgrade) |    12     | No          | You are [[Fast](/../Keywords/Keyword_Fast)] |
