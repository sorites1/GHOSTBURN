# Neuromatrix \[Augment\]

*The Neuromatrix augment, implanted in the brain, stimulates the prefrontal cortex and boosts neurotransmitters, improving cognitive focus and executive function.* 

|   Cost    | Augment               | Mesh Cost | Mod Slots | Keywords                                            |
| :-------: | --------------------- | :-------: | :-------: | :-------------------------------------------------- |
| 100,000 ⌆ | Neuromatrix (Level 1) |     4     |     0     | [[Aug Increase](/../Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Increase)] |
| 400,000 ⌆ | Neuromatrix (Level 2) |     6     |     0     | \[Aug Increase\]                                    |
| 800,000 ⌆ | Neuromatrix (Level 3) |     8     |     0     | \[Aug Increase\]                                    |

Neuromatrix grants its level as an \[Aug Increase\] to your Intelligence score. That is, Neuromatrix (Level 1) gives you an \[Aug Increase\] of 1 to your Intelligence score, Neuromatrix (Level 2) gives you an \[Aug Increase\] of 2, and Neuromatrix (Level 3) gives you an \[Aug Increase\] of 3.
