# Doc-in-a-Box \[Gear\]

*This portable box opens into two halves, like a tackle box, to reveal an array of instruments and connected devices. The Doc-in-a-Box system guides you through the diagnosis and treatment of wounds and injuries. If the patient's condition is dire, it can even help with field amputations and surgery.*

|   Cost   | Gear                   | Keywords                                        |
| :------: | ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| 10,000 ⌆ | Doc-in-a-Box (Level 1) | [[Gear Bonus](/../Keywords/Keyword_Gear_Bonus)] |
| 25,000 ⌆ | Doc-in-a-Box (Level 2) | \[Gear Bonus\]                                  |
| 75,000 ⌆ | Doc-in-a-Box (Level 3) | \[Gear Bonus\]                                  |

Doc-in-a-Box grants its level as a \[Gear Bonus\] to your Medicine skill. That is, Doc-in-a-Box (Level 1) gives you a +1 \[Gear Bonus\] to Medicine, Doc-in-a-Box (Level 2) gives you a +2 \[Gear Bonus\], and Doc-in-a-Box (Level 3) gives you a +3 \[Gear Bonus\].
