# FNGR Razors \[Augment\]

*Flick-Nick-Gouge-Retract. FNGR Razors by Ranco are implanted under the fingernails on one hand. They can be extended or retracted with a flick of the fingers.*

FNGR Razors have a Base Value of 2 for its Melee [Damage Array](../../../Playing_the_Game/Rolling_the_Dice/Damage_Array.md). FNGR Razors cannot be used to perform [Finishing Move](../../../Skills_and_Tricks/Melee/Finishing_Move.md).

You can extend and retract FNGR Razors for free (0 AP).

FNGR Razors are [[Incompatible](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Incompatible.md)] with [VPR Blade](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/VPR_Blade.md) and [TGR Claws](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/TGR_Claws.md) in the same arm. 

FNGR Razors can be installed as a [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] in the [Cybernetic Arm](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Body_Augments/Cybernetic_Arm.md) augment.
