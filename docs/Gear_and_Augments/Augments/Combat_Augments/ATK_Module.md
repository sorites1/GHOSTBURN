# ATK Module \[Augment\]

*Auto-Target-Kill. The ATK Module by Ranco is a cranial implant that works by analyzing data from Cybernetic Eyes, Gunwires, and Glide-By-Wire Reflexes to provide computer-aided adjustments that improve combat outcomes.*

You must have at least one [Cybernetic Eye](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Body_Augments/Cybernetic_Eye.md) (any level) installed, as well as [Glide-By-Wire Reflexes](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Utility_Augments/Glide_By_Wire_Reflexes.md) (any level) and [Gunwires](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/Gunwires.md).

The ATK Module has the following effects: [Gunwire Grip](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Firearms/Firearms_Mods/Gunwire_Grip.md) grants a +3 [[Gear Bonus](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Gear_Bonus.md)] (instead of a +2) and [Gunwires](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/Gunwires.md) grant a +2 [[Aug Bonus](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Bonus.md)] to Firearms (instead of a +1). If you have Cybernetic Eye (Level 3) installed, [Gunwires](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/Gunwires.md)  grant a +3 [[Aug Bonus](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Bonus.md)] to Firearms (instead of a +2).
