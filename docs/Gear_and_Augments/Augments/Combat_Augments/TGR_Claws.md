TGR Claws have a Base Value of 4 for its Melee [Damage Array](../../../Playing_the_Game/Rolling_the_Dice/Damage_Array.md). 

You can extend and retract TGR Claws for free (0 AP).

TGR Claws are [[Incompatible](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Incompatible.md)] with [VPR Blade](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/VPR_Blade.md) and [FNGR Razors](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/FNGR_Razors.md) in the same arm. 

TGR Claws can be installed as a [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] in the [Cybernetic Arm](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Body_Augments/Cybernetic_Arm.md) augment.
