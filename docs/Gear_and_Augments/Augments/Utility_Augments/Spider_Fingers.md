*Popularized by street hackers, Spider Fingers require the augmentation of four fingers on each hand. When you use a Cyber Rig, hatches on your knuckles pop open to reveal an array of long, thin, articulated metal digits that let you tap its keys with blistering speed.*

|   Cost   | Augment        | Mesh Cost | Keywords                                                                                                                      |
| :------: | -------------- | :-------: | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 40,000 ⌆ | Spider Fingers |     2     | [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)], [[Physical](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Physical.md)], [[Two Hands](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Two_Hands.md)] |

When you make a Hacking roll using your [[Cyber Rig](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Cyber_Rig.md)], lower the DL by one.

Spider Fingers can be installed as a [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] in the [Cybernetic Arm](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Body_Augments/Cybernetic_Arm.md) or [Cybernetic Hand](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Body_Augments/Cybernetic_Hand.md) augments.
