# Glide-By-Wire Reflexes \[Augment\]

*Glide-By-Wire Reflexes are attached to your spinal column and spread throughout your limbs. They interpret your movements to deliver computer-aided precision to achieve desired outcomes.*

|   Cost    | Augment                          | Mesh Cost | Keywords                                                                               |
| :-------: | -------------------------------- | :-------: | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 125,000 ⌆ | Glide-By-Wire Reflexes (Level 1) |     5     | [[Aug Increase](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Increase.md)], [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] |
| 300,000 ⌆ | Glide-By-Wire Reflexes (Level 2) |     8     | [[Aug Increase](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Increase.md)], [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] |
| 750,000 ⌆ | Glide-By-Wire Reflexes (Level 3) |    12     | [[Aug Increase](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Increase.md)], [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] |

The Glide-By-Wire Reflexes augment grants its level as an \[Aug Increase\] to your Agility score. That is, Glide-By-Wire Reflexes (Level 1) gives you an \[Aug Increase\] of 1 to your Agility score, Glide-By-Wire Reflexes (Level 2) gives you an \[Aug Increase\] of 2, and Glide-By-Wire Reflexes (Level 3) gives you an \[Aug Increase\] of 3. 

You should update your Drive, Firearms, and Stealth skills to use your new Agility score.

Glide-By-Wire Reflexes can be installed as a [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] in the [Cybernetic Spine](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Body_Augments/Cybernetic_Spine.md) augment.
