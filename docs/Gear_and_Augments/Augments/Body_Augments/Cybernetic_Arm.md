# Cybernetic Arm \[Augment\]

*A Cybernetic Arm is a full replacement of one of your existing arms and includes a Cybernetic Hand.*

|   Cost   | Augment                  | Mesh Cost | Mod Slots | Keywords |
| :------: | ------------------------ | :-------: | :-------: | -------- |
| 20,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Arm (Base)    |     5     |     0     | -        |
| 40,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Arm (Level 1) |     5     |     1     | -        |
| 60,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Arm (Level 2) |     5     |     2     | -        |
| 80,000 ⌆ | Cybernetic Arm (Level 3) |     5     |     3     | -        |

Cybernetic Arm (Base) is a replacement only. It performs all the functions of a natural arm and hand. 

If you have two Cybernetic Arms, they may be of different levels and each has its own Mod Slots. 

## Augment Mods

The following augments have the [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] keyword and can be installed in a Cybernetic Arm with a free Mod Slot. Each \[Mod\] augment takes one Mod Slot. Mods: [FNGR Razors](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/FNGR_Razors.md), [Gunwires](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/Gunwires.md), [Multitool](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Utility_Augments/Multitool.md), [SLAP Gun](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/SLAP_Gun.md), [Spider Fingers](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Utility_Augments/Spider_Fingers.md), [TGR Claws](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/TGR_Claws.md), and [VPR Blade](../../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/VPR_Blade.md)

## Strength Mod

You can also install a Strength Mod in a free Mod Slot in a Cybernetic Arm to permanently raise your Strength, as shown below. You may install this \[Mod\] multiple times, and its effects are cumulative. Each time you install a Strength Mod, it uses one Mod Slot. 

|   Cost    | Mod               | Keywords                                                                               |
| :-------: | ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 100,000 ⌆ | +1 Strength (Aug) | [[Aug Increase](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Aug_Increase.md)], [[Mod](../../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Mod.md)] |

Recalculate your [Hit Points and Health Conditions](../../../Characters/Health_and_Healing/Hit_Points_and_Conditions.md) using your increased Strength score and make the necessary adjustments to your character sheet. You should also update your Athletics, Brawl, and Melee skills to use your new Strength score.
