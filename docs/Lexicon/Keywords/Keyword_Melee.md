# Melee

An [[Attack](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attack.md)] made using a Melee Weapon, such as a knife, katana, or sledgehammer, against an opponent who is [[Close](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Close.md)] to you. \[[Bladed]\] augments, such as [FNGR Razors](../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/FNGR_Razors.md) or [VPR Blade](../../Gear_and_Augments/Augments/Combat_Augments/VPR_Blade.md) are also consider melee weapons. To make a \[Melee\] \[Attack\], use one of the tricks found under the [Melee Skill](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Melee/Melee_Skill.md).
