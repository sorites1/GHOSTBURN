# High Speed

A [[Vehicle](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle.md)] or [[Drone](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone.md)] traveling at 100 kph (about 60 mph) or higher. When you are moving at \[High Speed\] and are called upon to make a Drive roll, raise the DL by one. For each additional 100 kph, raise the DL by one again.
