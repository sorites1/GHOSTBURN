# Friendly

When an NPC has a \[Friendly\] [[Attitude](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude.md)] towards you, they are helpful and kind to you. When you use a [[Social](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Social.md)] trick on a \[Friendly\] NPC, lower the DL by one. If the target's \[Attitude\] is \[Friendly\], it cannot be improved. If something causes it to worsen, it drops to \[Neutral\].
