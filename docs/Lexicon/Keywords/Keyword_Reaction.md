# Reaction

This trick is used in response to a trigger defined in its description. When the condition for the trigger occurs, you may use this trick, even if it's not your turn. \[Reaction\] actions are free (0 AP). 

You have a number of \[Reaction\] actions equal to your Agility score. When you spend a \[Reaction\], it is gone for the rest of the scene. All of your \[Reaction\] actions refresh at the start of a new scene. 

When you use a trick as a \[Reaction\], its outcome is applied before the condition that triggered it. In some cases, this can mean that the triggering condition never actually takes place. 


!!! note "-GET -Reaction //example"
    For example, a thug attempts a [Melee Strike](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Melee/Melee_Strike.md) against you. You spend a \[Reaction\] and roll [Dodge](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Insight/Dodge.md). You score a Cool Success, which causes the thug's \[Attack\] to be \[Downgraded\]. The GM rolls for the thug and scores a Success, but due to the player's Dodge, the thug's outcome is \[Downgraded\] to a Fade. This deals [[Minimum Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Minimum_Damage.md)], but you are wearing [[Armor](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Armor.md)] so the damage is [[Reduced](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Reduced.md)]. Because it is already \[Minimum Damage\], it is \[Reduced\] to [[Absolute Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Absolute_Damage.md)] of 1. However, Cools Success on [Dodge](../../Skills_and_Tricks/Insight/Dodge.md) says, "if you would take [[Absolute Damage](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Absolute_Damage.md)], you instead take no damage and may immediately [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)]." In this case, even though the thug rolled a Success, the final result was that no damage was dealt and you got to \[Move\] away for free.
