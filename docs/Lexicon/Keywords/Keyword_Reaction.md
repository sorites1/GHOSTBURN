# Reaction

This trick is used in response to a trigger defined in its description. When the condition for the trigger occurs, you may use this trick, even if it's not your turn. \[Reaction\] actions are free (0 AP). 

You have a number of \[Reaction\] actions equal to your Agility score. When you spend a \[Reaction\], it is gone for the rest of the scene. All of your \[Reaction\] actions refresh at the start of a new scene. 

When you use a trick as a \[Reaction\], its outcome is applied before the condition that triggered it. In some cases, this can mean that the triggering condition never actually takes place.
