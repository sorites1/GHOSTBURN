# Crashes

When a [[Vehicle](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vehicle.md)] or [[Drone](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone.md)] \[Crashes\], it collides with an immovable object or hazard and comes to a sudden stop. If the \[Vehicle\] or \[Drone\] was traveling at [[High Speed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_High_Speed.md)], it is totaled. If the driver was wearing a safety belt, they take 5 damage. Otherwise, they take 50 points of damage. At [[Moderate Speed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Moderate_Speed.md)], a \[Vehicle\] takes 30 damage and a \[Drone\] takes 15 damage. A \[Vehicle\] driver takes 3 damage with a safety belt or 30 damage without it. At [[Low Speed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Low_Speed.md)], the \[Vehicle\] or \[Drone\] takes 10 damage or 5 damage, respectively. A driver takes 1 damage with a safety belt or 10 damage without it.
