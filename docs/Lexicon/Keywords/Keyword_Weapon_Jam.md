# Weapon Jam

Some tricks result in a \[Weapon Jam\] on a Fade or Glitch. If you are using a [[Firearm](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Firearm.md)] and get a \[Weapon Jam\], you must spend 1 AP before you can use that weapon again. If you are controlling a [[Drone](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Drone.md)] and get a \[Weapon Jam\], you must recover the drone and then spend 1 AP to use its weapon again.
