# Prone

**Cause:** When you lay down on the ground or are forced down by a condition or an attack, you gain \[Prone\].

**Effect:** Attacks made against you from a [[Range](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Range.md)] of [[Nearby](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Nearby.md)] or [[Far](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Far.md)] have their DL raised by one. [[Melee](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Melee.md)] and [[Unarmed](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Unarmed.md)]attacks made against you have their DL lowered by one. On your turn while \[Prone\], you may spend 1 AP to stand up., While you remain \[Prone\], you may only [[Move](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Move.md)] by using [[Crawl](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Crawl.md)]. If you are swimming when forced to go \[Prone\], disregard the rest of this Effect and you begin [[Drowning](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Drowning.md)].

**Removal:** When you stand up, you lose the \[Prone\] condition.
