# Compliant

**Cause:** When a person or [[Group](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group.md)] is compelled to perform actions under duress, they are \[Compliant\]. This condition cannot be applied to players.

**Effect:** The [[Attitude](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Attitude.md)] of the \[Compliant\] person or [[Group](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Group.md)] shifts to [[Hostile](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Hostile.md)]. They follow your instructions, even if they go against their own self-interests, but they will not put their loved ones in harm's way. They will not attempt to escape or retaliate as long as you remain in the [[Vicinity](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Vicinity.md)].

**Removal:** If a target suffers a \[Break\], they lose \[Compliant\]. Forcing the target to do worse and more dangerous things (GM's discretion) requires a [[Basic](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Basic.md)] Presence roll with a DL of Easy (8). Failure means the target suffers a [[Break](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Break.md)]. Forcing the target to put their loved ones in harm's way causes them to automatically \[Break\]. If you or your crew attack the target, they \[Break\]. If you leave the \[Vicinity\] for more than one turn, the target automatically loses \[Compliant\] and flees or prepares to attack upon your return.
