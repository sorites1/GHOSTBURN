# Wounded

**Cause:** When you are reduced to half your maximum hit points (or less), you gain the \[Wounded\] condition. this condition follows and replaces [[Healthy](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Healthy.md)].

**Effect:** All skill actions have their DL raised by one.

**Removal:** When you have more than half your maximum hit points, you lose the \[Wounded\] condition. You also lose the \[Wounded\] condition if you advance to [[Severely Wounded](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Severely_Wounded.md)], [[Death's Door](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Deaths_Door.md)], or [[Dying](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Dying.md)].
