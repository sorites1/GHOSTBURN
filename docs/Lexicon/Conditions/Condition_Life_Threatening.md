# Life Threatening

**Cause:** When you sustain an injury that requires emergency treatment, without which you could could die, you have a \[Life Threatening\] condition.

**Effect:** You need immediate medical attention. At the start of your turn, make a [[Basic](../../Lexicon/Keywords/Keyword_Basic.md)] Resilience roll with a DL of Moderate (14). Failure means you take 3 damage. This stacks with [[Bleeding](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Bleeding.md)].

**Removal:** Receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Treatment entry of the injury. causes you to lose the \[Life Threatening\] condition.
