# Unaware

**Cause:** When you gain [[Unconscious](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Unconscious.md)] or [[Stunned](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Stunned.md)], you also gain \[Unaware\]. You can also be rendered \[Unaware\] by other circumstances.

**Effect:** You are [[Blinded](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Blinded.md)], [[Deafened](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Deafened.md)], and [[Off Guard](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Off_Guard.md)]. 

**Removal:** When you lose \[Blinded\] or \[Deafened\], you lose \[Unaware\].
