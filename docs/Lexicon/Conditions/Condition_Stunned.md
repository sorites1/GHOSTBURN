# Stunned

**Cause:** When a skill action, condition or effect causes you to have a momentary mental or physical shutdown, you are \[Stunned\]. 

**Effect:** You are [[Immobilized](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Immobilized.md)] and [[Incapable](../../Lexicon/Conditions/Condition_Incapable.md)]. 

**Removal:** At the start of your turn, you lose the \[Stunned\] condition.
