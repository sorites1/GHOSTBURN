# Disfigured, Major

**Cause:** When a condition or injury dramatically alters your appearance in an unnatural way, you gain \[Disfigured, Major\]. This condition stacks with itself and with [[Disfigured Minor](/../Conditions/Condition_Disfigured_Minor)].

**Effect:** Persuade tricks have their DL raised by one and their outcomes [[Downgraded](/../Keywords/Keyword_Downgraded)]. Intimidate tricks have their DL lowered by one.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Disfigured, Major\] is removed, you lose the \[Disfigured, Major\] condition. If you gained \[Disfigured, Major\] as the result of an injury, receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Treatment entry of the injury description. causes you to lose \[Disfigured, Major\].
