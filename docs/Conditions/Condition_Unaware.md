# Unaware

**Cause:** When you gain [[Unconscious](../Conditions/Condition_Unconscious.md)] or [[Stunned](../Conditions/Condition_Stunned.md)], you also gain \[Unaware\]. You can also be rendered \[Unaware\] by other circumstances.

**Effect:** You are [[Blinded](../Conditions/Condition_Blinded.md)], [[Deafened](../Conditions/Condition_Deafened.md)], and [[Off Guard](../Conditions/Condition_Off_Guard.md)]. 

**Removal:** When you lose \[Blinded\] or \[Deafened\], you lose \[Unaware\].
