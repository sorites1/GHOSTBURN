# Unaware

**Cause:** When you gain [[Unconscious](/../Conditions/Condition_Unconscious)] or [[Stunned](/../Conditions/Condition_Stunned)], you also gain \[Unaware\]. You can also be rendered \[Unaware\] by other circumstances.

**Effect:** You are [[Blinded](/../Conditions/Condition_Blinded)], [[Deafened](/../Conditions/Condition_Deafened)], and [[Off Guard](/../Conditions/Condition_Off_Guard)]. 

**Removal:** When you lose \[Blinded\] or \[Deafened\], you lose \[Unaware\].
