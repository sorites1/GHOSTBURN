## Raining

**Cause:** When a steady rainfall makes it difficult to see and hear, it is \[Raining\].

**Effect:** Targets in an area where it is \[Raining\] have \[[[Condition_Concealment_Partial|Concealment, Partial]]\]. Tricks with the \[[[Keyword_Vocal|Vocal]]\] keyword have their DL raised by one.

**Removal:** When rainfall thins or stops in an area, it loses \[Raining\].
