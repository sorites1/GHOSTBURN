## Movement Impaired

**Cause:** When a condition or injury makes it harder to move, you are \[Movement Impaired\].

**Effect:** It costs 2 AP to \[[[Keyword_Move|Move]]\] \[[[Keyword_Nearby|Nearby]]\] or use a trick with the \[Move\] keyword. Athletics tricks have their DL raised by one.

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Movement Impaired\] is removed, you lose the \[Movement Impaired\] condition. If you gained \[Movement Impaired\] as the result of an injury, receiving Surgery or Cybernetic Installation, as described in the Recovery entry of the injury description. causes you to lose \[Movement Impaired\].
