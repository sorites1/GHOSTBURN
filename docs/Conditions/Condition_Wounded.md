# Wounded

**Cause:** When you are reduced to half your maximum hit points (or less), you gain the \[Wounded\] condition. this condition follows and replaces [[Healthy](/../Conditions/Condition_Healthy)].

**Effect:** All skill actions have their DL raised by one.

**Removal:** When you have more than half your maximum hit points, you lose the \[Wounded\] condition. You also lose the \[Wounded\] condition if you advance to [[Severely Wounded](/../Conditions/Condition_Severely_Wounded)], [[Death's Door](/../Conditions/Condition_Deaths_Door)], or [[Dying](/../Conditions/Condition_Dying)].
