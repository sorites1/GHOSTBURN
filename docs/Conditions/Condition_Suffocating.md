## Suffocating

**Cause:** When a condition or effect makes it almost impossible for you to breathe, you are \[Suffocating\]. 

**Effect:** At the start of your turn, take 5 damage and make a basic Resilience roll with a DL of Severe (18). If you fail, you fall \[[[Condition_Unconscious|Unconscious]]\].

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to be \[Suffocating\] is removed, you lose the \[Suffocating\] condition.
