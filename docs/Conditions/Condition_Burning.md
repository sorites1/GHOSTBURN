# Burning

**Cause:** When an attack, trick, or effect causes you to be set on fire, you gain \[Burning\].

**Effect:** At the end of your turn, take 3 damage.

**Removal:** When you (or someone else) spend 1 AP to put out the fire on your body, you lose the \[Burning\] condition, and you are [[Prone](../Conditions/Condition_Prone.md)].
