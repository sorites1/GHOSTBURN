# Speech Impairment

**Cause:** When a condition or effect makes it hard for you to speak, you have \[Speech Impairment\]. 

**Effect:** Skill actions with the [[Vocal](../Keywords/Keyword_Vocal.md)] keyword have their DL raised by one. 

**Removal:** When the condition that caused you to have a \[Speech Impairment\] is removed, you lose the \[Speech Impairment\] condition. In rare cases, this condition is permanent.
