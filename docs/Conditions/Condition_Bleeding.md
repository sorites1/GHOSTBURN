# Bleeding

**Cause:** When an attack, trick, or effect causes you to lose blood, you gain \[Bleeding\]. You cannot have more than one instance of \[Bleeding\] at a time.

**Effect:** At the end of your turn, take 1 damage.

**Removal:** When you gain the [[Stabilized](../Conditions/Condition_Stabilized.md)] condition, you lose the \[Bleeding\] condition.
