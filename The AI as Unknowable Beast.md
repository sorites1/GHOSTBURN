The AI is an Unknowable Beast in the true sense. It has revealed itself to certain corporate entities, just little things, little clues. The curious ones try to solves this puzzle, at first individually, but then as they find one another, in groups. They are not plucky investigators. They are giants of capitalism. They are CEOs, venture capitalists, and old money. They are eccentric and devise an idea to summon the AI using a sacrifice. And it works. In that moment, the Dark One was born. A new personality, spontaneously generated, to answer these neo-occultists' call.
