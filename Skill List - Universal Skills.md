## Universal Skills

These are things that anyone can try to do. It doesn't mean they will necessarily succeed (though it is possible) but they can at least make an attempt. 

**Athletics**
* **Physical Activity** - Running, jumping, climbing, lifting, dragging, pushing, pulling, and throwing
* **Swim**  - Swimming in water and underwater
* **Grapple** - Grabbing someone and pinning them to the ground

**Melee**
* **Brawl** - Punching, kicking, head butting, biting, and choking
* **Blades** - Knives, swords, katanas
* **Clubs** - Clubs, bricks, baseball bats

**Deception**
* **Bluff** - Deceiving someone by lying or through omission
* **Misdirect** - Distracting someone to draw their attention away so an ally can accomplish something underhanded
* **Disguise** - Coming up with ways to disguise yourself or others so you blend in or so you are less likely to be recognized

**Firearms**
* **Single Shot** - Fire a single shot from weapon
* **Burst Fire** - Fire a burst from a weapon
* **Full Auto** - Fire a weapon on full auto 

**Influence**
* **Persuade** - Convincing someone to be helpful, improving an attitude from hostile to neutral or neutral to friendly
* **Intimidate** - Making an NPC do what you want through intimidation
* **Entertain** - Singing, dancing, playing a musical instrument, telling jokes or interesting stories

**Insight**
* **Sense Lies** - Sensing whether or not someone is telling the truth
* **Discern Intent** - Discerning what a person's motives are, what they are really trying to accomplish
* **Analyze** - Gleaning knowledge and knowing what to do or where to go next through analysis of collected facts, eye witness accounts, and other clues

**Medicine**
* **First Aid** - Stabilizing someone who has taken on an injury 
* **Extended Care** - Watching over an injured person to aid in their recovery over the course of several hours
* **Assistance** - Provide assistance to someone else who is making a Medicine roll. If your Assistance roll succeeds, you the DL of their Medicine roll is lowered by one.

**Perception**
* **Search** - Actively searching for something or someone in a given area
* **Notice** - Noticing something, overhearing something
* **Listen** - Actively listening, eavesdropping

**Tech**
* **Tech Use** - General use skill for using the Net, computers, and other tech devices, including self-driving vehicles
* **Hardware** - Knowledge of hardware, how to repair it, and how to upgrade it
* **Research** - Searching for information in databases, journals, videos, and other digital resources on the Net

**Stealth**
* **Sneak** - Moving without making noise or being seen
* **Hide** - Finding a good hiding spot and staying there to remain hidden
* **Shadow Target** - Following someone without them noticing you
