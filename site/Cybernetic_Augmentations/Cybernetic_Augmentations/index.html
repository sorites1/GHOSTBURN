    Cybernetic Augmentations

    Cybernetic Augmentations¶

    Characters in GHOSTBURN 2325 can get cybernetic augmentations installed in their body.

    Mesh¶

    You have an attribute called Mesh which represents the limit on how much cybernetics you can cram in your body. All characters have a Mesh of 10, plus their Strength score, plus their Resilience score. For example, a character with a Strength score of 3 and a Resilience score of 2 has a Mesh of 15 (10 + 3 + 2).

    Mesh Cost¶

    Cybernetic augmentations have a cost in creds, as well as a a Mesh cost. You add up the Mesh cost for all of the augments you have to determine the total Mesh cost. Then you compare that value to your character's Mesh attribute. Your Mesh cost cannot exceed your Mesh attribute.

    Cred Cost¶

    The cost in cred for each piece of cyberware is equal to (Mesh Cost x Cyberware Level x 10,000). Cyberware that does not have a level is considered level 1 for purposes of this calculation. For example, a Datajack has a Mesh Cost of 1, so it costs 10,000 cred. Wired Reflexes 3, on the other hand, has a Mesh Cost of 14 (and it's level 3), so it costs 420,000 cred (14 x 3 x 10,000).

    Data/Comm Implants¶

    These are implants used for data storage, processing, transfer, or communication.

    Augment Mesh Cost Reduces Psi Description
    Datajack 1 Yes Allows the user to establish a direct neural interface (DNI) with a device designed for brain-machine interaction, such as a cyber rig, which facilitates access to the lower layer of Net for the purpose of hacking
    Digital Recorder 1 Yes A small camera and microphone combo used to take pictures and video recordings, as well as audio recordings
    Minidisk Drive 1 Yes Allows the user to download data to a small, removable disk, which can then be ejected and air-gapped from the user and the Net
    Language Translator 2 Yes Translates foreign language, both written and verbal, into a language the user can understand in real time
    Darkline Node 2 Yes Used exclusively for ultra-secure communications, such as those used by Mil-Tech ops channels and ghost teams, and which offers no access to the Net
    Netlink 2 Yes Tech array used to access the the Net for communications, maps, and audio/video content, including calls, radio communications, music, entertainment, stock prices, live broadcasts, and weather alerts
    Datavault 3 Yes Allows the user to securely store data without having access to it; commonly used by data couriers
    ATTACK Computer 6 Yes Automated Tactical Threat Assessment and Containment Kit (a.k.a. ATTACK Computer) works with all available data to synthetically identify and prioritize threats, dynamically create tactical maps, and constantly update the user with detailed combat data and recommendations
    ### Weapon Implants

    These are weapon implants.

    Augment Mesh Cost Reduces Psi Description
    Cyber Fangs 1 No Retractable or permanent metallic fangs
    Gunlink 1 Yes Displays distance to target and improves firearms accuracy through vector mapping. When you use Firearms to make a [Gun] [Attack] with a weapon that has a gunlink, gain a +2 gear bonus.
    Hand Zapper 1 No Small taser built into the palm
    Finger Razors (one hand) 1 No Retractable razors slide out from below the fingernails
    Monofilament Whip (one index finger) 1 No A weighted, razor-sharp monofilament whip (so impossibly thin it is almost invisible) housed in the tip of the user's index finger can be deployed in a split second
    Toxin Dispenser 1 No An internal reservoir contains a toxin that can be released as a gas from the mouth or as liquid and delivered as an injection, such as through a finger needle, or by coating a weapon implant like finger razors.
    Cyber Claws (one arm) 2 No Three claws retractable extend from the forearm
    Cyber Blade (one arm) 2 No A long, retractable blade extends from the forearm
    Hidden Pistol (one arm) 3 No A concealed pistol snaps out of the inner forearm and into the hand
    Retractable SMG (one arm) 4 No Arm panels open and the hand snaps down to transform into an SMG
    Internal Flamethrower (one arm) 5 No Internal tubing feeds fuel to the nozzle in the palm; external fuel tanks optional
    Internal Shotgun (one arm) 5 No The arm locks in place and a small shotgun barrel extends from the palm
    Internal Grenade Launcher (one arm) 6 No The hand flicks up to reveal the muzzle of a grenade launcher which can be loaded with up to three mini-grenades

    Utility Implants¶

    Augment Mesh Cost Reduces Psi Description
    Multitool (one hand) 1 No A multitool implant in the finger grants a +1 bonus to Tech (Repair Device) and Tech (Disable Device)
    Spider Fingers (one hand) 1 Yes Allows the user's fingers to split open, revealing an array of long, thin fingerlike digits which enable a hacker to type faster on their cyber rig; grants the user a +1 bonus to all Hacking skill actions; if both hands have the Spider Fingers implant, the user gains a +2 bonus to all Hacking skill actions
    Grapple Gun (one arm) 2 No A small grappling hook can be fired from this arm modification and a strong, metal wire rapidly unspools from inside the forearm; can be automatically retracted
    Chem Analyzer 4 Yes Internal chemistry lab accepts a chemical sample (orally or as a retractable specimen tray), analyzes its chemical structure and provides a detailed report
    Skillset 1 5 Yes Download a skillset to gain Trained in a skill and a +1 bonus to each of its skill actions
    Skillset 2 7 Yes Download a skillset to gain Proficient in a skill and a +2 bonus to each of its skill actions
    Skillset 3 9 Yes Download a skillset to gain Expert in a skill and a +3 bonus each of its skill actions
    Pain Dial 1 3 Yes Allows the user to ignore the effects of Wounded for the remainder of the scene
    Pain Dial 2 6 Yes Allows the user to ignore the effects of Wounded and Severely Wounded for the remainder of the scene
    Pain Dial 3 9 Yes Allows the user to ignore the effects of Wounded, Severely Wounded, and Death's Door for the remainder of the scene
