# Character Creation

[[Characters/Character_Creation/Background|▌ 1. Background ⇒]]

In this game, each player creates a character to play. As the player, you make all the decisions about your character, including their background, natural abilities, skills, and so on. 

To create a character in ***GHOSTBURN***, follow the steps below.

| Step                                            | Seven Questions                  |
| :---------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **[[Background\|1. Background]]**               | What was your old life?          |
| **[[Burn_and_Haunt\|2. Burn and Hook]]**         | How were you burned?             |
| **[[Contacts\|3. Contacts]]**                   | Who can you trust?               |
| **[[Ability_Scores\|4. Ability Scores]]**       | What are your natural abilities? |
| **[[Additional_Skills\|5. Additional Skills]]** | What are your skills?            |
| **[[Starting_Package\|6. Starting Package]]**   | What resources do you have?      |
| **[[Finishing_Touches\|7. Finishing Touches]]** | What is your name?               |
